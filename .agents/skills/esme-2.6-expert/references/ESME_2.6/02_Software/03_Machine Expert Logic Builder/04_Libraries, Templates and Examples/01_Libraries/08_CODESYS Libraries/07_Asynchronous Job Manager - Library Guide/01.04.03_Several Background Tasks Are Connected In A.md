## Several Background Tasks Are Connected In A Row

Like on a conveyor belt, the [BackgroundTask](rJ9NnPzj8kCZsoW9jcVtGOgoM-k_backgroundtask.html#rj9nnpzj8kczsow9jcvtgogom_k_backgroundtask_backgroundtask_fb)s are connected in series via their parameter/result queues. Each task processes a portion of a chain of transformations and passes its result on to its subsequent task.
