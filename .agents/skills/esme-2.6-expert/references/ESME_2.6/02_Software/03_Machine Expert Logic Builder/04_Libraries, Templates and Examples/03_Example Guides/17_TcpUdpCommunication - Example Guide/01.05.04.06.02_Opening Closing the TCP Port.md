# Opening/Closing the TCP Port

Opening/Closing the TCP Port

In the visualization, enter the number of the TCP [port](../glossary/glossary.htm#XREF_D_SE_0055638_16) you want to open and the IP address of the corresponding [Ethernet](../glossary/glossary.htm#XREF_D_SE_0055638_7) interface. To open this TCP port, click the Listen button in the visualization.

You can close an open port by clicking the Close button.
