# q_xOvldWrn

q\_xOvldWrn

The calculated torque value of the measurement process stage of the FB is higher than 90% of i\_rDrvTrqMax. A advisory is given by the yellow light and the horn by being activated for 1 s on and off. The advisory is reset by a new movement/measurement resulting in a lower torque value than 90% of i\_rDrvTrqMax. If using a load cell the input value of i\_rSenIputActl is compared to 90% of i\_stOVLD.dwLoadNom.
