## Task

Interface with additional methods for controlling the standard station.

## Description

The methods of the interface IF\_ControlStandardStation provide additional options for controlling the processes at the station. You can:

* move out the carriers from the process position in order to clear the process ([DriveProcessEmpty](DriveProcEmpty-EED35245.html#DriveProcEmpty-EED35245))
* hold the process ([HoldProcess](HoldProcess-EED4C135.html#HoldProcess-EED4C135))
* stop the carriers in the process ([StopProcess](StopProcess-EED58DEE.html#StopProcess-EED58DEE))
* restart the process ([Restart](Restart-EED689B5.html#Restart-EED689B5))

The methods are executed immediately, without considering any preconditions.
