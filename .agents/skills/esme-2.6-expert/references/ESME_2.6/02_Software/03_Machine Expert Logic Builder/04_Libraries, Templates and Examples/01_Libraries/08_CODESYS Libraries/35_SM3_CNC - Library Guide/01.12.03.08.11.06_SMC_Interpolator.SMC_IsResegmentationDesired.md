# SMC\_Interpolator.SMC\_IsResegmentationDesired (ACT)

If dOverride or single step change, or if the queue changed and the velocity profile has been computed up to the end of the queue, or if an m-function has been preacknowledged (bRecheckVelocities), a fresh computation of the segments is desired.
