This function releases all ressources of a specific receiver.

When calling [DriverClose](UzaP1MMIvsGx5wgQfvzFSZKw87g_driverclose.html#uzap1mmivsgx5wgqfvzfszkw87g_driverclose_driverclose_fun) all receivers connected to this driver are released automatically. Do not call DeleteReceiver afterwards!
