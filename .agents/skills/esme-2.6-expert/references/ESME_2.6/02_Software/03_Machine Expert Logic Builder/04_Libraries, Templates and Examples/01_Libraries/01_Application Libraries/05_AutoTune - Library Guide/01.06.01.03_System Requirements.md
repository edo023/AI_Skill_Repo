# System Requirements

System Requirements

Version 1.0.3.0 of the library functions only with the corresponding firmware versions of PacDrive LXM62, LXM52 or ILM62 and the corresponding firmware version of the PacDrive Controller and the EcoStruxure Machine Expert.

The following table summarizes the system requirements for AutoTune:

| Library | Drive (LXM62, LXM52, ILM62) | PacDrive Controller | EcoStruxure Machine Expert |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| AutoTune 1.1..x.0 | V01.36.09.00 or higher | V01.36.01.00 or higher | V01.36.01.00 or higher |
| AutoTune 1.2.x.0 | V01.50.04.00 or higher | V01.50.01.00 or higher | V01.50.01.00 or higher |
| AutoTune 1.3.x.0 | V01.51.07.00 or higher | V01.51.01.00 or higher | V01.51.01.00 or higher |

At least one drive of the type LXM62, LXM62Linear, LXM52, LXM52Linear or ILM62 is required.

Recommended configuration:

oFlashDisk with 16MB RAM in the PacDrive Controller.
