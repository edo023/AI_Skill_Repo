# ERROR\_CODES (GVL)

The ERROR\_CODES constants are used to identify a specific device or bus error. They are part of [ERROR\_INFO](90iJL2-wmafqXnWvFCFQsy8bs2s_error-info.html#90ijl2_wmafqxnwvfcfqsy8bs2s_error_info_error_info_struct) structure. The constants are divided up into different categories. The categories determine the severity of the occurred error. The exact classification of each error is done individually by each device and each bus.
