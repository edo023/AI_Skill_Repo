# IndicatorConstants (GVL)

This GVL defines the states of the two device LEDs according to CiA 303-3. These states are returned by [GetCiAState](cfVRbhf2vNgtpEfyDfOis0FqThk_getciastate.html#cfvrbhf2vngtpefydfois0fqthk_getciastate_getciastate_fun) and set by [SetCiAState](cfVRbhf2vNgtpEfyDfOis0FqThk_setciastate.html#cfvrbhf2vngtpefydfois0fqthk_setciastate_setciastate_fun). The Error LED gc\_usiERR\_... (red) is represented by the lower 4 bit of CiA State, the RUN LED gc\_usiRUN\_... (green) by the higher 4 bit.
