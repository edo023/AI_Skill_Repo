# Belt Types

Belt Types

The [FB\_Infeed](../Function_Blocks/Function_Blocks-3.htm#XREF_D_SE_0080279_1) POU distinguishes between two belt types:

oSeries

Belts of type Series are driven by a servo motor that is controlled by the [FB\_Infeed](../Function_Blocks/Function_Blocks-3.htm#XREF_D_SE_0080279_1) POU. Belts of type Series can perform feed forward motions and correction motions.

oMonitoring

Belts of type Monitoring are not controlled by [FB\_Infeed](../Function_Blocks/Function_Blocks-3.htm#XREF_D_SE_0080279_1). Only their motions as well as the motions of the products on this belt are monitored. Since the belt is not controlled by the [FB\_Infeed](../Function_Blocks/Function_Blocks-3.htm#XREF_D_SE_0080279_1) POU, no feed forward motion or correction motion can be started on this belt. A belt of type Monitoring can also be driven by a frequency converter.

Belts of type Monitoring are used to monitor motions of the products also on belts of the infeed distance that are not to be used for correction motions. This information usually is very helpful to create an algorithm.
