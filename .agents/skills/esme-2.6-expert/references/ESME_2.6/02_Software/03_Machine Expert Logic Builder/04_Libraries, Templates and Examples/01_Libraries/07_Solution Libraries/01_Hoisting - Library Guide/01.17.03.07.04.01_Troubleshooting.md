# Troubleshooting

Troubleshooting

| Issue | Cause | Solution |
| --- | --- | --- |
| q\_xAlrm of the function block is TRUE. | One or more of the input parameters are out of range. | Verify the input parameters. |
| The crane enters restricted areas during movement of the trolley or bridge. | The nominal or linear speed of the axis or the deceleration ramp time are not parameterized correctly on the function block. | Verify and modify the mentioned parameters. |
