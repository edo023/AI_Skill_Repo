# wMeasFreq

wMeasFreq

This is the frequency in RPM at which the torque measurement is conducted. It must be bigger or equal to the LSP setting of the drive. The smaller the value chosen, the earlier the measurement will result in overload detection. We recommend setting this frequency higher than the slip of the motor, even when running in [closed loop](../glossary/glossary.htm#XREF_D_SE_0024697_737).
