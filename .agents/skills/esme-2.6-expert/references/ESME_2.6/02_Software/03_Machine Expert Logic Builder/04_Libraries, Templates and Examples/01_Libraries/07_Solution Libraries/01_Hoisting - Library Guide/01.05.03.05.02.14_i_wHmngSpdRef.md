# i_wHmngSpdRef

i\_wHmngSpdRef

The speed reference is used for reference/limit switch search. When the reference/limit switch is found, the axis continues with movement to homing edge at 1/4 of i\_wHmngSpdRef.

When the speed is too high, it would lead to overshooting of homing position or decreased accuracy of homing. Therefore, the FB detects this case and triggers a homing alarm.
