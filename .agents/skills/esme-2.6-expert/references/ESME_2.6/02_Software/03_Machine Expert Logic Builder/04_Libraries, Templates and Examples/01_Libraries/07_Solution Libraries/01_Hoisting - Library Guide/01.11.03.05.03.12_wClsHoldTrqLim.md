# wClsHoldTrqLim

wClsHoldTrqLim

Torque limitation of hold motor during [closing on stack](../glossary/glossary.htm#XREF_D_SE_0024697_780). The value must be high enough to keep hold cables straight, but low enough to allow sinking of the grab into material during closing on stack. The usual value is between 10 and 15% of nominal torque. Default value: 100 [0.1%]
