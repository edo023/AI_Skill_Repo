# Troubleshooting

Troubleshooting

| Issue | Cause | Solution |
| --- | --- | --- |
| The synchronized movement stops with position deviation alarm. | One of the synchronized axes has exceeded the deviation threshold. | Start a trace of deviations of all synchronized axes and identify the axis causing the issue. |
| Motor speeds oscillate during synchronized movement. | Proportional gain of the position controller is too great. | Decrease the value of proportional gain. Take a trace of motor speeds and position deviations of synchronized axes. |
