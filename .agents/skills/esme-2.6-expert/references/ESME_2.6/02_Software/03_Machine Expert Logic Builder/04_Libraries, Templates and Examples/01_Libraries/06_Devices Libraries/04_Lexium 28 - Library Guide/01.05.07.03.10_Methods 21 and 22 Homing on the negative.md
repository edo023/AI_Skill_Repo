# Methods 21 and 22: Homing on the negative reference switch

Methods 21 and 22: Homing on the negative reference switch

The initial movement is in the positive direction if the negative reference switch is active.

The initial movement is in the negative direction if the negative reference switch is inactive.

oMethod 21:

The home position is in the positive direction where the reference switch becomes inactive.

oMethod 22:

The home position is in the negative direction where the reference switch becomes active.
