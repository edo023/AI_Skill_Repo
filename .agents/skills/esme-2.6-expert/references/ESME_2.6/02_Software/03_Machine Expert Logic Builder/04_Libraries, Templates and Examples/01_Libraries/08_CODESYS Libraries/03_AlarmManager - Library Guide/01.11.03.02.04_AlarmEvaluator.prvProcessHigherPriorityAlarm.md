# AlarmEvaluator.prvProcessHigherPriorityAlarm (METH)

METHOD prvProcessHigherPriorityAlarm

Checks, whether the passed alarm is “shadowed” by a higher priority alarm, i.e. is automatically acknowledged by such an alarm.
