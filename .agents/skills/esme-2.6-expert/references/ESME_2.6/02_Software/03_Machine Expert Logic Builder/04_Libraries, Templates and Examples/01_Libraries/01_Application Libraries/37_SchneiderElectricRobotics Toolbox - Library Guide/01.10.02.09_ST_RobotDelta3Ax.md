## Description

Structure to store information on the kinematic parameters of a Delta robot with three axes. Refer to *[ROB.IF\_RobotConfiguration.Delta3Ax](../../../../../api/crossBook?lang=en-US&virtualBookName=PD.Lib.Robotic&topicID=D_SE_0075523)* for further details on the parameters contained in the structure.

## Structure Elements

| Name | Data type | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| lrLengthABC0 | LREAL | Length of the distance between the axes A, B, and C and the center of the base plate. |
| lrLengthABC1 | LREAL | Length of the upper arm mounted on axis A, B, or C. |
| lrLengthABC2 | LREAL | Length of the lower arm mounted on axis A, B, or C. |
| lrLengthABC3 | LREAL | Length of the distance between the suspension points of the lower arms and the center of the flange plate. |
