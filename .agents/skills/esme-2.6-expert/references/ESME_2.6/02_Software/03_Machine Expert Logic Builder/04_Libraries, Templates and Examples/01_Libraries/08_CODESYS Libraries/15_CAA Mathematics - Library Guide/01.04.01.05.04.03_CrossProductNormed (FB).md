# CrossProductNormed (FB)

FUNCTION\_BLOCK CrossProductNormed

This function will calculate the Cartesian product of two vectors ![](images/d919ca62c83c4fc4a52026a38825d8c9.jp.jpg). The result will be returned in vector ![](images/facfbc5955c74dc1b6fe6f2bb95e9462.jp.jpg).

**Note**

Keep in mind that, due to rounding errors, the input of two collinear vectors will not necessarily result in the null vector.
