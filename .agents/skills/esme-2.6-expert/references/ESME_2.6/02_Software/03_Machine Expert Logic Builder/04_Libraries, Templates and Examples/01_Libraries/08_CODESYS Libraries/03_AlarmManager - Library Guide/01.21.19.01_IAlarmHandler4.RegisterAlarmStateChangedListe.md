# IAlarmHandler4.RegisterAlarmStateChangedListener (METH)

METHOD RegisterAlarmStateChangedListener : BOOL

Registers a listener for a change in the alarm state in order to give the possibility to call its method AlarmStateChanged() in case the alarm state changed.
