# CalcHesseRepresentation (FB)

FUNCTION\_BLOCK CalcHesseRepresentation

This function block will retrieve the normal form (due to Hesse) of a plane ![](images/284dd7d7c6ba431bb7d2e2ac977c346b.jp.jpg) in the three dimensional space: ![](images/aef8d1a4db864ea9b75cbe8fd6a7bc2b.jp.jpg), where ![](images/a9758fda48ad446f87999092d8c4813d.jp.jpg) is the normed normal on the plane and ![](images/90f0dc16044c4dc892e30d1cab054563.jp.jpg) a point on the plane. The plane is originally specified by three of its points.
