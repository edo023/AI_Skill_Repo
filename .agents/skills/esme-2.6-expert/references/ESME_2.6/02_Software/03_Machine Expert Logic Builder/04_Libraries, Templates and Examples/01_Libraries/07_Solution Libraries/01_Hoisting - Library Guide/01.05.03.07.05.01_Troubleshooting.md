# Troubleshooting

Troubleshooting

| Issue | Cause | Solution |
| --- | --- | --- |
| The axis oscillates during motion. | Proportional gain or feed forward gain of position controller are too high. | Stop the axis using reset or quick stop input. The axis will stop after the time defined in i\_stPosPara.wPosFailTime elapses.  Decrease value of proportional and/or feed forward gain. |
