# ITextListInfo.GetIdIterator (METH)

METHOD GetIdIterator : COL.IIterator

An iterator to provide the possibility to loop over all IDs of the TextList. The order of the IDs might differ to the order in the TextList.

**Note**

The returned iterator might be null.
