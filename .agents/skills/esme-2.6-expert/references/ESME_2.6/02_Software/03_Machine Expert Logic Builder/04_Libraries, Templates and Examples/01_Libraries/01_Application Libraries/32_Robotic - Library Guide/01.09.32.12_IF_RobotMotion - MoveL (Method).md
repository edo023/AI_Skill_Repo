## Task

Linear motion in three-dimensional space.

## Description

With the method MoveL(...), a motion job can be issued in order to move linearly to a target position in three-dimensional space.

For further information on trajectory behavior, refer to [Behavior of the Trajectory Inside the Blending Zones](BehaviourOfTheTrajectoryInsideTheBl-D6F69A1E.html).

| --- | --- | --- |
