# xOptimRampDecEn

xOptimRampDecEn

This parameter enables additional stop distance optimization. It allows a considerable shortening of a stop distance. Performance of this function depends on the maximum allowed steepness of acceleration and deceleration ramp (see description of i\_stPAS.wAswRampLim. Sharper acceleration and deceleration ramps results in shorter stop distances and less time but at the cost of possibly higher mechanical stress to the crane structure.
