# CrossProduct (FB)

FUNCTION\_BLOCK CrossProduct

This function will calculate the Cartesian product (outer product) of two vectors ![](images/a650862d9a854827ad7297e5f5fb48d0.jp.jpg). The result will be returned in vector ![](images/20ef8303ee974780b437c0706626d89e.jp.jpg).

**Note**

Keep in mind that, due to rounding errors, the input of two collinear vectors will not necessarily result in the null vector.
