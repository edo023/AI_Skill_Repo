The temperature inside the motor encoder is too high.

| Cause | Solution |
| --- | --- |
| Insufficient ventilation or ambient temperature too high. | Verify and correct if necessary the device fans and ventilation slots (if available). |
| Detected hardware error: The temperature sensor is inoperable. | Replace the device. |
