# 8567 Segment coil overload

## Description

[Refer to Diagnostic class (standard): 3](D-SE-0063169.html#D-SE-0063169)

| [Device reactions](D-SE-0063393.html#D-SE-0063393__D-SE-0063393.2) | Default reaction | [Minimum reaction](D-SE-0063393.html#D-SE-0063393__D-SE-0063393.4) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [Multi carrier transport system](DeviceReactions-MultiCarrier-36D130D7.html#DeviceReactions-MultiCarrier-36D130D7) | AD | – |

One or several coils of the multi carrier segment are overloaded.

| Cause | Solution |
| --- | --- |
| A carrier is mechanically blocked. | Verify that the carrier can move freely. |
| The mechanical load on the carrier is too heavy. | * Verify the permissible total mass of the carrier. * Reduce the acceleration at this position of the track. |
