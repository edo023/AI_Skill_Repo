# 8564 Carrier is lost

## Description

[Refer to Diagnostic class (standard): 3](D-SE-0063169.html#D-SE-0063169)

| [Device reactions](D-SE-0063393.html#D-SE-0063393__D-SE-0063393.2) | Default reaction | [Minimum reaction](D-SE-0063393.html#D-SE-0063393__D-SE-0063393.4) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [Multi carrier transport system](DeviceReactions-MultiCarrier-36D130D7.html#DeviceReactions-MultiCarrier-36D130D7) | AD | – |

A carrier is no longer recognized by the encoder system.

| Cause | Solution |
| --- | --- |
| A carrier has been removed from the Multi carrier transport system. | * Verify that the carriers are correctly installed on the track. * Verify the encoder magnet of the carrier. Replace the encoder magnet or the carrier if necessary. * Replace the carrier or the segment. |
| The encoder system is disturbed or inoperable. |
