# Collecting Data

## Overview

To open the  Connect to controller via... window, click the Collect data...  button in the Home  window. The Connect to controller via... window appears in one of the variants described in this chapter. By default, the General tab is selected. This tab includes the Network Device Identification function and allows you to access a controller for selecting data.

To connect to a PacDrive M controller, click the PacDriveM  tab. The specific settings for PacDrive M controllers are described in the chapter [*Connecting to PacDriveM Controllers*](D-SE-0042151.html#D-SE-0042151).
