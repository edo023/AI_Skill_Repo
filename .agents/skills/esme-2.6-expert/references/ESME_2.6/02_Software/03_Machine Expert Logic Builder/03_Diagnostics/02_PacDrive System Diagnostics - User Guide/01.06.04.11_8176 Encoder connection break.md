# 8176 Encoder connection break

The physical connection to the encoder is interrupted.

| Cause | Solution |
| --- | --- |
| Physical connection to the encoder interrupted. | Verify correct physical connection to the encoder. |

The following diagnostic messages provide additional information on the effects of the physical connection interruption to the encoder communication:
