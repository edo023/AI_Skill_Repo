# 8553 Segment power consumption near limit

## Description

[Refer to Diagnostic class (standard): 2](D-SE-0063169.html#D-SE-0063169)

| [Device reactions](D-SE-0063393.html#D-SE-0063393__D-SE-0063393.2) | Default reaction | [Minimum reaction](D-SE-0063393.html#D-SE-0063393__D-SE-0063393.4) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [Multi carrier transport system](DeviceReactions-MultiCarrier-36D130D7.html#DeviceReactions-MultiCarrier-36D130D7) | D | – |

The power consumption of a multi carrier segment is high.

| Cause | Solution |
| --- | --- |
| High acceleration of several carriers on one segment to a high velocity.  NOTE: An even higher load can cause the internal current monitoring to trip and then lead to the error [8109 DC bus undervoltage](D-SE-0063851.html#D-SE-0063851). | * Reduce the acceleration. * Reduce the velocity. * Reduce the number of accelerated carriers per segment. * Distribute the acceleration of the carriers over several segments. |
