# FAQ

| Question | Answer |
| --- | --- |
| When a PROFINET Device or a Module is marked red (error), does that affect other devices or modules? | No. They are not affected during a data exchange. |
| Is a diagnosis in the field device is deleted when I reset or restart the application? | This varies: Hardware problems are not solved by this, but errors by an incorrect parameterization are. |
| Can a controller delete or confirm the diagnosis of the PROFINET Device? | No, the PROFINET Device manages the diagnosis entries and sets the status of the modules. The PROFINET Controller merely confirms the receipt of incoming alarms at the log level. |
