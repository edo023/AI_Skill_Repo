# Device and Submodule Status

In addition to direct access to the outputs of the [device function block](a7cb6e4a8d7fd9cce97d157237e88bb0.html#UUID-a3f59a59-fdf5-e8be-cce5-671ee57b205e_section-idm4527713815736032611709937608), there is also the possibility of using the `ProfinetCommon.DeviceIterator` POU to iterate over all devices engineered for a controller.

In the same way, the `ProfinetCommon.SubmoduleIterator` POU is used for iterating the submodules configured for a PROFINET Device and for evaluating the individual `ProfinetCommon.SubmoduleState`.
