# Diagnostic Messages DM1 and DM2

For DM1 and DM2 messages, function blocks for receiving (in the case of remote ECUs) and also for sending (in the case of local ECUs) are available in the `IoDrvJ1939` library.

* `DM1_Read`
* `DM2_Read`
* `DM1_Write`
* `DM2_Write`
