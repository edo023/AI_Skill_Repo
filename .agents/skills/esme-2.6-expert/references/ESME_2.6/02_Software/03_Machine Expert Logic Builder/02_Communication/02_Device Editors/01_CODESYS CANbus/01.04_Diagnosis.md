# Diagnosis

This chapter describes the general diagnosis in the case of CAN problems – regardless of the used fieldbus stack. Most problems are traced back to incorrect wiring or faulty bus device or CAN driver. Therefore it is advisable to refer to the [Diagnosis of Hardware Failures](0130877728d24fce85384a90b7f51e01.html#UUID-7e738421-21dd-2b6a-953c-bf3c2b9199c1_section-idm460850454796963257014143003) chapter as well as the diagnosis counters of the respective CAN controllers.
