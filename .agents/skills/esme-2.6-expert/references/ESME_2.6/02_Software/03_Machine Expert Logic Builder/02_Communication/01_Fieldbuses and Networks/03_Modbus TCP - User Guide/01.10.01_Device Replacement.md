# Device Replacement

In case of device replacement, the main steps are:

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Power off the machine or the part of the machine affected. |
| 2 | Unmount the device. |
| 3 | Mount the new device. |
| 4 | Power on the new device. |
| 5 | [Prepare the device to be recognized by the system](D-SE-0056612.html#D-SE-0056612). |
| 6 | [Apply the correct device configuration](D-SE-0057261.html#D-SE-0057261). |
| 7 | Acknowledge the device replacement (depends on your application). |
