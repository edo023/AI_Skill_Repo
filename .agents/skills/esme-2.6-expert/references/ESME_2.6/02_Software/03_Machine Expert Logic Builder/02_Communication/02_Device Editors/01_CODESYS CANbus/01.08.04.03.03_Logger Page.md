# Logger Page

A filtered view of the [PLC logger](../../../../../../api/crossBook?lang=en-US&virtualBookName=../&topicID=) is displayed at both Controller level and Device level.

This shows only the entries that were created by the respective device, for example change of the CANopen state, emergency messages, SDO errors, and monitoring errors.
