## Industrial Ethernet Port

To configure the Industrial Ethernet port:

1. Double-click one of the following nodes in the Devices tree:

   * TM241CE•••: MyController > Ethernet\_1
   * TM251MESE: MyController > Ethernet\_2
   * TM262•: MyController > Ethernet\_1 or MyController > Ethernet\_2
2. Configure the network settings.
