# Visualization Element: Legend

Symbol:

![](images/uuid-1f79f6bb-8f36-1068-6f72-80e246.png)

Category: **Special Controls**

The element is used as a legend for another element (for example, a trend). The legend is assigned in the properties of the other element.

IMPORTANT:

**Not available in integrated visualizations**

The use of trends or alarms and the elements required for them is not provided in an integrated visualization.
