# Compatibility of Controller Connection Mechanism

## Overview

To connect to a controller, different mechanisms are supported. For detailed information, refer to the respective chapters in the EcoStruxure Machine Expert Programming Guide ([Communication Settings in Controller Selection Mode](../../../../../api/crossBook?lang=en-US&virtualBookName=SoMProg&topicID=D_SE_0083385), [Communication Settings in Simple Mode](../../../../../api/crossBook?lang=en-US&virtualBookName=SoMProg&topicID=D_SE_0083386), [Communication Settings in Classic Mode](../../../../../api/crossBook?lang=en-US&virtualBookName=SoMProg&topicID=D_SE_0088469)).

## Restrictions for SoMachine / SoMachine Motion Controllers

For SoMachine / SoMachine Motion controllers (firmware compatible to SoMachine V3.1 and earlier), the following functionalities are available:

* Find controller.
* Select controller.
* Connect controller.

For SoMachine / SoMachine Motion controllers (firmware compatible to SoMachine V3.1 and earlier), the following functionalities are not available:

* Identify controller.
* Get information.
