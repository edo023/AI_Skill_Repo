# Effect of the velocity profile on the use of MC\_SetOverride

It is generally recommended to use `MC_SetOverride` together with a trapezoidal or quadratic velocity profile of the axes. Quadratic-smoothed and sin² velocity profiles do not respond well to restarting or interrupting the movement in certain cases (see [Interruption of Movements](_sm_interrupt_single_axis_movement.html#_sm_interrupt_single_axis_movement)). Because a change to the override is equivalent to a restart of the movement with changed limits, it is also possible for the commanded limits to be exceeded when using these velocity profiles in combination with `MC_SetOverride`.
