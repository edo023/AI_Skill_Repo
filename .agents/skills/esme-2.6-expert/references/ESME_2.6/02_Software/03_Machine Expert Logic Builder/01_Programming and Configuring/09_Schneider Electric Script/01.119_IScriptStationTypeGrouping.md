grouping\_pattern

The grouping pattern for this station type in shorthand notation. The grouping pattern defines A) the number of groups B) the number of carriers in each group C) the process gap of the first carrier in a group and D) the inside gap of the following carriers. In the shorthand notation the groups are separated by '/' and the gaps are sparated by ':'. Example: "30:20:10/40:10" defines that there are two groups, the first group has three carriers, with process gap = 30 and inside gaps = 20 and 10. The second group has two carrier with process gap = 40 and inside gap = 10.
