# Configuring a "background image"

Requirement: A project with a visualization is open.

1. Open the visualization and click **Visualization → Background**.
2. In the **Input Assistant** dialog, select an image.

   * The image is used as the background image of the visualization. Do not confuse this with the "device image", which can also be used for help when creating a visualization.

TIP:

In the [Dialog: Properties](_visu_dlg_object_properties.html#_visu_dlg_object_properties) dialog of a visualization object, you can use the **Include background image** property also to configure whether the background image is always displayed completely or truncated.
