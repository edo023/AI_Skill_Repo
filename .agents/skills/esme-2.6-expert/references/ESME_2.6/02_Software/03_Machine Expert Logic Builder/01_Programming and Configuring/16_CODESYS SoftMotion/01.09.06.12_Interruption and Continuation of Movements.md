# Interruption and Continuation of Movements

While an axis group is in motion, the movement can be stopped. This can be an intentional stop (`MC_GroupHalt`, `MC_GroupStop`), or a pause on the path (`MC_GroupInterrupt`), or a halt due to an error. In all cases, CODESYS SoftMotion provides function blocks to move the axis group back to the planned path and continue it.
