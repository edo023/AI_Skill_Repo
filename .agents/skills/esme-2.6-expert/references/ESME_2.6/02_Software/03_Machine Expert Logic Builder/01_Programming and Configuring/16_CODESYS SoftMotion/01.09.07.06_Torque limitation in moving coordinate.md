# Torque limitation in moving coordinate systems

If the axis group moves in a movable coordinate system, then the torque limits can be respected only when the axis group can follow the movable coordinate system exactly. If this is not possible and tolerances for the deviation are configured by means of `SMC_GroupSetPathTolerance`, then torque limits may be exceeded when trying to compensate the deviation. If an error occurs because the deviations are outside the tolerances, then the axis group stops. During this stop, the torque limits may also be exceeded. The set torques are calculated in each case and sent to the drives.
