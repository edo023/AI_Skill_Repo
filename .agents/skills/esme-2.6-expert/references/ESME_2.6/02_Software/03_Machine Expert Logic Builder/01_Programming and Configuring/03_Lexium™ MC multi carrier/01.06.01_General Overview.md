# General Overview

The Multicarrier Configuration editor provides the following tabs:

* Overview

  Provides an overview of the tracks in multi-track mode.

  Refer to [Overview Tab (Exclusive to Multi-track Mode)](OverviewTab-E4C6B98A.html).
* Track

  Your track is made of segments.

  This is a track-specific tab. In multi-track mode, it is displayed under the track editor.

  Refer to [Track Tab](TPC_MLS-Config_Tab_Track-D99C4272.html).
* Stations

  Stations are used to process products, such as loading, filling, labeling, unloading a product.

  This is a track-specific tab. In multi-track mode, it is displayed under the track editor.

  Refer to [Stations Tab](TPC_MLS-Config_Tab_Stations-DA9DBC46.html).
* Station Types

  The Station Types tab allows you to define a set of property values for a specific kind of standard station, such as Clamping, Declamping or Grouping.

  Refer to [Station Types Tab](StationTypeTab-F03E51E0.html).
* Carriers

  Carriers are used to transport the products on your track.

  Refer to [Carriers Tab](TPC_MLS-Config_Tab_Carriers-DAAA0843.html).
* Tools

  Tools are mounted on the carriers to hold the products during the transport.

  Refer to [Tools Tab](TPC_MLS-Config_Tab_Tools-DCC4C529.html).
* Products

  Your products are held by the tools on the carriers and transported on the carriers on your track.

  Refer to [Products Tab](TPC_MLS-Config_Tab_Products-DDB83FF9.html).
* Emulation

  The 3-D emulation displays your configured track allowing you to visualize your application running virtually.

  The Emulation tab emulates either a scene that contains all tracks of the project (only for multi-track projects) or the track selected with the track editor.

  Refer to [Emulation Tab](TPC_MLS-Config_Tab_Visualization-DA963F21.html).
