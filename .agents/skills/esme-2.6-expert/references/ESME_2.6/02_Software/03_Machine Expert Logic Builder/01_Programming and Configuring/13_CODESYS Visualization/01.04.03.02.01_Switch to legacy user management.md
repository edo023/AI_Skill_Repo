# Switch to legacy user management

You have initially created a runtime-based user management. Now you want to decouple this from the device user management and have a separate management only for the visualization users.

1. In the Visualization Manager, open the **User Management** tab and click the **Groups** tab.
2. Click the **Convert to Legacy** button.

   * User management is decoupled from device user management and converted to legacy. The **Users** tab opens.
