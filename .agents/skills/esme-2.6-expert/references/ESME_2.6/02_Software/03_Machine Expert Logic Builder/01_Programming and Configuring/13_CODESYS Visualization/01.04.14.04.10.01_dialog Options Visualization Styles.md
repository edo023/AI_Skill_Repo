# Dialog: Options: Visualization Styles

Symbol: ![](images/uuid-3056b4cc-718a-0fb0-8b73-132c10.png)

**Function**: The dialog is used for configuring the display of library visualizations and visualizations in the POU view in the visualization editor. In addition, it is used for configuring the tab **Visualization Manager** – **Settings** (**Style Settings** group).

**Call**: **Tools → Options** menu, **Visualization Styles** category

These settings are not applied at visualization runtime. At runtime, only the settings of the Visualization Manager are available on the **Settings** tab.

NOTE:

Most options are applied to elements in the POU view and not generally to the device tree view.
