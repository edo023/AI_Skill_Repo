## Description

The method AddSlaveChannel of the interface [IF\_MultiAxisCustomJob](IF_MultiAxisCustomJob-4D7A4899.html) assigns a subordinate axis to the axis group of the multi-axis custom job. You must add at least one subordinate channel for the multi-axis custom job to operate. When the multi-axis custom job is started, the subordinate axes transfer control to the custom job, and any motion jobs currently running on these axes are aborted.

This method must be called before the multi-axis custom job is started. Subordinate channels cannot be added or modified while the job is in the state Executing.

For details on the interaction of function blocks, interfaces and methods of a multi-axis custom job, refer to [Multi-Axis Custom Jobs](Multi-AxisCustomJobs-572EC46B.html).
