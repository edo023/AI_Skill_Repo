# General Information

The Lexium™ MC multi carrier is a transport system to be used in machines. It uses linear motion technology to move products individually through the machine.

Carriers are moved on a closed or open track consisting of arc and straight segments. Process steps can be decoupled and run at different velocities.

Attachment devices, made by you and herein called “tools”, must be installed on the carriers to transport the products on the track.

NOTE: For a general product overview including video presentations, refer to the [Lexium™ MC12 multi carrier](https://www.se.com/ww/en/product-range/52642224-lexium%E2%84%A2-mc12-multi-carrier-/) website.
