Task

Solutions of the 3nd order equation

![G-SE-0068641.1.gif-high.gif](images/G-SE-0068641.1.gif-high.gif)

Description

The solutions are complex numbers which are returned in a structure of type [ST\_PolynomialZeros](../Structures/Structures-39.htm#XREF_D_SE_0087784_1).
