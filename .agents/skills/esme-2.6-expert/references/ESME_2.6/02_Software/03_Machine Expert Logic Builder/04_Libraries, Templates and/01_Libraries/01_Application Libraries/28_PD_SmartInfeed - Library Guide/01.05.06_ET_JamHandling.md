Description

The enumeration describes the strategy with which SmartInfeed handles overlapping products.

Products can overlap at the product transfer between two belts if the subsequent product moves slower than the preceding one. In this situation, it might happen that the preceding belt shifts its first product onto the subsequent belt, although there already is a product located at this position on the subsequent belt.

Enumeration Elements

| Name | Value | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| None | 0 | Products may overlap. The position of the products is determined according to the motions of the belts. |
| StrictFrontProduct | 1 | Overlapping is prevented. If two products have to overlap due to the motions of the belts, the preceding product is assumed as fixed. The front edge of the subsequent product must not move beyond the rear edge of the preceding product. |
