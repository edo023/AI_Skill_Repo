## Task

Forward transformation of the kinematics.

## Description

The positions of the axes are transferred at the i\_lrPositionA, i\_lrPositionB, i\_lrPositionC, i\_lrPositionD , i\_lrPositionE , and i\_lrPositionF inputs.

They must be converted to Cartesian coordinates and/or orientations within this method and returned as return values at the q\_stCoordinate and q\_stOrientation outputs. The orientation must be provided in the convention ET\_OrientationConvention.ZYX.

Those calculated values are further processed by the robot.
