## Task

Additional transformation for the TCP.

## Description

Your function block implementing the additional transformation for the TCP (Tool Center Point) must extend the FB\_AdditionalTransformationTCP.

Also refer to chapter [Using AdditionalTransformationTCP](D-SE-0082754.html#D-SE-0082754).

You cannot overwrite the method Change. The access modifier FINAL is set.
