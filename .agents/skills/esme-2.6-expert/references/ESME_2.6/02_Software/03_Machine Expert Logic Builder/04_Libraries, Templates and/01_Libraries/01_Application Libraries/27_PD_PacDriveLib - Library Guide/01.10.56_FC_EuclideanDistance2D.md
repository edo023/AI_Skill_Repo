Task

Calculates the Euclidean distance between two points in the 2-dimensional space.

Description

Calculates the Euclidean distance between two points in the 2-dimensional space, which are given by their position vectors. The Euclidean distance is defined to be the square root of the sum of the squares of the elements of the vector.
