Task

Rotation of a point about a straight line in the 3-dimensional space.

Description

The point i\_stP is rotated by an angle of i\_lrAngle in its orthogonal plane of the straight line i\_stLine. The angle of rotation is in this case interpreted in the mathematically positive direction (clockwise) around a vector along the straight line, with the orientation of point 2 of the straight line of point 1.
