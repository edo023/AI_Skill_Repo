| Issue | Cause | Solution |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Calculated load spectrum values do not correspond to actual usage of the crane. | Load information is incorrect or inaccurate. | Verify the actual load values during operation of the hoist. |
| Motor speeds oscillate during synchronized movement. | Proportional gain of the position controller is too great. | Decrease the value of proportional gain. Start a trace of motor speeds and position deviations of synchronized axes. |
