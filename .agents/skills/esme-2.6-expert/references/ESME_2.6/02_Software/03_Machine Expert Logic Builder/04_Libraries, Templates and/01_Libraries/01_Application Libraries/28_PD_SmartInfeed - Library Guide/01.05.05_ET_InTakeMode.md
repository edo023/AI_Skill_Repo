# ET_InTakeMode

![](images/logo.png)

ET\_InTakeMode

ET\_InTakeMode - General Information

Overview

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| Type: | List type |
| Available as of: | V1.0.0.0 |
| Versions: | Current version |

Description

The enumeration describes the method for a belt detecting the acceptance of a new product.

Enumeration Elements

| Name | Value | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| NoTpLeadEdge | 1 | No Touchprobe is allocated to the belt. The belt cannot detect any new products. It only takes over products of the preceding belt.  A product is allocated to a belt as soon as the front edge of the product has moved onto the belt further than [lrInTakeLength](../Interfaces/Interfaces-2.htm#XREF_D_SE_0080311_1). The product length is kept. |
| NoTpTrialEdge | 2 | No Touchprobe is allocated to the belt. The belt cannot detect any new products. It only takes over products of the preceding belt.  A product is allocated to the belt as soon as the rear edge of the product is located on the belt. The product length is kept. |
| TpLeadEdgeSetLength | 3 | The belt takes over the products that are detected by the filtered signals of the [ifTouchProbe](../Interfaces/Interfaces-34.htm#XREF_D_SE_0080349_1).  A new product is detected as soon as the belt has passed a distance of more than [lrInTakeLength](../Interfaces/Interfaces-2.htm#XREF_D_SE_0080311_1) after the front edge of the product was detected. The length of the product is set to [lrSetLength](../Interfaces/Interfaces-2.htm#XREF_D_SE_0080311_1). |
| TpLeadEdgeMeasure | 4 | The belt takes over the products that are detected by the filtered signals of the [ifTouchProbe](../Interfaces/Interfaces-34.htm#XREF_D_SE_0080349_1).  A new product is detected as soon as the belt has passed a distance of more than [lrInTakeLength](../Interfaces/Interfaces-2.htm#XREF_D_SE_0080311_1) after the front edge of the product was detected. The length of the product is measured by means of the signals of the [ifTouchProbe](../Interfaces/Interfaces-34.htm#XREF_D_SE_0080349_1). |
| TpTrialEdgeSetLength | 5 | The belt takes over the products that are detected by the filtered signals of the [ifTouchProbe](../Interfaces/Interfaces-34.htm#XREF_D_SE_0080349_1).  A new product is detected as soon as [ifTouchProbe](../Interfaces/Interfaces-34.htm#XREF_D_SE_0080349_1) has detected a new product end. The length of the product is set to [lrSetLength](../Interfaces/Interfaces-2.htm#XREF_D_SE_0080311_1). |
| TpTrialEdgeMeasure | 6 | The belt takes over the products that are detected by the filtered signals of the [ifTouchProbe](../Interfaces/Interfaces-34.htm#XREF_D_SE_0080349_1).  A new product is detected as soon as [ifTouchProbe](../Interfaces/Interfaces-34.htm#XREF_D_SE_0080349_1) has detected a new product end. The length of the product is measured by means of the signals of the [ifTouchProbe](../Interfaces/Interfaces-34.htm#XREF_D_SE_0080349_1). |
