## Description

Structure to store information on the kinematic parameters of an articulated robot with two axes. Refer to *[ROB.IF\_RobotConfiguration.Articulates2Ax](../../../../../api/crossBook?lang=en-US&virtualBookName=PD.Lib.Robotic&topicID=D_SE_0075516)* for details on the parameters contained in the structure.
