* SR\_<MyRobot>.ifRobotFeedback

  Property ifRobotFeedback of type ROB.IF\_RobotFeedback added.

  General robotic feedback interface.
* SR\_<MyRobot>.ifTSeriesFeedback

  Property ifTSeriesFeedback of type SER.IF\_TSeriesFeedback added.

  Feedback to verify whether a Lexium T Robot reaches a mechanical limit, even without a real kinematic.
