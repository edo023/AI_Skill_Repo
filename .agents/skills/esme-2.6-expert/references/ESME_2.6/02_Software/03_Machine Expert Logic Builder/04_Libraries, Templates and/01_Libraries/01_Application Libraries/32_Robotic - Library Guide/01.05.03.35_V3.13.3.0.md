## Modifications

* The verification of the emergency parameters in the method ConfigDone() is improved.
* If tracking moves the start or target position of a MoveJ() out of the work envelope, the calculation of the according axes positions is improved.
* The behavior of the robot in case ClearSegmentsFromId() is called when tracking is active and a MoveSync() is called after a MoveAsync()is improved.
* In some rare cases, MoveAsync() incorrectly reported that MoveSync() was already triggered because it depends on RTP cycles. This behavior is improved.
* The behavior of warmstart is improved.
* TrackingSource is improved to work with FastMove.
