| --- | --- |

## Task

Moving the carriers in the process out of the station.

## Description

With the method DriveProcessEmpty, you can move the carriers in the process out of the station.

Additionally, the station is set to hold: Carriers that are waiting at the waiting position will not be moved into the process.

For reactivating the process of the station, you must call the method [Restart](Restart-EED689B5.html#Restart-EED689B5).

## Inputs

The method has no inputs.

## Outputs

The method has no outputs.
