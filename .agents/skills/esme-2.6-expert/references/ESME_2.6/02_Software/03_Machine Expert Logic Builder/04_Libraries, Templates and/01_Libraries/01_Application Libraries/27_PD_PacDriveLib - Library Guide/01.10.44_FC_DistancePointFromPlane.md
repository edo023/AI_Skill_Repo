| Available as of: | V1.0.3.0 |
| Versions: | Current version |

Task

Calculates the distance of a point from a plane in a 3-dimensional space.

Description

This calculates the distance of the point i\_stPoint from the plane i\_stPlane. The calculated distance is marked with a sign. The sign states in which of the half spaces defined by the plane i\_stPlane the point i\_stPoint is located. In addition, the perpendicular of the point onto the plane is calculated.
