## Task

Axes of the robot move to a specific position to verify whether the robot is calibrated correctly.

## Description

By calling up the method, the axes of the Lexium P Robot move to a specific position. At the end of the movement, verify whether the ball pins have the same gap.

For additional information, refer to the chapter [Calibration](D-SE-0075364.html#D-SE-0075364).
