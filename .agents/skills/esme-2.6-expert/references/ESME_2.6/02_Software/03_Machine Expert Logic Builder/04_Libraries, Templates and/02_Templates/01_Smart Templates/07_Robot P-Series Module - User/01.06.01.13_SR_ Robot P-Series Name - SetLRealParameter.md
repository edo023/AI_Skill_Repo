## Task

Set/overwrite a parameter for a P-Series robot.

## Description

By calling the method, a parameter contained in the enumeration *[ET\_Parameter](../../../../../api/crossBook?lang=en-US&virtualBookName=PD.Lib.SchneiderElectricRoboticsParameters&topicID=D_SE_0081304)* can be parametrized.

NOTE: The method must be called after the method *[FB\_RobotPSeries.InitializeRobot](../../../../../api/crossBook?lang=en-US&virtualBookName=PD.Lib.SchneiderElectricRoboticsParameters&topicID=D_SE_0074974)* has been called but before IF\_Configuration.SchneiderElectricRobot (SER.FB\_RobotPSeries) of Robotic/RoboticModule is called.
