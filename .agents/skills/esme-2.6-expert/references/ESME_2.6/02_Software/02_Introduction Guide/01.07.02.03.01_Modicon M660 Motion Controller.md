# Modicon M660 Motion Controller

## Overview

The M660 controller family consists of two types.

M660C devices are purpose-built motion controllers. Features include:

* Up to 64 axes with Sercos
* I/O cycle time as low as 1 ms
* Intel Raptor Lake-P processors

M660P controllers include the capabilities of an industrial PC. The industrial PC opens up opportunities to implement, for example:

* Data acquisition and processing at the edge instead of at a remote, centralized host
* OS virtualization (Windows)
* Extensive logging, analytics and diagnostics
* Visualization, dashboards

M660 controllers support Sercos topologies such as line, star with switch, ring, and ring of ring with switches that allow you to implement complex systems for distributed real-time data transfer and distributed synchronous motion with Modicon Edge I/O NTS modules, servo drives, motors and third-party equipment.

Depending on the version, M660P controllers feature SSDs with a storage capacity of up to 1 GB.

M660C and M660P controllers are equipped with two USB 3.2 Gen 1 Type-A ports. M660P controllers are equipped with an additional USB 2.0 Type-A port.

The programming environment is EcoStruxure Machine Expert (compliant with IEC 61131-3)​.
