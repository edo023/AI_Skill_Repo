## Box iPC Modular and Display

The HMIBMU is the Universal Box iPC modular.

The HMIBMP is the Performance Box iPC modular.

The HMIBMU/BMP have an operating voltage of 24 Vdc.

The following table describes the Universal and Performance Box iPCs modular:

| Reference | Processor | USB | Serial Port | Ethernet Port | Other Interface |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| HMIBMU | Celeron-2980U  1.6 GHz | 4 | 1 | 2 | 2 x Display ports,  2 x HDD/SSD slots,  CFast slot,  Slide-in compact slot,  2 x PCI/PCie slots,  2 x Optional interfaces. |
| HMIBMP | i7-4650U  1.7 GHz | 4 | 1 | 2 |

Display for HMIBMx:

* HMIDM6422 - Display 4:3 12” single touch
* HMIDM6522 - Display W12” multi-touch
* HMIDM7421 - Display 4:3 15” single touch
* HMIDM7521 - Display W15” multi-touch
* HMIDM9521 - Display W19” multi-touch
* HMIDMA521 - Display W22” multi-touch
