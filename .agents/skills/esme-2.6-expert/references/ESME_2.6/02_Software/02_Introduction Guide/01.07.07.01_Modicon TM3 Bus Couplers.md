# Modicon TM3 Bus Couplers

## Introduction

The TM3 bus couplers are devices designed to manage EtherNet/IP, Modbus TCP, Modbus Serial Line or CANopen communications, depending on the reference, in association with TM3 / TM2 I/O expansion modules in a distributed architecture.

For more details, see [Modicon TM3 Bus Couplers - Hardware Guide](../../../../../api/crossBook?lang=en-US&virtualBookName=tm3bchw&topicID=D_SE_0084312).
