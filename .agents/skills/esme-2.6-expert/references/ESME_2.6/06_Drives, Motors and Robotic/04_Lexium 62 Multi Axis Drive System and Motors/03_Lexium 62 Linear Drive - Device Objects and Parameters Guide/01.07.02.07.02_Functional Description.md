# Functional Description

Functional Description

The reaction in the drive is defined by ControllerEnableStopMode when Controller­Enable is switched off.

| ControllerEnableStopMode | Reaction when switching off ControllerEnable |
| --- | --- |
| User-defined stop / 0 | [Reaction](../../../../../../api/crossBook?lang=en-US&virtualBookName=PD.Diagnostic&topicID=D_SE_0063389_1) BD2 (default): based on the state of the drive, a reference value profile is calculated in the drive according to the ControllerStopDec, ControllerStopJerk, and UserDefinedStopMode parameter. The brake behaves according to the behavior defined by BrakeMode. By default, it engages at a speed of rotation of less than 10 revolutions per minute. The drive becomes torque-free after [StopTimeLim](../General_2/General_2-9.htm#XREF_D_SE_0071531_1) + [BrakeCouplingTime](../Motor_2/Motor_2-16.htm#XREF_D_SE_0071793_1) at the latest. If the speed of rotation exceeds 10 rpm after StopTimeLim (default value: 400 ms), then diagnostic message 8140 Motor StopTimeLim exceeded is triggered. |
| Best possible stop / 1 | [Reaction](../../../../../../api/crossBook?lang=en-US&virtualBookName=PD.Diagnostic&topicID=D_SE_0063389_1) BD1: drive is stopped with [MaxDrivePeakCurrent](../General_2/General_2-6.htm#XREF_D_SE_0071528_1) peak current. MaxDrivePeakCurrent  can be influenced via [UserDrivePeakCurrent](../General_2/General_2-7.htm#XREF_D_SE_0071529_1). The brake behaves according to the behavior defined by BrakeMode. By default, it engages at a speed of rotation of less than 10 revolutions per minute. The drive becomes torque-free after [StopTimeLim](../General_2/General_2-9.htm#XREF_D_SE_0071531_1) + [BrakeCouplingTime](../Motor_2/Motor_2-16.htm#XREF_D_SE_0071793_1) at the latest. If the speed of rotation exceeds 10 rpm after StopTimeLim (default value: 400 ms), then diagnostic message 8140 Motor StopTimeLim exceeded is triggered. |
| Coast down / 2 | [Reaction](../../../../../../api/crossBook?lang=en-US&virtualBookName=PD.Diagnostic&topicID=D_SE_0063389_1) AD: the motor is connected torque-free. The brake behaves according to the behavior defined by BrakeMode. By default, it engages immediately. |

NOTE: Due to the reaction AD, the motor holding brake can incur significant wear to the point of becoming ineffective.
