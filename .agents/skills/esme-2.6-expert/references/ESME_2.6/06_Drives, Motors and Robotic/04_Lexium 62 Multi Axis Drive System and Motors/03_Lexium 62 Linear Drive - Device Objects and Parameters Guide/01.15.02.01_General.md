# General

General

The YOffset Generator is a position generator for easily generating Y offsets for an axis. For example, this is required to perform axis correction using [Touchprobe](../../../../../../api/crossBook?lang=en-US&virtualBookName=PD.Lib.SystemInterface&topicID=D_SE_0085588_1) (measured input). This generator works independently of other [motion generators](../../../../../../api/crossBook?lang=en-US&virtualBookName=RefActualValues/RefActualValues-1.htm#XREF_D_SE_0071489_1) on the axis. For instance, while a [MultiCam()](../../../PD.Lib.PacDriveLib&topicID=D_SE_0087312_1) function block is running, a correction is being performed simultaneously through the YOffset generator.
