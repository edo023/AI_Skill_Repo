# System Internals

## General

This chapter contains internal system parameters. With these parameters, real-time data can be modified. Without extensive knowledge of the PacDrive system and the consequential potential for the incorrect use of these parameters, there is a substantial risk for machine damage or injury during the commissioning of the machine or thereafter. Observe all possible safety-related measures when using these parameters.

| WARNING | |
| --- | --- |
|  | UNINTENDED MACHINE OPERATION  * Ensure that only qualified personnel having full knowledge of PacDrive systems are allowed to affect internal system parameters. * Ensure that all personnel designing and commissioning equipment based on PacDrive systems have received safety training to recognize and avoid the hazards involved when affecting internal system parameters. * Contact your local Schneider Electric service representative for more information concerning the consequences of affecting internal system parameters.  Failure to follow these instructions can result in death, serious injury, or equipment damage. |
