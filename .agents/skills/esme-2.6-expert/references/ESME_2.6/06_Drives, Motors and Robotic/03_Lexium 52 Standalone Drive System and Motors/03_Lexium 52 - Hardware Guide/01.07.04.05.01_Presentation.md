# Presentation

Presentation

Keep a stock of the most important components to make certain your machine is functioning and ready for operation.

Replace devices with the same hardware configuration to help ensure compatibility.

Indicate the following information on the replacement equipment order:

oUnicode: LXM52DU60C

oHardware revision: for example, RS:02

This information can be found on the logistic [nameplate](../LMC100HW_System_Overview/LMC100HW_System_Overview-8.htm#XREF_D_SE_0049330_1).

For more information concerning the replacement of components, refer to [Replacing Components and Cables](LMC100HW_Installation_and_Maintenance-13.htm#XREF_D_SE_0049351_1).
