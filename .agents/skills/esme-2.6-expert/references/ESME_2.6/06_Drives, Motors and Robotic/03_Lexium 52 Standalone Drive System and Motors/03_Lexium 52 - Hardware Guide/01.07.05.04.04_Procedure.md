# Procedure

Procedure

Proceed as follows for cable replacement:

oBe sure that the cables clearly indicate their connections before disconnecting.

oReplace cables with an identical type and length.

oRefer to any documentation from the original machine manufacturer before replacing cables.

oDisconnect/Attach the cable from the equipment components involved.
