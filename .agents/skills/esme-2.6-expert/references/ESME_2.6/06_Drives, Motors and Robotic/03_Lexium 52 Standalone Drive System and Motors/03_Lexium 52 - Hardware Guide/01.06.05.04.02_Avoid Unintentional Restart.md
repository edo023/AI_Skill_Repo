# Avoid Unintentional Restart

Avoid Unintentional Restart

The unintentional restart of the equipment must be avoided by appropriate means, depending on your particular application.

|  |
| --- |
| Danger_Color.gifDANGER |
| UNINTENTIONAL RESTART OF THE MOTOR |
| oEnsure that a restart of the motor is not possible after a return of power or the tripping of a functional safety device unless preceded by a deliberate enable signal from the system.  oEnsure that the enable signal meets the specified safety criteria. |
| Failure to follow these instructions will result in death or serious injury. |
