# Document Scope

Document Scope

Read and understand the material contained in this manual before you work on the Lexium 52 for the first time. Take particular note of the chapter [Specific Safety Information](../LMC100HW_Specific_Safety_Information/LMC100HW_Specific_Safety_Information-2.htm#XREF_D_SE_0049322_1). Only those persons who meet the criteria described in [Qualification of Personnel](../LMC100HW_Specific_Safety_Information/LMC100HW_Specific_Safety_Information-4.htm#XREF_D_SE_0049321_1) are allowed to work with the Lexium 52.

A copy of this manual must be available for personnel who work with the Lexium 52.

This manual is to help you use the capabilities of the Lexium 52 safely and properly.

Follow the instructions within this manual to help:

oReduce risks

oReduce repair costs and downtime of the Lexium 52

oIncrease the service life of the Lexium 52

oIncrease reliability of the Lexium 52
