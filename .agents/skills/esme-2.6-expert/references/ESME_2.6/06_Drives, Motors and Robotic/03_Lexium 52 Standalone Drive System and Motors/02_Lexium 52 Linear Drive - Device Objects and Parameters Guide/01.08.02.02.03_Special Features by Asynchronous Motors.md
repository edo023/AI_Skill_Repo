# Special Features by Asynchronous Motors

Special Features by Asynchronous Motors

By asynchronous motors the rated speed is parameterized as standard in MaxVel. By the V/F control this leads to that the rated speed is not being reached by rated torque. This happens because the maximum stator frequency is defined with MaxVel without considering the slip. To reach the rated speed by rated torque here, the UserMaxVel has to be parameterized with the idle running speed. The maximum speed is not monitored by asynchronous motors without encoder (in open-loop V / f control mode, [ControlMode](../../../../../../api/crossBook?lang=en-US&virtualBookName=PD.Parameter.LXM52Drive&topicID=D_SE_0071561_1) = open-loop control / 1).

NOTE: MaxVel is limited by firmware to uiNominalSpeed \* 0.9. For a higher velocity use the [UserMaxVel](General_2-3.htm#XREF_D_SE_0071525_1) parameter.
