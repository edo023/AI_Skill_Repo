# General

General

If on the release of [ControllerEnable](../State_2/State_2-9.htm#XREF_D_SE_0071477_1) is waited for, then AxisState is 2 and [InternalDeviceState](../State_2/State_2-16.htm#XREF_D_SE_0071488_1) 0xA1. When switching on ControllerEnable (1), the brake relay is closed (brake released), and the motor torque is turned on if no error message is present and the DC bus is loaded. After the [BrakeDisconnectionTime](../Motor_2/Motor_2-15.htm#XREF_D_SE_0071792_1) has lapsed (Standard value: 100 ms) (2), the AxisState switches to 3 and the travel orders can be sent to the drive.
