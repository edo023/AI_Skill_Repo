# Electrical Issues

| Issue | Cause | Solution |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Motor does not start or has difficulty starting | Overload | Reduce load. |
| Unsuitable settings for the drive | Correct drive settings. |
| Cable damaged | Replace damaged cables. |
| Excessive heat | Overload | Reduce power. |
| Heat at the terminals or connectors | Poor contact | Tighten the terminals / connectors with the specified tightening torque. See the appropriate user guide for the drive associated with the motor. In addition, verify the cable connection on the motor. |
