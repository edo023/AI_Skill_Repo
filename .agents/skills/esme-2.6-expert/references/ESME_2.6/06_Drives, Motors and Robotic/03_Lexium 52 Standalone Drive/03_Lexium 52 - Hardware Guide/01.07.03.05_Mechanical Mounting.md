# Mechanical Mounting

![](images/logo.png)

Mechanical Mounting

Procedure

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Screw pan-head screws M6 (socket head cap screws) into the prepared installation holes. |
| 2 | Keep a distance of 10 mm (0.39 in) between the screw head and the mounting surface. |
| 3 | Hook in device and verify the vertical mounting arrangement. |
| 4 | Tighten the mounting screws (torque: 4.6 Nm (41 lbf in)). |
