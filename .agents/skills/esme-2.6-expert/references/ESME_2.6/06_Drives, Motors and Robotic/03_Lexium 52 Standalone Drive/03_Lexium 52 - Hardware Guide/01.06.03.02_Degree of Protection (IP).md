# Degree of Protection (IP)

![](images/logo.png)

Degree of Protection (IP)

Overview

Install components such that a degree of protection corresponding to the actual operational environment is set up.

For more information on the degree of protection of the component, refer to [Ambient Conditions](../LMC100HW_Technical_Data/LMC100HW_Technical_Data-2.htm#XREF_D_SE_0051518_1).

The following ambient conditions may damage the components:

oOil

oMoisture

oElectromagnetic interference

oAmbient temperature

oMetal dust deposits
