# Ground Monitoring

## Description

When the power stage is enabled, the device monitors the motor phases for ground errors. A ground error occurs when one or more motor phases are short-circuited to the ground (earth) of the application.

A ground error of one or more motor phases is detected. A ground error of the DC bus or the braking resistor is not monitored.

If the ground error monitoring is deactivated, the drive may be rendered inoperable by a ground error.
