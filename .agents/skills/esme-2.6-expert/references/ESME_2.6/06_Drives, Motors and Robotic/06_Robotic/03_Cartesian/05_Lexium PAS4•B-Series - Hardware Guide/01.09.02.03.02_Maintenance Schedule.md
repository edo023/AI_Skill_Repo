# Maintenance Schedule

Maintenance Schedule

Perform the following maintenance tasks only once:

| Date | Action |
| --- | --- |
| Two months after the date of manufacturing (DOM) | o[Verify the axis by visual inspection for any damage or missing parts](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-9.htm#XREF_D_SE_0062341_1). Especially moving parts and parts at risk for collisions such as carriage, contact plate, toothed belt, toothed belt tensioner, ball bearing of the toothed belt pulley, cover strip, cover strip clamp or mounting elements.  o[Verify the tension of toothed belt and adjust it](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-5.htm#XREF_D_SE_0062299_1), if necessary. |

Perform the following maintenance tasks within the stated intervals:

| Interval | Action |
| --- | --- |
| Every 400 hours of operation  or  every month | o[Clean the axis](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-8.htm#XREF_D_SE_0088562_1), if necessary.  o[Lubricate the guide system](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-20.htm#XREF_D_SE_0057273_1), if necessary. |
| Every 1,000 hours of operation  or  every three months | For PAS4•BR / PAS4•HR:  o[Clean the axis](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-8.htm#XREF_D_SE_0088562_1).  o[Lubricate the roller guide](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-22.htm#XREF_D_SE_0061786_1) |
| Every 2,000 hours of operation  or  every six months | o[Clean the axis](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-8.htm#XREF_D_SE_0088562_1).  o[Verify the toothed belt abrasion](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-9.htm#XREF_D_SE_0062341_22) and [replace it](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-18.htm#XREF_D_SE_0061823_1), if necessary.  o[Verify the tension of toothed belt and adjust it](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-5.htm#XREF_D_SE_0062299_1), if necessary.  o[Verify the axis by visual inspection for any damage or missing parts](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-9.htm#XREF_D_SE_0062341_1). Especially moving parts and parts at risk for collisions such as carriage, contact plate, toothed belt, toothed belt tensioner, ball bearing of the toothed belt pulley, cover strip, cover strip clamp or mounting elements.  oVerify the sensors for functionality.  oVerify the guide system for unusual noises. If unusual noises occur, refer to [Troubleshooting](../ROBOTICS_Troubleshooting/ROBOTICS_Troubleshooting.htm#XREF_D_SE_0065635_1).  oVerify all parts for bolted connections.  oFor PAS4•BB / PAS4•HB: [lubricate the recirculating ball bearing guide](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-23.htm#XREF_D_SE_0061787_1)  oFor PAS4•BR / PAS4•HR: [lubricate the roller guide](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-22.htm#XREF_D_SE_0061786_1) |
| Annually  or  after an uncontrolled stop (stop category 0) | oVerify the brake function during operations. For further information, refer to the corresponding motor manual of the optionally pre-installed Schneider Electric motor. |
| Every 20,000 hours of operation | oReplace the optionally pre-installed Schneider Electric [motor](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-14.htm#XREF_D_SE_0062731_1). |
| Every 30,000 hours of operation | oReplace the optionally pre-installed Schneider Electric [gearbox](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-14.htm#XREF_D_SE_0062731_1). |
| Every four years after the date of manufacturing (DOM) | o[Replace the toothed belt as a precautionary measure](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-18.htm#XREF_D_SE_0061823_1).  oSuggestion: Replace the [toothed belt pulleys](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-19.htm#XREF_D_SE_0087889_1) when replacing the toothed belt. |

NOTE: The gearbox (optional equipment) and the ball bearings are lubricated for life.
