# Replacing the Double Coupling (only PAD42BB)

![](images/logo.png)

Replacing the Double Coupling (only PAD42BB)

Overview

A douple coupling is only mounted in the PAD42BB.

The following graphic presents the components of a double coupling:

![G-SE-0083150.1.gif-high.gif](images/G-SE-0083150.1.gif-high.gif)

1   Screw (1 piece)

2   Front cone (1 piece)

3   Shaft (1 piece)

4   Rear cone (1 piece)

Prerequisites

You need the following tools to remove the double coupling:

oSet of hex keys

oSoft-faced hammer

oScrew M8 (minimum length: 35 mm (1.38 in))

NOTE: Do not use ball head hex keys. Excessive torque may cause the ball head to break away.

For suitable parts, refer to [Replacement Equipment and Accessories](../ROBOTICS_Replacement_Equipment/ROBOTICS_Replacement_Equipment-1.htm#XREF_D_SE_0065517_1).

Preparing the Removal of the Double Coupling

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | If the axis is mounted tilted or vertically, remove the payload or support the payload and the carriage to keep it from falling. |
| 2 | Remove the following components depending on the axis configuration:  o[Shaft extension](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-16.htm#XREF_D_SE_0106424_1)  o[Motor and/or gearbox](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-14.htm#XREF_D_SE_0106422_1)  o[Coupling](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-15.htm#XREF_D_SE_0106423_1) |

Removing the Double Coupling

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Manually move the carriages into end position on the left-hand side. |
| 2 | Loosen the screw (1) at the double coupling (2).  NOTE: Usually the screw is located on the side where the motor and/or gearbox is mounted.  G-SE-0083036.1.gif-high.gif |
| 3 | Carefully tap on the screw head with a soft-faced hammer so that the rear cone (3) of the double coupling comes loose.  NOTE:  oIf you cannot reach the screw head, use a hex key as extension and tap carefully on the hex key.  oIf you want to replace the toothed belt only, you do not need to proceed with the next steps. |
| 4 | Remove the screw and the rear cone. |
| 5 | Manually move the carriages (4) into end position on the right-hand side.  G-SE-0083037.1.gif-high.gif |
| 6 | Insert and tighten the M8 screw (5) into the front cone of the double coupling until the front cone comes loosen. |
| 7 | Remove the screw with the front cone.  G-SE-0083038.1.gif-high.gif |
| 8 | Pull the shaft of the double coupling carefully out of the hollow shaft of the toothed belt pulleys.  G-SE-0083669.1.gif-high.gif |

Preparing the Mounting of the Double Coupling

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Clean all parts. |
| 2 | Inspect all parts for damage. |

NOTE: Polluted or damaged parts may cause run-out which has an adverse effect on the service life of the axis.

|  |
| --- |
| NOTICE |
| UNINTENDED EQUIPMENT OPERATION |
| oReplace any damaged parts immediately.  oClean all parts before assembly and use. |
| Failure to follow these instructions can result in equipment damage. |

Mounting the Double Coupling

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Manually move the carriages into end position on the right-hand side. |
| 2 | Fit the shaft of the double coupling (1) into the hollow shaft of the toothed belt pulleys.  G-SE-0083013.1.gif-high.gif |
| 3 | Align the double coupling so that it is centered between the end blocks.  G-SE-0083014.1.gif-high.gif |
| 4 | Fit the screw (3), the front cone (2) and the rear cone (4) into the shaft of the double coupling.  G-SE-0083015.1.gif-high.gif |
| 5 | Press against the rear cone and tighten the screw of the double coupling.  Tightening torque: 14 Nm (124 lbf-in)  G-SE-0083016.1.gif-high.gif      NOTE: The double coupling shall not rotate further during tightening. If it does, verify that the carriages are at the end position and the toothed belt is installed correctly. |
| 6 | If applicable, mount the [shaft extension](../ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment/ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment-8.htm#XREF_D_SE_0106253_1). |
| 7 | If applicable, mount the [motor and/or gearbox](../ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment/ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment-4.htm#XREF_D_SE_0106085_1). |
