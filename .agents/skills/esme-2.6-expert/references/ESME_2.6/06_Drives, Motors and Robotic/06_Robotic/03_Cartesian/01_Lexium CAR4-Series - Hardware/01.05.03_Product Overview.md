General Description of the

The  is a [cantilever axis](../glossary/glossary.htm#XREF_D_SE_0058496_4), which can operate forward and backward in one dimension. The axis with rod design is based on an [axis body](../glossary/glossary.htm#XREF_D_SE_0058496_27) and a guide with linear ball bearings.

Depending on the size of the particular axis, a  is driven by a gear rack or a toothed belt.

Characteristics of the

The axis provides the following features and options:

oHigh rigidity for short [strokes](../glossary/glossary.htm#XREF_D_SE_0058496_24)

oLow moving mass

oCompact installation space

oDifferent stroke lengths available

oLubricated for life

oEasy integration into machines and systems

oMotor and gearbox mounting via compact coupling systems

oFastening threads with counterbores for locating dowels at the end plates for reproducible mounting of the payload

oOptions

oAntistatic toothed belt

o[Increased corrosion resistance](../ROBOTICS_Technical_Data/ROBOTICS_Technical_Data-2.htm#XREF_D_SE_0067139_8)

o[Sensors](../glossary/glossary.htm#XREF_D_SE_0058496_37) as normally open contacts / normally closed contacts and NPN/PNP version

oDifferent motors and gearboxes
