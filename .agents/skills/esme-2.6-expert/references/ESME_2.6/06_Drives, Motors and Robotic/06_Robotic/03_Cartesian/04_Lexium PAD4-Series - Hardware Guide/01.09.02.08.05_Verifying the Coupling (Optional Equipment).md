# Verifying the Coupling (Optional Equipment)

Verifying the Coupling (Optional Equipment)

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Remove the motor and/or the gearbox as described in [Replacing the Motor and/or Gearbox](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-14.htm#XREF_D_SE_0106422_1). |
| 2 | Verify the coupling by a visual inspection for damage. |

NOTE: A worn-out or damaged coupling must be replaced. For detailed information, refer to [Replacing the Coupling](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-15.htm#XREF_D_SE_0106423_1).
