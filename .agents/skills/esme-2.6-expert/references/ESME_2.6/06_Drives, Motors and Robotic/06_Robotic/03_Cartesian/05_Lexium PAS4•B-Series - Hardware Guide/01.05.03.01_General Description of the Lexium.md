# General Description of the Lexium PAS4•B-Series

General Description of the Lexium PAS4•B-Series

The Lexium PAS4•B-Series is a [portal axis](../glossary/glossary.htm#XREF_D_SE_0058496_32) which can operate forward and backward in one dimension. The axis is based on an axis body and a toothed belt driven carriage. It can be equipped with up to three carriages for moving multiple or long loads.

Depending on the size of the particular axis, the Lexium PAS4•B-Series is equipped with a roller guide or with a recirculating ball bearing guide.
