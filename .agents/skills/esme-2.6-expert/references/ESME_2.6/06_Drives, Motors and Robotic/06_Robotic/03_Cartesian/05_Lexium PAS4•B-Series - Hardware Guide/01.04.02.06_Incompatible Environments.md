# Incompatible Environments

Incompatible Environments

The components must not be used in the following environments:

oHazardous (explosive) atmospheres

oMobile, movable, or floating systems

oLife support systems

oDomestic appliances

oUnderground

oHighly saline environments (refer to [Technical Data](../ROBOTICS_Technical_Data/ROBOTICS_Technical_Data-1.htm#XREF_D_SE_0061396_1) for materials used)

oEnvironments with increased radioactive radiation

oWash-down areas (food or pharmaceutical industry)

oHigh-hygienic areas

oOily surroundings (only if optional a cover strip is used)

oVacuum

This equipment has been designed to operate outside of any hazardous location. Only install this equipment in zones known to be free of a hazardous atmosphere.
