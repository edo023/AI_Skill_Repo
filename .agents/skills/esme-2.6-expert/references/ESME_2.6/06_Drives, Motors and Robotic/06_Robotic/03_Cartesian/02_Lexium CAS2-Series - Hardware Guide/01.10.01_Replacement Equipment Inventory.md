# Replacement Equipment Inventory

## Overview

Keeping a stock of important components helps ensure that equipment downtime is kept to a minimum. Only exchange devices with identical types to help ensure compatibility.

Indicate the following information on the replacement equipment order, which can be found on the [logistic type plate](CAS2_TypePlate.html):

| Parameter | Position on type plate | Example value |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Axis type | First line | CAS24BBM1000A2NR/ 2 3G 6 H7 6 |
| Identification number | Id.No. | 0073000100758 |
| Serial number | Ser.No. | 2006804032 |
