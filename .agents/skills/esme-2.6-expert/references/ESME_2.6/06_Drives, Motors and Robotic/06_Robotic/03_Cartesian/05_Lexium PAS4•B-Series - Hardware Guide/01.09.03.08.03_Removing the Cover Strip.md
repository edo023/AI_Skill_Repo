# Removing the Cover Strip

Removing the Cover Strip

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Loosen the two setscrews at the cover strip clamp (1).  G-SE-0054325.2.gif-high.gif |
| 2 | Remove the two screws (4) at the cover strip clamp.  G-SE-0054326.2.gif-high.gif |
| 3 | Remove the cover strip clamp and the clamping plate (2). |
| 4 | Remove the two screws (6) at the rubber buffer (5).  G-SE-0054186.1.gif-high.gif |
| 5 | Remove the rubber buffer. |
| 6 | Remove the two screws (9) by means of which the holding plate (10) and the strip deflector (8) are mounted to the carriage (7).  G-SE-0054187.1.gif-high.gif |
| 7 | Remove the holding plate (10) and the strip deflector. |
| 8 | Remove the cover strip (11). |
| 9 | Peel off the magnetic strips (12) from the axis body when they are damaged or worn out.  G-SE-0073734.1.gif-high.gif |
| 10 | Mount the new cover strip and, if necessary, the new strip deflectors and magnetic strips as described in [Mounting the Cover Strip](../ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment/ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment-9.htm#XREF_D_SE_0092415_1). |
