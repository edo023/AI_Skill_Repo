# Replacing the Sensors

Replacing the Sensors

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Disconnect the sensor. |
| 2 | Loosen the screw (1) at the slotted side of the sensor holder (2) until the sensor (3) can be pulled out from below.  G-SE-0072712.1.gif-high.gif |
| 3 | Mount the new sensor as described in [Mounting the Sensors](../ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment/ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment-7.htm#XREF_D_SE_0061740_1). |
| 4 | Connect the new sensor.  For further information, refer to [Technical Data of the Sensors](../ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment/ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment-6.htm#XREF_D_SE_0061778_1). |
