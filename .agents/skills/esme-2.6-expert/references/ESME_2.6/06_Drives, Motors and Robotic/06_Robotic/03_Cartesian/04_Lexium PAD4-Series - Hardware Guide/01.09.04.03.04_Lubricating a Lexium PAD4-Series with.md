# Lubricating a Lexium PAD4-Series with Recirculating Ball Bearing Guide

Lubricating a Lexium PAD4-Series with Recirculating Ball Bearing Guide

NOTE: Use the grease lubrication gun for the following steps.

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Press the nozzle at right angles against the grease nipple (1) and inject the specified amount of lubricant. Then repeat this step with the grease nipple (2). For the correct type and quantity of lubricant, refer to [Lubrication Amount](#XREF_D_SE_0061787_3).  NOTE:  oWhen applying the lubricant, you must not exceed a maximum flow rate. Therefore, the minimum injection time of 3 seconds per lubrication gun stroke must be adhered to.  oMove the carriage between the strokes to distribute the grease into the lubricant reservoirs evenly.
