# Replacing the Shaft Extension

Replacing the Shaft Extension

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Loosen the screw (1) at the shaft extension (2).  G-SE-0073735.1.gif-high.gif      NOTE: As long as the toothed belt is installed correctly and the carriage is at the end position, the toothed belt pulley does not rotate further. For detailed information about toothed belt installation, refer to [Mounting the Toothed Belt](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-18.htm#XREF_D_SE_0061823_12). |
| 2 | Carefully tap on the screw head with a soft-faced hammer so that the cone of the shaft extension comes loose.  NOTE:  If you cannot reach the screw head, use a hex key as extension and tap carefully on the hex key. |
| 3 | Pull the shaft extension carefully out of the hollow shaft of the toothed belt pulley (3).  G-SE-0073736.1.gif-high.gif      NOTE: This may require a considerable force. |
| 4 | [Mount the new shaft extension](../ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment/ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment-8.htm#XREF_D_SE_0061742_1). |
