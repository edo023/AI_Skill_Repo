# Overview

Overview

The use of sensors can provide some protection against hazards (for example, collision with mechanical stop caused by incorrect reference values).

Fastening threads for sensors mounting are located at:

oSensor holding plate (CAR40)

oAxis body adapter plate (CAR41)

oAxis body (CAR42 / CAR43 / CAR44)
