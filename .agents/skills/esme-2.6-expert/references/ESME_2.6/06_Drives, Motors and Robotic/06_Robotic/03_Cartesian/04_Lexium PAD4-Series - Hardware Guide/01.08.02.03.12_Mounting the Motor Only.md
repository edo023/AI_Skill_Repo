# Mounting the Motor Only

Mounting the Motor Only

If possible, position the axis such that the top of the carriages points upwards during installation.

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Remove the setscrew (1) at the drive unit adaptation.  G-SE-0083132.1.gif-high.gif |
| 2 | Verify that the orientation of the clamping screw (2) is the same as the orientation of the setscrew of the drive unit adaptation. |
| 3 | Fit the motor (5) into the clamping hub and to the drive unit adaptation and secure the motor from falling. Verify that there is no clearance between the motor and the drive unit adaptation such that the surfaces are flush.  G-SE-0082878.1.gif-high.gif      NOTE: Greasing every centering collar provides easier dismounting. Use mineral oil-based lubricants without additives, or use silicon-based lubricants. |
| 4 | Fasten the motor with the four screws (3) and washers (4) crosswise. Use the [standard tightening torque](../ROBOTICS_Transport_and_Comissioning/ROBOTICS_Transport_and_Comissioning-6.htm#XREF_D_SE_0088555_4). |
| 5 | Tighten the screw of the clamping hub through the hole of the drive unit adaptation.  Tightening torque: 14 Nm (124 lbf-in) |
| 6 | Insert the setscrew into the drive unit adaptation and tighten it.  Tightening torque: 4 Nm (35.4 lbf-in) |
