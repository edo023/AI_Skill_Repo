# Transport

Transport

The Lexium PAS4•B-Series must be handled with care. Shocks and impacts may damage the axis. Damage may lead to reduced running accuracy, reduced service life, or to inoperable equipment.

The axis is pre-assembled before transport.

The axis is transported in a wooden box or a cardboard box. The respective box dimensions vary according to the dimensions of the axis and the order quantity.

For detailed information about transport conditions, refer to [Ambient Conditions](../ROBOTICS_Technical_Data/ROBOTICS_Technical_Data-2.htm#XREF_D_SE_0056648_1).
