# Storage

Storage

The Lexium EAC1-Series can be stored inside the packaging or unpacked. In both cases, ensure that it is stored in a sheltered and dry place. Avoid humidity which can have corrosive effects on the actuator.

NOTE: When stored, the actuator needs to be on a level surface.

For detailed information about storage conditions, refer to [Ambient Conditions](../ROBOTICS_Technical_Data/ROBOTICS_Technical_Data-2.htm#XREF_D_SE_0056648_1).
