# Lubricating the Lexium PAD4-Series

![](images/logo.png)

Lubricating the Lexium PAD4-Series

Overview

The axis is lubricated with grease from a factory-prefilled external reservoir. The carriage features two lubrication nipples at each side for lubrication.

The maintenance intervals at normal [ambient conditions](../ROBOTICS_Technical_Data/ROBOTICS_Technical_Data-2.htm#XREF_D_SE_0056648_1) are indicated in the [maintenance plan](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-4.htm#XREF_D_SE_0106442_1).

Shorten the indicated intervals accordingly in severe conditions or at the following influencing factors:

oDust and dirt particles

oHigh operating temperatures

oHeavy loads

oHeavy vibration

oPermanent short-distance positioning

Prerequisites

You need the following tools to lubricate a Lexium PAD4-Series with recirculating ball bearing guide:

oLubrication gun

oLubricant

For suitable lubrication equipment, refer to [Replacement Equipment and Accessories](../ROBOTICS_Replacement_Equipment/ROBOTICS_Replacement_Equipment-1.htm#XREF_D_SE_0065517_1).
