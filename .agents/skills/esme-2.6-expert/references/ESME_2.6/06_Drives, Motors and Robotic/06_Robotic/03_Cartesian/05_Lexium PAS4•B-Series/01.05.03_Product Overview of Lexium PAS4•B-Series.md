# Product Overview of Lexium PAS4•B-Series

![](images/logo.png)

Product Overview of Lexium PAS4•B-Series

General Description of the Lexium PAS4•B-Series

The Lexium PAS4•B-Series is a [portal axis](../glossary/glossary.htm#XREF_D_SE_0058496_32) which can operate forward and backward in one dimension. The axis is based on an axis body and a toothed belt driven carriage. It can be equipped with up to three carriages for moving multiple or long loads.

Depending on the size of the particular axis, the Lexium PAS4•B-Series is equipped with a roller guide or with a recirculating ball bearing guide.

Characteristics of Lexium PAS4•B-Series

The axis provides the following features and options:

oDifferent [stroke](../glossary/glossary.htm#XREF_D_SE_0058496_24) lengths are available per mm.

oMounting threads with counterbores for locating dowels at the carriage for reproducible mounting of the payload.

oGrease nipples at the side of the carriage for external lubrication.

oEasy integration into systems and machines due to axis bodies with T-slots.

oSensors adjustable in T-slots.

oMotor mounting via compact coupling system.

oThe cover strip and the wiper in the carriage further help to keep pollution, chips, dust, and foreign objects away from the [linear guide](../glossary/glossary.htm#XREF_D_SE_0058496_11) inside the axis.

oMotor mounting possible at four sides, can be changed at any later point in time.

oOptions

oAntistatic toothed belt

o[Increased corrosion resistance](../ROBOTICS_Technical_Data/ROBOTICS_Technical_Data-2.htm#XREF_D_SE_0067139_8)

oCover strip and wiper in the carriage

oDistribution of the payload to up to three carriages

oCarriage available in various lengths

oSensors as normally open contacts / normally closed contacts and NPN/PNP version

oDifferent motors and gearboxes

Types of Mechanical Drive Elements

All sizes of the Lexium PAS4•B-Series are available with a toothed belt (Lexium PAS4•B-Series) or without a drive for use as [support axis](../glossary/glossary.htm#XREF_D_SE_0058496_26) (Lexium PAS4•H versions). For a detailed name description of the Lexium PAS4•B-Series, refer to [Type Code](ROBOTICS_System_Overview-4.htm#XREF_D_SE_0061606_1).
