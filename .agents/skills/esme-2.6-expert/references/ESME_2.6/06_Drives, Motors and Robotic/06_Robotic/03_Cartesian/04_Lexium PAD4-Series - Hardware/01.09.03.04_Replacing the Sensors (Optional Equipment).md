# Replacing the Sensors (Optional Equipment)

![](images/logo.png)

Replacing the Sensors (Optional Equipment)

Prerequisites

You need the following tools to remove the sensors:

oSet of hex keys

NOTE: Do not use ball head hex keys. Excessive torque may cause the ball head to break away.

For suitable parts, refer to [Replacement Equipment and Accessories](../ROBOTICS_Replacement_Equipment/ROBOTICS_Replacement_Equipment-1.htm#XREF_D_SE_0065517_1).

Replacing the Sensors

Perform the following steps for each sensor to be replaced:

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Disconnect the sensor (3).  G-SE-0083046.1.gif-high.gif |
| 2 | Loosen the screw (1) on the slotted side of the sensor holder (2) until the sensor can be pulled out from below. |
| 3 | Mount the new sensor as described in [Mounting the Sensors](../ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment/ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment-7.htm#XREF_D_SE_0106252_1). |
| 4 | Connect the new sensor.  For further information, refer to [Technical Data of the Sensors](../ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment/ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment-6.htm#XREF_D_SE_0061778_1). |
