# Maintenance Plan

![](images/logo.png)

Maintenance Plan

Overview

The maintenance intervals may have to be adapted to the greatly varying operational hours depending on the application.

For procedures to replace the different parts, refer to [Replacing Parts](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-10.htm#XREF_D_SE_0056722_1).

Maintenance Schedule

Perform the following maintenance tasks after delivery:

| Date | Action |
| --- | --- |
| After two months of service life | oVerify the axis by [visual inspection](../ROBOTICS_Transport_and_Comissioning/ROBOTICS_Transport_and_Comissioning-10.htm#XREF_D_SE_0088556_1) for any damage or missing parts, especially for moving parts and parts at risk for collisions such as end plate, toothed belt tensioner, ball bearing of the toothed belt pulley/rack pinion, linear ball bearing, sliding bearing, guide rods, gear rack, or mounting elements.  o[Verify the tension of toothed belt and adjust it](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-5.htm#XREF_D_SE_0062299_1) if necessary. |

Perform the following maintenance tasks within the stated intervals:

| Interval | Action |
| --- | --- |
| Every 400 hours of operation  or  every month | o[Clean the axis](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-8.htm#XREF_D_SE_0088562_1) if necessary.  oVerify the lubrication of the guide rods and, if necessary, lubricate the guide rods to avoid corrosion.  oVerify the lubrication of the gear rack and [lubricate the gear rack](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-20.htm#XREF_D_SE_0057273_1) if necessary (only CAR40). |
| Every 2,000 hours of operation  or  every six months | o[Clean the axis](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-8.htm#XREF_D_SE_0088562_1).  o[Verify the toothed belt abrasion](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-9.htm#XREF_D_SE_0062341_1) and [replace the toothed belt](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-17.htm#XREF_D_SE_0067294_1) if necessary.  o[Verify the tension of toothed belt and adjust it](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-5.htm#XREF_D_SE_0062299_1) if necessary.  o[Verify the axis by visual inspection for any damage or missing parts](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-9.htm#XREF_D_SE_0062341_1). Especially moving parts and parts at risk for collisions such as end plate, toothed belt tensioner, ball bearing of the toothed belt pulley/rack pinion, linear ball bearing, sliding bearing, guide rods, gear rack, or mounting elements.  oVerify the sensors for functionality.  oVerify all parts for bolted connections.  o[Lubricate the guide rods slightly to avoid corrosion](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-20.htm#XREF_D_SE_0057273_1).  o[Lubricate the gear rack (only CAR40)](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-20.htm#XREF_D_SE_0057273_1). |
| Annually  or  after an uncontrolled stop (stop category 0) | oVerify the brake function during operations. For further information, refer to the corresponding motor manual of the optionally pre-installed Schneider Electric motor. |
| Every 20,000 hours of operation | o[Replace the optionally pre-installed Schneider Electric motor](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-14.htm#XREF_D_SE_0067303_1). |
| Every 30,000 hours of operation | o[Replace the optionally pre-installed Schneider Electric gearbox](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-14.htm#XREF_D_SE_0067303_1). |
| Every four years after the date of manufacturing (DOM) | o[Replace the toothed belt as a precautionary measure](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-17.htm#XREF_D_SE_0067294_1).  oSuggestion: Replace the [toothed belt pulley](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-18.htm#XREF_D_SE_0067335_1) and [deflection pulleys](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-19.htm#XREF_D_SE_0071837_3) when replacing the toothed belt. |

NOTE: The gearbox (optional equipment), ball bearings, and linear ball bearings are lubricated for life.
