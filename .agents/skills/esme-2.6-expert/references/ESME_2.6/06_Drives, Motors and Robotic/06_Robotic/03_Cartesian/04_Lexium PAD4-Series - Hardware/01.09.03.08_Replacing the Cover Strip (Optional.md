# Replacing the Cover Strip (Optional Equipment)

![](images/logo.png)

Replacing the Cover Strip (Optional Equipment)

Overview

NOTE: If the cover strip is worn out, also replace the strip deflectors at the carriage.

Prerequisites

You need the following tools to remove the cover strip:

oSet of hex keys

NOTE: Do not use ball head hex keys. Excessive torque may cause the ball head to break away.

For suitable parts, refer to [Replacement Equipment and Accessories](../ROBOTICS_Replacement_Equipment/ROBOTICS_Replacement_Equipment-1.htm#XREF_D_SE_0065517_1).

Replacing the Cover Strip

To replace one cover strip perform the tasks described below at both ends of the carriage and at both end blocks.

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Loosen the two setscrews at the cover strip clamp (1).  G-SE-0083029.1.gif-high.gif |
| 2 | Remove the two screws (4) at the cover strip clamp (3).  G-SE-0083030.1.gif-high.gif |
| 3 | Remove the cover strip clamp and the clamping plate (2). |
| 4 | Remove the two screws (6) at the rubber buffer (5) and then remove the rubber buffer.  G-SE-0083031.1.gif-high.gif |
| 5 | Remove the two screws (10) by means of which the holding plate (9) and the strip deflector (8) are mounted to the carriage (7).  G-SE-0083032.1.gif-high.gif |
| 6 | Remove the holding plate and the strip deflector. |
| 7 | Remove the cover strip (11). |
| 8 | Verify the magnetic strips (12). If they are damaged or worn out, peel them off from the axis body.  G-SE-0083033.1.gif-high.gif |
| 9 | Mount the new cover strip, the new strip deflectors and, if applicable, the new magnetic strips as described in [Mounting the Cover Strip](../ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment/ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment-9.htm#XREF_D_SE_0106254_1).  NOTE: To make sure to have the appropriate cover strip length, place the old cover strip and the new cover strip next to each other and cut the new cover strip to the same length. |
