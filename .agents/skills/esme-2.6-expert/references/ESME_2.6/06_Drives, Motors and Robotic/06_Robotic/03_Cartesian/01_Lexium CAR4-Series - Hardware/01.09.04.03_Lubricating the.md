Lubricating the

Lubricating the Linear Ball Bearings

The linear ball bearings are lubricated for life.

Lubricating the Guide Rods

The guide rods must be lubricated slightly to avoid corrosion.

The maintenance intervals at normal [ambient conditions](../ROBOTICS_Technical_Data/ROBOTICS_Technical_Data-2.htm#XREF_D_SE_0056648_1) are indicated in [maintenance plan](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-4.htm#XREF_D_SE_0067382_1). In severe conditions, for example, high velocity or adverse ambient conditions, shorten the indicated intervals accordingly.

For lubricating the guide rods, only use Microlube GL 261 or alternative lubricants with label K1N-30 according to DIN 51825.

NOTE: Clean the guide rods before lubrication.

Lubricating the Gear Rack (CAR40)

The gear rack must be lubricated.

The maintenance intervals at normal [ambient conditions](../ROBOTICS_Technical_Data/ROBOTICS_Technical_Data-2.htm#XREF_D_SE_0056648_1) are indicated in the [maintenance plan](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-4.htm#XREF_D_SE_0067382_1). Shorten the indicated intervals accordingly in severe conditions or at the following influencing factors:

oDust and dirt particles

oHigh operating temperatures

oHeavy loads

oHeavy vibration

oPermanent short-distance positioning

For lubricating the gear rack, only use Microlube GL 261 or alternative lubricants with label K1N-30 according to DIN 51825.

NOTE: Clean the gear rack before lubrication.

Lubricating the Slide Bearings (CAR40)

The slide bearings are lubricated for life.
