# Fastening the Magnetic Strips

Fastening the Magnetic Strips

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Clean the mounting surfaces of the magnetic strips at the axis body (1) such that they are free of grease.  G-SE-0072714.1.gif-high.gif |
| 2 | Place the magnetic strips along the axis body and cut them with the scissors to the same length as the axis body. |
| 3 | Peel off the film of the magnetic strips (2) and fasten them with the glued side onto the mounting side of the axis body.  G-SE-0073733.1.gif-high.gif      NOTE: Allow the adhesive to cure for 24 hours. |
