# Incompatible Environments

Incompatible Environments

The components must not be used in the following environments:

oHazardous (explosive) atmospheres

oFloating systems

oLife support systems

oDomestic appliances

oUnderground

oHighly saline environments

oEnvironments with increased radioactive radiation

oHigh-hygienic areas

oOily surroundings

oVacuum

The actuator has been designed to operate outside of any hazardous location. Only install the actuator in zones known to be free of a hazardous atmosphere.
