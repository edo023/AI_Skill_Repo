Troubleshooting

Overview

| Malfunction | Probable cause | Solution |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Sensor overtravelled. | Detected sensor error. | Adjust or replace the sensors. For more information, refer to [Replacing the Sensors](../ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair/ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-13.htm#XREF_D_SE_0086232_1). |
| Detected controller error. | Verify the controller. |
| Motor load increases, controller switches off because of overload. | Linear guides or toothed belt pulleys under mechanical tension or excessive friction caused by poor lubrication. | Contact your local Schneider Electric service representative. |
| Noise and vibrations. | Velocity too high. | Reduce velocity. |
| Poor lubrication (in the case of noise). | Lubricate, for more information, refer to [Lubrication](../ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair/ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-20.htm#XREF_D_SE_0057273_1). |
| Carriage has backlash. | Contact your local Schneider Electric service representative. |
| Ambient conditions. | Verify [ambient conditions](../ROBOTICS_Technical_Data/ROBOTICS_Technical_Data-2.htm#XREF_D_SE_0056648_1). |
| Running inaccuracy and noise of the guides. | Poor lubrication. | Lubricate, for more information, refer to [Lubrication](../ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair/ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-20.htm#XREF_D_SE_0057273_1). |
| Damage to components of the linear guide, for example by shock or impact on the carriage. | The components of the linear guide must be replaced, contact your local Schneider Electric service representative. |
| The carriage has backlash and positions inaccurately. | Backlash in guides after a collision. | Contact your local Schneider Electric service representative. |
| Worn out or damaged toothed belt or elastomer coupling after a collision. | [Replace the toothed belt](../ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair/ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-18.htm#XREF_D_SE_0061823_1) and/or [replace the elastomer coupling](../ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair/ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-15.htm#XREF_D_SE_0087887_1). |
