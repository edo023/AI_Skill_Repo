# Verifying the Linear Guide

Verifying the Linear Guide

The linear guide consists of the carriage and the recirculating ball bearing guide.

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Inspect the carriage for backlash. If the carriage has backlash, the preload has been modified or the linear guide is worn out. For further information, contact your local Schneider Electric service representative. |
| 2 | [Remove the toothed belt](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-18.htm#XREF_D_SE_0106425_10). |
| 3 | Verify the recirculating ball bearing guide by a visual inspection for damage. |
| 4 | Manually move the carriages (without toothed belt) and inspect for irregular noise or vibration. Irregular noise or vibration indicates a deformation in the linear guide. Deformation causes rapid wear. |

NOTE: Worn-out or damaged parts of the linear guide must be replaced. Contact your local Schneider Electric service representative.
