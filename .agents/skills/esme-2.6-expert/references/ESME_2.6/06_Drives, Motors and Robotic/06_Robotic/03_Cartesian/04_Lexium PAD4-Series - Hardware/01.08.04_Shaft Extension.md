# Shaft Extension

![](images/logo.png)

Shaft Extension

Mounting the Shaft Extension

Overview

A shaft extension can be mounted to either side of the two end blocks and used for example to couple an external encoder.

Prerequisites

You need the following tools to mount a shaft extension:

oSoft-faced hammer

oTorque wrench with a set of hexagon sockets

For suitable parts, refer to [Replacement Equipment and Accessories](../ROBOTICS_Replacement_Equipment/ROBOTICS_Replacement_Equipment-1.htm#XREF_D_SE_0065517_1).

Mounting the Shaft Extension

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Manually move the carriages (1) into end position on the right-hand side. |
| 2 | Fit the shaft extension (3) into the hollow shaft of the toothed belt pulley (2). Verify that there is no clearance between the shaft extension and the toothed belt pulley such that the surfaces are flush.  G-SE-0082885.1.gif-high.gif      NOTE: To completely fit the shaft extension, you may have to tap the shaft extension at the last 2 mm (0.079 in) slightly with a soft-faced hammer (not on the screw head). |
| 3 | Tighten the screw (4) of the shaft extension.  Tightening torque: 10 Nm (89 lbf-in)  NOTE: The shaft extension shall not rotate further during tightening. If it does, verify that the carriages are at the end position and the toothed belt is installed correctly. |
