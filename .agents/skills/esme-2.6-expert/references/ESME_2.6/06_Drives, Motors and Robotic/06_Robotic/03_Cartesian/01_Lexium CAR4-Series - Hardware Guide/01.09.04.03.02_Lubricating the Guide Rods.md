# Lubricating the Guide Rods

Lubricating the Guide Rods

The guide rods must be lubricated slightly to avoid corrosion.

The maintenance intervals at normal [ambient conditions](../ROBOTICS_Technical_Data/ROBOTICS_Technical_Data-2.htm#XREF_D_SE_0056648_1) are indicated in [maintenance plan](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-4.htm#XREF_D_SE_0067382_1). In severe conditions, for example, high velocity or adverse ambient conditions, shorten the indicated intervals accordingly.

For lubricating the guide rods, only use Microlube GL 261 or alternative lubricants with label K1N-30 according to DIN 51825.

NOTE: Clean the guide rods before lubrication.
