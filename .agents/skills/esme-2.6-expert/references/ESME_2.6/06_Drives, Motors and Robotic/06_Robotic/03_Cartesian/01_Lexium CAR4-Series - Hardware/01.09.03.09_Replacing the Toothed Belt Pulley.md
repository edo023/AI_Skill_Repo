# Replacing the Toothed Belt Pulley

![](images/logo.png)

Replacing the Toothed Belt Pulley

Overview

Replacing the toothed belt pulley can cause unintended movements.

|  |
| --- |
| Warning_Color.gifWARNING |
| UNINTENDED MOVEMENTS DUE TO DISMOUNTING |
| Secure the moving parts of the axis mounted in a vertical or tilted position against unintended movements. |
| Failure to follow these instructions can result in death, serious injury, or equipment damage. |

Prerequisites

You need the following tools to replace the toothed belt pulley:

oSet of hex keys

NOTE: Do not use ball head hex keys. Excessive torque may cause, the ball head to tear off. A torn off ball head is difficult to remove from the screw.

For suitable parts, refer to [Replacement Equipment and Accessories](../ROBOTICS_Replacement_Equipment/ROBOTICS_Replacement_Equipment-3.htm#XREF_D_SE_0076671_1).

Preparing the Replacement of the Toothed Belt Pulley

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Remove the following components depending on the axis configuration:  o[Shaft extension](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-16.htm#XREF_D_SE_0067305_1)  o[Motor and/or gearbox](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-14.htm#XREF_D_SE_0067303_1)  o[Elastomer coupling](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-15.htm#XREF_D_SE_0067304_1) |
| 2 | [Remove the toothed belt](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-17.htm#XREF_D_SE_0067294_10). |

Removing the Toothed Belt Pulley

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Remove the four outer screws (1) at the axis body adapter plate (2).  G-SE-0065050.1.gif-high.gif |
| 2 | Remove the axis body adapter plate complete with the toothed belt pulley (4) and the two deflection pulleys (3) as a unit.  NOTE: If you want to replace the toothed belt only, omit the following steps. |
| 3 | Secure the deflection pulleys and the toothed belt pulley to keep it from falling and remove the two screws (5) in the middle at the axis body adapter plate.  G-SE-0065051.1.gif-high.gif |
| 4 | Remove the deflection pulleys. |
| 5 | Remove the toothed belt pulley. |

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Remove the four outer screws (2) and the two screws in the middle (3) from the axis body adapter plate (1).  G-SE-0061815.2.gif-high.gif |
| 2 | Remove the axis body adapter plate. |
| 3 | Remove the toothed belt pulley with bearings (5) carefully from the axis body (4).  G-SE-0061829.2.gif-high.gif |

Preparing the Mounting of the Toothed Belt Pulley

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Clean all parts. |
| 2 | Inspect all parts for damage. |

NOTE: Polluted or damaged parts may cause run-out which has an adverse effect on the service life of the axis.

|  |
| --- |
| NOTICE |
| UNINTENDED EQUIPMENT OPERATION |
| oReplace any damaged parts immediately.  oClean all parts before assembly and use. |
| Failure to follow these instructions can result in equipment damage. |

Mounting the Toothed Belt Pulley

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Clean both bearing seats in the axis body and, if necessary, clean the axis body. |
| 2 | Insert the new toothed belt pulley (4) carefully into the axis body adapter plate (2).  G-SE-0065250.1.gif-high.gif |
| 3 | Fasten the deflection pulleys (3) to the axis body adapter plate with the two screws (1) in the middle. Use a low tightening torque to allow the pulleys to move easily. |
| 4 | [Mount the toothed belt and the axis body adapter plate](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-17.htm#XREF_D_SE_0067294_18). |
| 5 | Fasten the two deflection pulleys to the axis body adapter plate with the two screws in the middle. Use the [standard tightening torque](../ROBOTICS_Transport_and_Comissioning/ROBOTICS_Transport_and_Comissioning-6.htm#XREF_D_SE_0088555_4). |
| 6 | Mount the [toothed belt tensioners](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-17.htm#XREF_D_SE_0067294_12). |
| 7 | If applicable, mount the [shaft extension](../ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment/ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment-8.htm#XREF_D_SE_0067187_1). |

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Clean both bearing seats in the axis body and, if necessary, clean the axis body. |
| 2 | [Mount the toothed belt, the new toothed belt pulley and the axis body adapter plate](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-17.htm#XREF_D_SE_0067294_18). |
| 3 | If applicable, mount the [shaft extension](../ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment/ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment-8.htm#XREF_D_SE_0067187_1). |
| 4 | Mount the [toothed belt tensioners](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-17.htm#XREF_D_SE_0067294_12). |
| 5 | [If applicable, mount the motor and/or gearbox](../ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment/ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment-4.htm#XREF_D_SE_0067152_1). |
