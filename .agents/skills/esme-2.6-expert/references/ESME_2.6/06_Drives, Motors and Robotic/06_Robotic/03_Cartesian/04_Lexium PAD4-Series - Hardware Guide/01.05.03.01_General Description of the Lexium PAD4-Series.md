# General Description of the Lexium PAD4-Series

General Description of the Lexium PAD4-Series

The Lexium PAD4-Series is a [portal axis](../glossary/glossary.htm#XREF_D_SE_0058496_32) which can operate forward and backward in one dimension. The axis is based on an axis body with two inner recirculating ball bearing guides and toothed belt driven carriages. Depending on the mechanical drive element, there are three different designs available:

oThe axis type PAD42BB is equipped with two coupled toothed belts that drive the carriages only by one motor or one motor and gearbox.

oThe axis type PAD42EB is equipped with two uncoupled toothed belts that drive the carriages by two motors or two motors and gearboxes independently.

oThe axis type PAD42PB is equipped with one toothed belt that drives the carriages by one motor or motor and gearbox. The side of the axis without drive is used for support.

NOTE: The carriages of the PAD42BB and PAD42PB must be connected before commissioning.

The axis can be equipped with up to three carriage couples for moving multiple or long loads.

For a detailed overview of the configuration possibilities, refer to [Type Code](ROBOTICS_System_Overview-4.htm#XREF_D_SE_0104489_1).
