# Transport Conditions

Transport Conditions

The Lexium EAC1-Series must be handled with care. Shocks and impacts may damage the actuator. Damage may lead to reduced running accuracy, reduced service life, or to an inoperable actuator.

The actuator is pre-assembled before transport.

The actuator is transported in a wooden box or a cardboard box. The respective box dimensions vary according to the dimensions of the actuator and the order quantity.

Take into account that the piston rod may be damaged during transport if it is extended.

|  |
| --- |
| NOTICE |
| INOPERABLE EQUIPMENT |
| The piston rod must be retracted before transport. |
| Failure to follow these instructions can result in equipment damage. |
