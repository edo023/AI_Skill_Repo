# Emissions

Emissions

Some small amount of grease emissions are to be expected over time. However, excessive grease emissions on or at the gearbox may be an indication of a damaged axis.

|  |
| --- |
| NOTICE |
| INOPERABLE EQUIPMENT INDICATED BY GEARBOX GREASE EMISSIONS |
| oVerify the mechanics before, during, and after use.  oShut down the mechanics immediately if grease emissions appear on the axis mechanics. |
| Failure to follow these instructions can result in equipment damage. |
