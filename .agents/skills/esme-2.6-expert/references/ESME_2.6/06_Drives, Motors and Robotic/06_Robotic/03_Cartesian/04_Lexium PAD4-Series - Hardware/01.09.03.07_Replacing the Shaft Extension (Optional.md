# Replacing the Shaft Extension (Optional Equipment)

![](images/logo.png)

Replacing the Shaft Extension (Optional Equipment)

Prerequisites

You need the following tools to remove the shaft extension:

oSet of hex keys

oSoft-faced hammer

NOTE: Do not use ball head hex keys. Excessive torque may cause the ball head to break away.

For suitable parts, refer to [Replacement Equipment and Accessories](../ROBOTICS_Replacement_Equipment/ROBOTICS_Replacement_Equipment-1.htm#XREF_D_SE_0065517_1).

Replacing the Shaft Extension

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Manually move the carriages into end position on the left-hand side. |
| 2 | Loosen the screw at the shaft extension (1).  G-SE-0083047.1.gif-high.gif      NOTE: The shaft extension shall not rotate during tightening. If it does, verify whether the carriages are at the end position and the toothed belt is installed correctly. |
| 3 | Carefully tap on the screw head with a soft-faced hammer so that the cone of the shaft extension comes loose.  NOTE:  If you cannot reach the screw head, use a hex key as extension and tap carefully on the hex key. |
| 4 | Pull the shaft extension carefully out of the hollow shaft of the toothed belt pulley (2).  G-SE-0083048.1.gif-high.gif      NOTE: This may require a considerable force. |
| 5 | [Mount the new shaft extension](../ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment/ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment-8.htm#XREF_D_SE_0106253_1). |
