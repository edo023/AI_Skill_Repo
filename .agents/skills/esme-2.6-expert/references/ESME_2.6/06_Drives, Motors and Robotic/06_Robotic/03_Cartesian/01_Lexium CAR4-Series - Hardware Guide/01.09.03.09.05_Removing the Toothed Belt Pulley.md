# Removing the Toothed Belt Pulley

Removing the Toothed Belt Pulley

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Remove the four outer screws (1) at the axis body adapter plate (2).  G-SE-0065050.1.gif-high.gif |
| 2 | Remove the axis body adapter plate complete with the toothed belt pulley (4) and the two deflection pulleys (3) as a unit.  NOTE: If you want to replace the toothed belt only, omit the following steps. |
| 3 | Secure the deflection pulleys and the toothed belt pulley to keep it from falling and remove the two screws (5) in the middle at the axis body adapter plate.  G-SE-0065051.1.gif-high.gif |
| 4 | Remove the deflection pulleys. |
| 5 | Remove the toothed belt pulley. |

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Remove the four outer screws (2) and the two screws in the middle (3) from the axis body adapter plate (1).  G-SE-0061815.2.gif-high.gif |
| 2 | Remove the axis body adapter plate. |
| 3 | Remove the toothed belt pulley with bearings (5) carefully from the axis body (4).  G-SE-0061829.2.gif-high.gif |
