# Lubricating the Ball Screw

Lubricating the Ball Screw

The ball screw must be lubricated.

The maintenance intervals at normal [ambient conditions](../ROBOTICS_Technical_Data/ROBOTICS_Technical_Data-2.htm#XREF_D_SE_0056648_1) are indicated in the [maintenance plan](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-4.htm#XREF_D_SE_0081337_1). For the travel path at normal ambient conditions, refer to [Unimotion Maintenance- and Assembly Instructions PNCE Electric Cylinder](../front/front-4.htm#XREF_D_SE_0081290_18).

For lubricating the ball screw, only use Lubcon TURMOGREASE LC 802 EP or alternative lubricants with label K HC P 2/3 N-30 according to DIN 51825.
