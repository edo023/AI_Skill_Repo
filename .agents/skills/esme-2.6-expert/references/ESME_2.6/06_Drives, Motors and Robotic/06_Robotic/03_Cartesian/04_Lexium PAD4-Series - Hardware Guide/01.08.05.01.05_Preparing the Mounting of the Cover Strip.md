# Preparing the Mounting of the Cover Strip

Preparing the Mounting of the Cover Strip

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Clean all parts. |
| 2 | Inspect all parts for damage. |
| 3 | Remove the motor or the gearbox as described under [Replacing the Motor or Gearbox](../ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair/ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-14.htm#XREF_D_SE_0106422_1) so that you can easily move the carriage. |

NOTE: Polluted or damaged parts may cause run-out which has an adverse effect on the service life of the axis.
