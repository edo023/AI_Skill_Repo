# Replacing the Toothed Belt Pulley

![](images/logo.png)

Replacing the Toothed Belt Pulley

Prerequisites

You need the following tools to remove the toothed belt pulley:

oSet of hex keys

NOTE: Do not use ball head hex keys. Excessive torque may cause the ball head to break away. A broken ball head makes the removal of the screw difficult.

For suitable parts, refer to [Replacement Equipment and Accessories](../ROBOTICS_Replacement_Equipment/ROBOTICS_Replacement_Equipment-1.htm#XREF_D_SE_0065517_1).

Procedure Overview

Perform the following procedures to replace the toothed belt pulley:

o[Preparing the replacement of the toothed belt pulley](#XREF_D_SE_0087889_8)

o[Removing the toothed belt pulley](#XREF_D_SE_0087889_4)

o[Mounting the toothed belt pulley](#XREF_D_SE_0087889_5)

Preparing the Replacement of the Toothed Belt Pulley

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Remove the following components depending on the axis configuration:  o[Shaft extension](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-16.htm#XREF_D_SE_0087888_1)  o[Motor and/or gearbox](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-14.htm#XREF_D_SE_0062731_1)  o[Elastomer coupling](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-15.htm#XREF_D_SE_0087887_1) |
| 2 | If installed, remove the [cover strip and the strip deflectors](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-17.htm#XREF_D_SE_0061824_1). |
| 3 | Remove the [toothed belt](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-18.htm#XREF_D_SE_0061823_1). |

Removing the Toothed Belt Pulley

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Remove the circlip (1) at one side of the end block.  G-SE-0054340.1.gif-high.gif |
| 2 | Remove the toothed belt pulley with bearing (2) as a unit.  G-SE-0054341.1.gif-high.gif |

Mounting the Toothed Belt Pulley

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Clean both bearing seats in the end block (3). |
| 2 | Insert the new toothed belt pulley with the bearing (2).  G-SE-0054342.1.gif-high.gif |
| 3 | Mount the circlip (1). |
| 4 | Mount the [toothed belt](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-18.htm#XREF_D_SE_0061823_1). |
| 5 | If applicable, mount the [cover strip](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-17.htm#XREF_D_SE_0061824_1). |
| 6 | If applicable, mount the [shaft extension](../ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment/ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment-8.htm#XREF_D_SE_0061742_1). |
| 7 | If applicable, mount the [motor and/or gearbox](../ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment/ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment-4.htm#XREF_D_SE_0061741_1). |
