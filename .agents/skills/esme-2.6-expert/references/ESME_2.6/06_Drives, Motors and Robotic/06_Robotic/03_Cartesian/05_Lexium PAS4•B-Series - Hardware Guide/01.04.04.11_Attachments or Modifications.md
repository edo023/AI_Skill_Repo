# Attachments or Modifications

Attachments or Modifications

If different products are transported by the axis mechanics, then the product pickup must be consequently modified. For this reason, it is possible to build different product pickups (tool mounting) onto the carriage. In doing so, ensure that the articulation movement is not restricted and/or that no motion errors can result from the modifications. Attachments and rebuilds may not influence the operation of the protective devices in any way and all EMERGENCY STOP buttons must be accessible and operational all the time.
