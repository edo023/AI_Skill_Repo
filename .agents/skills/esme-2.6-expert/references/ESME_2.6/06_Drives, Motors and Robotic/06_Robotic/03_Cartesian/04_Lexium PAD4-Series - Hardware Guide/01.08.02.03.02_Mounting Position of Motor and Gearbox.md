# Mounting Position of Motor and Gearbox

Mounting Position of Motor and Gearbox

The correct mounting of the motor and gearbox is depending on the axis type:

oFor PAD42BB: mount the motor and/or gearbox to one of the accessible sides of the end blocks where the double coupling is installed. If needed, the double coupling can also be mounted to the other end of the axis. For further information, refer to [Replacing the Double Coupling](../ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair/ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-20.htm#XREF_D_SE_0106084_1)

oFor PAD42EB: you can mount motors and/or gearboxes to either accessible side of the end blocks.

oFor PAD42PB: you can mount the motor and/or gearbox to either side of the end block where the distance plate is installed.

The motor and the gearbox can be mounted in different arrangements (turned in increments of 4 x 90°). For further information, refer to [Motor and/or Gearbox Orientation and Configuration](../ROBOTICS_System_Overview/ROBOTICS_System_Overview-4.htm#XREF_D_SE_0104489_4).

NOTE: The maximum mass of the installed parts is limited by the torque at the end block.
