# Mounting the Shaft Extension

Mounting the Shaft Extension

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Fit the shaft extension (1) into the hollow shaft of the toothed belt pulley (2). Verify that there is no clearance between the shaft extension and the toothed belt pulley such that the surfaces are flush.  G-SE-0054322.2.gif-high.gif      NOTE: To completely fit the shaft extension, you may have to tap the shaft extension at the last 2 mm (0.079 in) slightly with a soft-faced hammer (not on the screw head). |
| 2 | Tighten the screw (3) of the shaft extension.
