# Replacing the Toothed Belt Pulley

![](images/logo.png)

Replacing the Toothed Belt Pulley

Overview

For the PAD42BB you need to remove the double coupling before replacing the toothed belt pulley. For further information, refer to [Replacing the Double Coupling](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-20.htm#XREF_D_SE_0106084_1).

Prerequisites

You need the following tools to remove the toothed belt pulley:

oPliers for circlip

NOTE: Do not use ball head hex keys. Excessive torque may cause the ball head to break away.

For suitable parts, refer to [Replacement Equipment and Accessories](../ROBOTICS_Replacement_Equipment/ROBOTICS_Replacement_Equipment-1.htm#XREF_D_SE_0065517_1).

Procedure Overview

Perform the following procedures to replace the toothed belt pulley:

o[Preparing the removal of the toothed belt pulley](#XREF_D_SE_0106426_8)

o[Removing the toothed belt pulley](#XREF_D_SE_0106426_4)

o[Preparing the mounting of the toothed belt pulley](#XREF_D_SE_0106426_10)

o[Mounting the toothed belt pulley](#XREF_D_SE_0106426_5)

Preparing the Removal of the Toothed Belt Pulley

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Remove the following components depending on the axis configuration:  o[Motor and/or gearbox](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-14.htm#XREF_D_SE_0106422_1)  o[Coupling](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-15.htm#XREF_D_SE_0106423_1)  o[Double coupling (only PAD42BB)](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-20.htm#XREF_D_SE_0106084_1)  o[Cover strip](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-17.htm#XREF_D_SE_0106097_1)  o[Shaft extension](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-16.htm#XREF_D_SE_0106424_1) |
| 2 | Remove the [toothed belt](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-18.htm#XREF_D_SE_0106425_10). |

Removing the Toothed Belt Pulley

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Remove the circlip (1).  G-SE-0083057.1.gif-high.gif |
| 2 | Remove the toothed belt pulley with bearing (2) as a unit.  G-SE-0083058.1.gif-high.gif |

Preparing the Mounting of the Toothed Belt Pulley

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Clean all parts. |
| 2 | Inspect all parts for damage. |

NOTE: Polluted or damaged parts may cause run-out which has an adverse effect on the service life of the axis.

|  |
| --- |
| NOTICE |
| UNINTENDED EQUIPMENT OPERATION |
| oReplace any damaged parts immediately.  oClean all parts before assembly and use. |
| Failure to follow these instructions can result in equipment damage. |

Mounting the Toothed Belt Pulley

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Clean both bearing seats in the end block (3). |
| 2 | Insert the new toothed belt pulley with bearing (1) as a unit.  G-SE-0083059.1.gif-high.gif |
| 3 | Mount the circlip (2). |
| 4 | Mount the [toothed belt](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-18.htm#XREF_D_SE_0106425_12). |
| 5 | For PAD42BB:  [Mount the double coupling](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-20.htm#XREF_D_SE_0106084_5). |
| 6 | If applicable, mount the [cover strip](../ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment/ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment-9.htm#XREF_D_SE_0106254_1). |
| 7 | If applicable, mount the [shaft extension](../ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment/ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment-8.htm#XREF_D_SE_0106253_1). |
| 8 | If applicable, mount the [motor and/or gearbox](../ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment/ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment-4.htm#XREF_D_SE_0106085_1). |
