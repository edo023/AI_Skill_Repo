# Cabling the Actuator

Cabling the Actuator

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Connect the motor.  For details about connecting the motor, refer to the corresponding motor manual. |
| 2 | If applicable, connect the sensors.  For details about sensor connections, refer to [Technical Data of the Sensors](../ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment/ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment-6.htm#XREF_D_SE_0081323_1). |
| 3 | Verify the correct routing and fastening of the cables to help prevent any collision of cables and moving parts. |

|  |
| --- |
