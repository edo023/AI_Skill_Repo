# Transport and Storage

## Transport

The Lexium CAS2-Series must be handled with care. Shocks and impacts may damage the axis. Damage may lead to reduced running accuracy, reduced service life, or to an inoperable axis.

The axis is assembled and tested for function before transport.

The axis is transported in a wooden box or a cardboard box. The respective box dimensions vary according to the dimensions of the axis and the order quantity.

For detailed information about transport conditions, refer to [*Ambient Conditions*](AmbientConditions-623F1CD9.html).

## Storage

The Lexium CAS2-Series can be stored inside the packaging or unpacked. In both cases, ensure that it is stored in a sheltered and dry place. Avoid humidity which can have corrosive effects to the axis.

NOTE: When stored, the axis needs to be on a level surface.

For detailed information about storage conditions, refer to [*Ambient Conditions*](AmbientConditions-623F1CD9.html).
