# Lubricating a Lexium PAS4•B-Series with Recirculating Ball Bearing Guide

Lubricating a Lexium PAS4•B-Series with Recirculating Ball Bearing Guide

NOTE: Use the grease lubrication gun for the following steps.

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Press the nozzle in a right angle against one of the lubrication nipples (1) and inject the specified amount of lubricant. For the correct type and quantity of lubricant, refer to [Lubrication Amount](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-22.htm#XREF_D_SE_0061786_3).  NOTE:  oWhen applying the lubricant, you must not exceed a maximum flow rate. Therefore, the minimum injection time of 3 seconds per lubrication gun stroke must be adhered to.  oMove the carriage between the strokes to distribute the grease into the lubricant reservoirs evenly.  G-SE-0054314.1.gif-high.gif |
| 2 | Repeat step 1 alternating for the first and the second lubrication nipple at one side of the carriage until you reach the specified number of strokes. |
