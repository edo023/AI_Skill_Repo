# Emergency Stop

Emergency Stop

The axis mechanics are not supplied with external brakes nor an emergency stop switch to engage any external brakes. However, the optional motor can be supplied with an internal holding brake (depending on the motor reference).

For more information about the motor, note the motor reference on the type plate and refer to the corresponding motor manual.

| ENTRAPMENT BY AXIS MECHANICS |
