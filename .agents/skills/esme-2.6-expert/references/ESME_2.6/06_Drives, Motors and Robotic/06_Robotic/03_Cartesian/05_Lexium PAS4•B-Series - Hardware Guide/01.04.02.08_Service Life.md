# Service Life

Service Life

The forces and torques (Fy, Fz, Mx, Mz, My) are calculated for an expected [service life](../glossary/glossary.htm#XREF_D_SE_0058496_22) of 30,000 km (18,641 mi). This period

oshortens with increasing load and dynamic or

oextends with decreasing load and dynamic.

For an application-specific calculation of the service life of the axis, refer to [Service Life](../ROBOTICS_Technical_Data/ROBOTICS_Technical_Data-9.htm#XREF_D_SE_0067147_1).
