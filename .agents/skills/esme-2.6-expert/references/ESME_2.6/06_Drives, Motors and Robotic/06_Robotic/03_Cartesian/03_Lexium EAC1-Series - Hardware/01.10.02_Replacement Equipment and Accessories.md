# Replacement Equipment and Accessories Inventory

![](images/logo.png)

Replacement Equipment and Accessories Inventory

Overview

Keeping a stock of important components helps ensure the availability of your machine. Only replace the equipment and the accessories of the actuator with identical types to help ensure compatibility.

Indicate the following information on the replacement equipment order, which can be found on the [logistic type plate](../ROBOTICS_System_Overview/ROBOTICS_System_Overview-5.htm#XREF_D_SE_0081310_1):

| Parameter | Example value | Position on type plate |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Actuator type | EAC12SF0250NFXS / 2 D O H7 0 | First line |
| Identification number | 0073000016016 | Id.No. |
| Serial number | 2700410441 | Ser.No. |
