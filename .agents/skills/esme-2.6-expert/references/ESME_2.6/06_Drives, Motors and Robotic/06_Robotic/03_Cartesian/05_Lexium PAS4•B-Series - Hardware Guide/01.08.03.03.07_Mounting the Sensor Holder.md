# Mounting the Sensor Holder

Mounting the Sensor Holder

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Slide each nut (3) into the T-slot at the cutout.  G-SE-0054312.1.gif-high.gif      NOTE: The slot of the sensor holder should point to the carriage. |
| 2 | Position the sensor holder (1) with the two screws at the nuts. Leave the two screws loose. |
| 3 | Slide the senor holder to the sensor position and tighten screw (2). For further information, refer to the dimensional drawing of the respective axis in [Mechanical Data](../ROBOTICS_Technical_Data/ROBOTICS_Technical_Data-3.htm#XREF_D_SE_0088553_1).  Tightening torque: 0.3 Nm (2.66 lbf-in). |
