# Maintenance Plan

![](images/logo.png)

Maintenance Plan

Overview

The maintenance intervals may have to be adapted to the greatly varying service live depending on the application.

For procedures to replace the different parts, refer to [Replacing Parts](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-10.htm#XREF_D_SE_0056722_1).

Maintenance Schedule

Perform the following maintenance tasks after delivery:

| Date | Action |
| --- | --- |
| After two months of operation | oVerify the axis by visual inspection for any damage or missing parts. Especially moving parts and parts at risk for collisions such as carriage, contact plate, toothed belt, toothed belt tensioner, ball bearing of the toothed belt pulley, cover strip, cover strip clamp, rubber buffer, sensor, or mounting elements.  o[Verify the tension of toothed belt and adjust it](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-5.htm#XREF_D_SE_0062299_1), if necessary. |

Perform the following maintenance tasks within the stated intervals:

| Interval | Action |
| --- | --- |
| Every 400 hours of operation  or  every month | o[Clean the axis](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-8.htm#XREF_D_SE_0088562_1), if necessary.  o[Lubricate the guide system](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-21.htm#XREF_D_SE_0057273_1), if necessary. |
| Every 2,000 hours of operation  or  every six months | o[Clean the axis](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-8.htm#XREF_D_SE_0088562_1).  oVerify the toothed belt abrasion and replace the toothed belt, if necessary.  o[Verify the tension of toothed belt and adjust it](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-5.htm#XREF_D_SE_0062299_1), if necessary.  oVerify the axis by visual inspection for any damage or missing parts. Especially moving parts and parts at risk for collisions such as carriage, contact plate, toothed belt, toothed belt tensioner, ball bearing of the toothed belt pulley, cover strip, cover strip clamp, rubber buffer, sensor, or mounting elements.  oVerify the sensors for functionality.  oVerify all parts for bolted connections.  oVerify the guide system for unusual noises. If unusual noises occur, refer to [Troubleshooting](../ROBOTICS_Troubleshooting/ROBOTICS_Troubleshooting.htm#XREF_D_SE_0065635_1).  o[Lubricate the guide system](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-23.htm#XREF_D_SE_0061787_1) |
| Annually  or  after an uncontrolled stop (stop category 0) | oVerify the brake function during operations. For further information, refer to the corresponding motor manual of the optionally pre-installed Schneider Electric motor. |
| Every four years after the date of manufacturing (DOM) | o[Replace the toothed belt as a precautionary measure](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-18.htm#XREF_D_SE_0106425_1).  oSuggestion: Replace the [toothed belt pulleys](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-19.htm#XREF_D_SE_0106426_1) when replacing the toothed belt. |

NOTE: The gearbox (optional equipment) and the ball bearings are lubricated for life.
