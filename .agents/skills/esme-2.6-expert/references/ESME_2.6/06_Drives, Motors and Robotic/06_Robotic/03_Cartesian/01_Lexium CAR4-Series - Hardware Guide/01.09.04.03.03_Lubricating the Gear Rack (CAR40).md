# Lubricating the Gear Rack (CAR40)

Lubricating the Gear Rack (CAR40)

The gear rack must be lubricated.

The maintenance intervals at normal [ambient conditions](../ROBOTICS_Technical_Data/ROBOTICS_Technical_Data-2.htm#XREF_D_SE_0056648_1) are indicated in the [maintenance plan](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-4.htm#XREF_D_SE_0067382_1). Shorten the indicated intervals accordingly in severe conditions or at the following influencing factors:

oDust and dirt particles

oHigh operating temperatures

oHeavy loads

oHeavy vibration

oPermanent short-distance positioning

For lubricating the gear rack, only use Microlube GL 261 or alternative lubricants with label K1N-30 according to DIN 51825.

NOTE: Clean the gear rack before lubrication.
