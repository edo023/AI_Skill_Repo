# Fastening the Magnetic Strips

Fastening the Magnetic Strips

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Clean the mounting surfaces of the magnetic strips at the axis body (1) such that they are free of grease.  G-SE-0082855.1.gif-high.gif |
| 2 | Manually move the carriages into end position on the right-hand side. |
| 3 | Place the magnetic strips flush with the face of the mounting surface along the axis body from the end blocks on the left-hand side to the carriages on the right-hand side and cut them with the scissors. |
| 4 | Peel off the film of the magnetic strips (2) inch by inch and fasten them with the glued side onto the mounting surface of the axis body.  G-SE-0082856.1.gif-high.gif      NOTE:  oDo not pull. Lay down the magnetic strip inch by inch on the mounting surface and then press it down.  oAllow the adhesive to cure for 24 hours before starting operation. |
| 5 | Manually move the carriages to the left-hand side until you have enough space on the right-hand side to fasten the magnetic strips to the rest of the mounting surfaces. |
| 6 | Place the magnetic strips flush with the face of the mounting surface along the axis body from the end of the already fastened magnetic strips to the end blocks on the right-hand side and cut them with the scissors. |
| 7 | Peel off the film of the magnetic strips (2) inch by inch and fasten them with the glued side onto the mounting surface of the axis body. |
