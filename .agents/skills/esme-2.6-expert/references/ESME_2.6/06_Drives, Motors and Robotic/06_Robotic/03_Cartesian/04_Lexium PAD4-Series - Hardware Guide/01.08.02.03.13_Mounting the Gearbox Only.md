# Mounting the Gearbox Only

Mounting the Gearbox Only

A flange plate is required if the gearbox does not have its own flange. If possible, position the axis such that the top of the carriages points upwards during installation.

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Fasten the flange plate (2) to the gearbox (3) with the four screws (1). Use the [standard tightening torque](../ROBOTICS_Transport_and_Comissioning/ROBOTICS_Transport_and_Comissioning-6.htm#XREF_D_SE_0088555_4). Verify that there is no clearance between the flange plate and the gearbox such that the surfaces are flush.  G-SE-0082873.1.gif-high.gif      NOTE: Greasing every centering collar provides easier dismounting. Use mineral oil-based lubricants without additives, or use silicon-based lubricants. |
| 2 | Remove the setscrew (4) at the drive unit adaptation.  G-SE-0083133.1.gif-high.gif |
| 3 | Verify that the orientation of the clamping screw (5) is the same as the orientation of the setscrew of the drive unit adaptation. |
| 4 | Fit the flange plate complete with gearbox into the clamping hub (6) and to the drive unit adaptation and secure the gearbox from falling. Verify that there is no clearance between the gearbox and the drive unit adaptation such that the surfaces are flush.  G-SE-0082874.1.gif-high.gif      NOTE: Greasing every centering collar provides easier dismounting. Use mineral oil-based lubricants without additives, or use silicon-based lubricants. |
| 5 | Fasten the flange plate complete with gearbox with the four screws (7). Use the [standard tightening torque](../ROBOTICS_Transport_and_Comissioning/ROBOTICS_Transport_and_Comissioning-6.htm#XREF_D_SE_0088555_4). |
| 6 | Tighten the screw of the clamping hub through the hole in the drive unit adaptation.  Tightening torque: 14 Nm (124 lbf-in) |
| 7 | Insert the setscrew into the drive unit adaptation and tighten the setscrew.  Tightening torque: 4 Nm (35.4 lbf-in) |
