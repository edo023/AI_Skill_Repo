# Options for Moving the Axis Without Drive Energy

Options for Moving the Axis Without Drive Energy

The axis mechanics are not equipped with an enclosure (see UL 1740).

NOTE: Take appropriate security measures concerning the specific use before operating the axis.

|  |
| --- |
| Warning_Color.gifWARNING |
| SAGGING OF THE AXIS |
| Ensure that release of the motor brakes poses no additional risks in the zone of operation. |
| Failure to follow these instructions can result in death, serious injury, or equipment damage. |

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Put the axis into a torque-free state. |
| 2 | Manually hold the carriage in position. |
| 3 | Open the motor brakes.  NOTE: The function for opening the brakes as well as for torque-free switching of the motors is not included within the scope of supply. Implement the same as a component part of the design of the electrical equipment. |
| 4 | Manually move the carriage.  NOTE: A greater force could be necessary because the motor and gearbox may pose resistance to movement. |
| 5 | Close the brakes. |
