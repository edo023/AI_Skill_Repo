# Mounting the Double Coupling

Mounting the Double Coupling

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Manually move the carriages into end position on the right-hand side. |
| 2 | Fit the shaft of the double coupling (1) into the hollow shaft of the toothed belt pulleys.  G-SE-0083013.1.gif-high.gif |
| 3 | Align the double coupling so that it is centered between the end blocks.  G-SE-0083014.1.gif-high.gif |
| 4 | Fit the screw (3), the front cone (2) and the rear cone (4) into the shaft of the double coupling.  G-SE-0083015.1.gif-high.gif |
| 5 | Press against the rear cone and tighten the screw of the double coupling.  Tightening torque: 14 Nm (124 lbf-in)  G-SE-0083016.1.gif-high.gif      NOTE: The double coupling shall not rotate further during tightening. If it does, verify that the carriages are at the end position and the toothed belt is installed correctly. |
| 6 | If applicable, mount the [shaft extension](../ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment/ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment-8.htm#XREF_D_SE_0106253_1). |
| 7 | If applicable, mount the [motor and/or gearbox](../ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment/ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment-4.htm#XREF_D_SE_0106085_1). |
