# Maintenance Plan

## Overview

The maintenance intervals may have to be adapted to the greatly varying operational live depending on the application.

NOTE: Contact your local Schneider Electric service representative prior to replacing any equipment or parts of the axis.

## Maintenance Schedule

Perform the following maintenance tasks after delivery:

| Date | Action |
| --- | --- |
| Before installing the axis | * Verify the axis by [visual inspection for any damage or missing parts](D-SE-0106466.html), especially for moving parts and parts at risk for collisions such as carriages, axis profile, contact plates, toothed belts, toothed belt tensioners, ball bearings of the toothed belt pulley, rubber buffers, sensors, or mounting elements. |

Perform the following maintenance tasks within the stated intervals:

| Interval | Action |
| --- | --- |
| Every 400 hours or 1,000 km (621 mi) of operation  or  every month | * [Clean the axis](D-SE-0088562.html#D-SE-0088562), if necessary. * [Lubricate the guide system](D-SE-0057273.html), if necessary. |
| Every 2,000 hours or 5,000 km (3,107 mi) of operation  or  every six months | * [Clean the axis](D-SE-0088562.html#D-SE-0088562). * Verify the [toothed belt abrasion](D-SE-0106466.html#D-SE-0106466). If a toothed belt needs to be replaced, contact your local Schneider Electric service representative . * Verify the axis by [visual inspection for any damage or missing parts](D-SE-0106466.html), especially for moving parts and parts at risk for collisions such as carriages, axis profile, contact plates, toothed belts, toothed belt tensioners, ball bearings of the toothed belt pulley, rubber buffers, sensors, or mounting elements. * Verify the sensors for functionality. * Verify all parts for bolted connections. * Verify the guide system for unusual noises. If unusual noises occur, refer to [*Troubleshooting*](D-SE-0065635.html#D-SE-0065635). * [Lubricate the guide system](D-SE-0057273.html). |
| Annually  or  after an uncontrolled stop (stop category 0) | * Verify the brake function during operations. For further information, refer to the corresponding motor manual of the optionally pre-installed Schneider Electric motor. |
| Every four years after the date of manufacturing (DOM) | * Replace the toothed belts as a precautionary measure. For further information, contact your local Schneider Electric service representative . * Suggestion: Replace the toothed belt pulley when replacing the toothed belt. For further information, contact your local Schneider Electric service representative |

NOTE: The gearbox and the ball bearings are lubricated for life.
