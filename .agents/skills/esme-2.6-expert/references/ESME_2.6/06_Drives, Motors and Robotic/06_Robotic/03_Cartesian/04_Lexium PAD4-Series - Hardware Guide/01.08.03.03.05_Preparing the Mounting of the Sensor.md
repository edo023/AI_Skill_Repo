# Preparing the Mounting of the Sensor

Preparing the Mounting of the Sensor

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Verify the sensor for correct type and function. |
| 2 | Verify that your controller and your interface are suitable for the sensor. |
| 3 | Clean all parts. |
| 4 | Inspect all parts for damage. |

NOTE: Polluted or damaged parts may cause run-out which has an adverse effect on the service life of the axis.

| --- |
