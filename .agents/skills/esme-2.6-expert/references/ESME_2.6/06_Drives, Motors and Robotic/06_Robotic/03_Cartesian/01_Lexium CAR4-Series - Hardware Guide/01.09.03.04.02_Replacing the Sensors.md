# Replacing the Sensors

Replacing the Sensors

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Disconnect the sensor (1).  G-SE-0061828.1.gif-high.gif |
| 2 | Loosen the sensor nut (2). |
| 3 | Remove the sensor from the fasting thread. |
| 4 | Mount the new sensor as described in [Mounting the Sensors](../ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment/ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment-7.htm#XREF_D_SE_0067153_1). |
| 5 | Connect the new sensor.  For further information, refer to [Technical Data of the Sensors](../ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment/ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment-6.htm#XREF_D_SE_0061778_1). |
