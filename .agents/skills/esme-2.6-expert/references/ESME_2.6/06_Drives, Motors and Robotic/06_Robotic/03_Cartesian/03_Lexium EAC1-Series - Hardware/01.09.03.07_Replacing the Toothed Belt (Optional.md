# Replacing the Toothed Belt (Optional Equipment)

![](images/logo.png)

Replacing the Toothed Belt (Optional Equipment)

Overview

The toothed belt is a part of the belt drive (optional equipment). Replacing the toothed belt can cause unintended movements.

|  |
| --- |
| Warning_Color.gifWARNING |
| UNINTENDED MOVEMENTS DUE TO DISMOUNTING |
| Secure the moving parts of the actuator mounted in a vertical or tilted position against unexpected movements. |
| Failure to follow these instructions can result in death, serious injury, or equipment damage. |

Prerequisites

You need the following tools to replace the toothed belt:

oSet of hex keys

NOTE: Do not use ball head hex keys. Excessive torque may cause the ball head to break away. A broken ball head makes the removal of the screw difficult.

For suitable parts, refer to [Replacement Equipment of the Lexium EAC1-Series](../ROBOTICS_Replacement_Equipment/ROBOTICS_Replacement_Equipment-3.htm#XREF_D_SE_0081355_1).

Preparing the Replacement of the Toothed Belt

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |

Replacing the Toothed Belt
