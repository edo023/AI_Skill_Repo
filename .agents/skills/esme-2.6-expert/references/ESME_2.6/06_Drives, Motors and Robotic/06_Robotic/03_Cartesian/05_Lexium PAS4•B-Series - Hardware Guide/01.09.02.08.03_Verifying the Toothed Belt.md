# Verifying the Toothed Belt

Verifying the Toothed Belt

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Verify the toothed belt by a visual inspection for damaged teeth and abrasion. To perform a visual inspection, maybe it is necessary to [remove the toothed belt](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-18.htm#XREF_D_SE_0061823_1). |

NOTE: A damaged toothed belt must be replaced. For detailed information, refer to [Replacing the Toothed Belt](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-18.htm#XREF_D_SE_0061823_1).
