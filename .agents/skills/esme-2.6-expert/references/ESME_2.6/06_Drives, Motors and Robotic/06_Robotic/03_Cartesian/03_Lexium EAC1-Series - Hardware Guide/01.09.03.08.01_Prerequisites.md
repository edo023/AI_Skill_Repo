# Prerequisites

Prerequisites

You need the following tools to replace accessories:

oSet of hex keys

NOTE: Do not use ball head hex keys. Excessive torque may cause the ball head to break away. A broken ball head makes the removal of the screw difficult.

For suitable parts, refer to [Replacement Equipment of the Lexium EAC1-Series](../ROBOTICS_Replacement_Equipment/ROBOTICS_Replacement_Equipment-3.htm#XREF_D_SE_0081355_1).
