# Mounting Position of the Motor and/or the Belt Drive

Mounting Position of the Motor and/or the Belt Drive

In case of a new motor and/or a new belt drive, you can mount the new motor to the motor adaptation and the belt drive to the drive cap. The motor and/or belt drive can be mounted in different arrangements (turned in increments of 4 x 90°). For further information, refer to [Motor and/or Belt Drive Orientation and Configuration](../ROBOTICS_System_Overview/ROBOTICS_System_Overview-4.htm#XREF_D_SE_0081309_4).
