# Mounting the Cover Strip

Mounting the Cover Strip

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Guide the cover strip (2) through the guide channel inside the carriage (1).  G-SE-0054189.1.gif-high.gif |
| 2 | Guide the cover strip through the strip deflectors (3) at both sides of the carriage.  G-SE-0054190.1.gif-high.gif |
| 3 | Fasten the strip deflector and the holding plate (4) to the carriage with the two screws (5) at both sides of the carriage. Use the [standard tightening torque](../ROBOTICS_Transport_and_Comissioning/ROBOTICS_Transport_and_Comissioning-6.htm#XREF_D_SE_0088555_4). Verify that there is no clearance between the carriage, the strip deflectors, and the holding plates such that the surfaces are flush.  G-SE-0054191.1.gif-high.gif |
| 4 | Place the cover strip from one of the two end blocks at a distance of 1…2 mm (0.04…0.08 in). Verify that there is no clearance between the cover strip and the magnetic strips such that the surfaces are flush.  G-SE-0056583.1.gif-high.gif |
| 5 | Fit the clamping plate (6) into place.  G-SE-0072845.1.gif-high.gif |
| 6 | Fasten the cover strip clamp (7) to the end block with the two screws (8). Verify that there is no clearance between the cover strip clamp and the end block such that the surfaces are flush  Tightening torque:  oFor PAS41: 0.6 Nm (5.3 lbf-in)  oFor PAS42: 1 Nm (8.9 lbf-in)  oFor PAS43: 1.5 Nm (13.3 lbf-in)  oFor PAS44: 3 Nm (26.6 lbf-in) |
| 7 | Tighten the two setscrews (9) of the cover strip clamp.  Tightening torque:  oFor PAS41: 0.2 Nm (0.0079 lbf-in)  oFor PAS42: 0.3 Nm (0.0118 lbf-in)  oFor PAS43: 0.4 Nm (0.0157 lbf-in)  oFor PAS44: 0.5 Nm (0.0197 lbf-in)  G-SE-0072844.1.gif-high.gif |
| 8 | Cut the cover strip at the other end with the metal shear to length such that the distance between the cover strip and the second end block is also 1…2 mm (0.04…0.08 in). |
| 9 | Repeat steps 5, 6 and 7 for the second end block. |
| 10 | Fasten the rubber buffers (10) to the holding plates at both sides of the carriage with the two screws (11). Verify that there is no clearance between the rubber buffers and the holding plates such that the surfaces are flush.  Tightening torque:  oFor PAS41: 1.1 Nm (9.7 lbf-in)  oFor PAS42: 1.1 Nm (9.7 lbf-in)  oFor PAS43: 2.5 Nm (22 lbf-in)  oFor PAS44: 2.5 Nm (22 lbf-in)  G-SE-0054193.1.gif-high.gif |
