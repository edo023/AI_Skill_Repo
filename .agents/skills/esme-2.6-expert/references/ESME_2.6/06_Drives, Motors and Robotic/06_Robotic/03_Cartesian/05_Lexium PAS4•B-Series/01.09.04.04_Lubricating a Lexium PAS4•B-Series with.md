# Lubricating a Lexium PAS4•B-Series with Recirculating Ball Bearing Guide

![](images/logo.png)

Lubricating a Lexium PAS4•B-Series with Recirculating Ball Bearing Guide

Overview

The axis is lubricated with grease from a factory-prefilled external reservoir. The carriage features two lubrication nipples at each side for lubrication.

The maintenance intervals at normal [ambient conditions](../ROBOTICS_Technical_Data/ROBOTICS_Technical_Data-2.htm#XREF_D_SE_0056648_1) are indicated in the [maintenance plan](ROBOTICS_Maintenance_and_Repair-4.htm#XREF_D_SE_0086231_1).

Shorten the indicated intervals accordingly in severe conditions or at the following influencing factors:

oDust and dirt particles

oHigh operating temperatures

oHeavy loads

oHeavy vibration

oPermanent short-distance positioning

You need the following tools to lubricate a Lexium PAS4•B-Series with recirculating ball bearing guide:

oLubrication gun

oLubricant

NOTE: Use the grease lubrication gun for the following steps.

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Press the nozzle in a right angle against one of the lubrication nipples (1) and inject the specified amount of lubricant. NOTE:  oWhen applying the lubricant, you must not exceed a maximum flow rate. Therefore, the minimum injection time of 3 seconds per lubrication gun stroke must be adhered to.  oMove the carriage between the strokes to distribute the grease into the lubricant reservoirs evenly. | 2 | Repeat step 1 alternating for the first and the second lubrication nipple at one side of the carriage until you reach the specified number of strokes. |
