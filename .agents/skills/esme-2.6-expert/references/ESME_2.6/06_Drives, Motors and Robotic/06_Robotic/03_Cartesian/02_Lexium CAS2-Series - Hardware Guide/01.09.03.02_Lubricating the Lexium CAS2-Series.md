# Lubricating the Lexium CAS2-Series

## Overview

The axis is pre-lubricated with grease from the factory and ready to use. Each guide carriage has two lubrication nipples on the front sides for lubrication (8 in total).

The maintenance intervals at normal [ambient conditions](AmbientConditions-623F1CD9.html) are indicated in the [maintenance plan](CAS2_MaintenancePlan.html).

Shorten the indicated intervals accordingly in severe conditions or at the following influencing factors:

* Dust and dirt particles
* High operating temperatures
* Heavy loads
* Heavy vibration
* Permanent short-distance positioning

## Prerequisites

You need the following tools to lubricate a Lexium CAS2-Series with recirculating ball bearing guide:

* Lubrication gun
* Lubricant

For suitable lubrication equipment, refer to [*Replacement Equipment and Accessories*](D-SE-0065517.html).

## Lubricating a Lexium CAS2-Series with Recirculating Ball Bearing Guide

NOTE: Use the grease lubrication gun for the following steps.

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Press the nozzle straight against the grease nipples (1) and inject the specified amount of lubricant. Then repeat this step with the other grease nipples located at the carriages (2).  For the correct type and quantity of lubricant, refer to [*Lubrication Amount*](#CAS2_Lubricating__D-SE-0061787.3).  NOTE:  * When applying the lubricant, you must not exceed a maximum flow rate. Therefore, the minimum injection time of 3 seconds per lubrication gun stroke must be adhered to. * Move the carriage 2 between the strokes to distribute the grease into the lubricant reservoirs evenly. |
