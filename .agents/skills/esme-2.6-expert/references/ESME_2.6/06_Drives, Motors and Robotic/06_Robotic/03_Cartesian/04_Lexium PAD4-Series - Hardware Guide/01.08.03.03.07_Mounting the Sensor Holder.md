# Mounting the Sensor Holder

Mounting the Sensor Holder

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Slide both nuts (1) into the T-slot at the cutout.  G-SE-0082881.1.gif-high.gif |
| 2 | Position the sensor holder (2) with the two screws (3) at the nuts. Tighten the screws slightly so that you can move the sensor holder together with the nuts.  G-SE-0082882.1.gif-high.gif      NOTE: The slot of the sensor holder should point to the carriages. |
| 3 | Slide the senor holder to the appropriate sensor position and tighten the screw on the closed side of the sensor holder (3).  Tightening torque: 0.3 Nm (2.66 lbf-in).  NOTE: For further information on the sensor position, refer to the dimensional drawing of the respective axis in [Mechanical Data](../ROBOTICS_Technical_Data/ROBOTICS_Technical_Data-3.htm#XREF_D_SE_0088553_1). |
