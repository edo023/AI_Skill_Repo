# Replacing the Coupling

Replacing the Coupling

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Remove the four screws (2) at the drive unit adaptation (1).  G-SE-0083024.1.gif-high.gif |
| 2 | Remove the drive unit adaptation. |
| 3 | Remove the four screws (4) at the coupling housing (3).  G-SE-0083025.1.gif-high.gif |
| 4 | Remove the coupling housing. |
| 5 | Remove the elastomer spider (5) from the expanding hub.  G-SE-0083026.1.gif-high.gif |
| 6 | Manually move the carriages into end position on the left-hand side. |
| 7 | Loosen the screw (6) on the expanding hub.  G-SE-0083027.1.gif-high.gif      NOTE: The expanding hub shall not rotate during tightening. If it does, verify whether the carriages are at the end position and the toothed belt is installed correctly. |
| 8 | Carefully tap on the screw head with a soft-faced hammer so that the cone of the expanding hub comes loose.  NOTE: If you cannot reach the screw head, use a hex key as extension and tap carefully on the hex key. |
| 9 | Pull the expanding hub carefully out of the hollow shaft of the toothed belt pulley (7).  G-SE-0083028.1.gif-high.gif      NOTE: This may require a considerable force. |
| 10 | Mount the new coupling as well as the motor or the gearbox as described in [Mounting the Motor and Gearbox](../ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment/ROBOTICS_Optional_Equipment-4.htm#XREF_D_SE_0106085_1). |
