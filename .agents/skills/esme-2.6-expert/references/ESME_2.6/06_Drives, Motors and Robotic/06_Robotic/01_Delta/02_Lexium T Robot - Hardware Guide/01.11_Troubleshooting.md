# Troubleshooting

## Overview

| Issue | Probable cause | Solution |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Trouble with components | End of component life cycle | Note the [maintenance plan](D-SE-0066024.html#D-SE-0066024). |
| Rattling noise | Screw joints came loose | Verify and tighten the screw joints if necessary. |
| Oil beads up at new gearbox | Initial oozing of the new gearbox | [Clean the gearbox dry](D-SE-0059478.html#D-SE-0059478). |
| Grease at new ball bearing | Initial oozing of the new bearings | [Clean the ball bearing dry](D-SE-0059478.html#D-SE-0059478). |
| Extensive position deviation after motor replacement | Robot is not calibrated | [Calibrate the robot](D-SE-0059492.html#D-SE-0059492). |
| Incorrect motor replacement | [Mount the motor correctly](D-SE-0066101.html#D-SE-0066101). |
