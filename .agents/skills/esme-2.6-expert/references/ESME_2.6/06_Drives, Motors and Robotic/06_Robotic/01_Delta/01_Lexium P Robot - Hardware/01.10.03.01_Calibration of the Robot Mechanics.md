# Calibration of the Robot Mechanics

## Overview

The robot has been calibrated in the factory and the data/positions of the axes can be read from the motor encoders. A further alignment is not necessary. However, in a service case, calibration via the control panel may become necessary in order to restore the intended operation.

## Carry Out the Calibration

Carry out the calibration in the following cases:

* Following the replacement of a component (motor, gearbox, upper arm).
* Following deletion or overwriting of the calibration data.

Calibrating the robot mechanics must be carried out by Schneider Electric personnel or qualified personnel only, as this requires an expert level of knowledge of the PacDrive System.

For this purpose, the `SchneiderElectricRobotics` library contains an interface that has the necessary modes and parameters.
