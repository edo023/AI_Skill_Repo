# Maintaining the Rolls on the Spring Pack of the Lower Arm

## Overview

Periodically verify and replace rolls in accordance with the [maintenance schedule](D-SE-0059471.html#D-SE-0059471__D-SE-0059471.3).

NOTE: Roll wear increases the wear of the springs.

## Maintaining the Rolls on the Spring Pack of the Lower Arm

| WARNING | |
| --- | --- |
|  | COLLISION OF COMPONENTS  * Replace rolls frequently. * Always replace rolls and springs together. * After replacing the rolls, verify them for free rotatability.  Failure to follow these instructions can result in death, serious injury, or equipment damage. |

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Verify whether the rolls can move by more than 0.5 mm (0.0197 in) in radial direction. |
| 2 | If the rolls can move by more, replace the [rolls](D-SE-0059487.html#D-SE-0059487) and the [springs](D-SE-0059486.html#D-SE-0059486). |
