# Maintaining the Slide Films

## Overview

The slide films on the telescopic axis wear with time so that backlash starts to occur on the telescopic axis.

How fast slide films wear depends on the following:

* Load applied
* Paths traveled
* Movement speeds and transverse accelerations
* Quantity and type of contamination
* Material of the slide films

## Verifying the Wear of the Slide Films

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Measure the circumferential backlash of the slide films. |
| 2 | If the backlash is greater than +/-0.5° or no longer sufficient for the application, [replace the slide films](D-SE-0059482.html#D-SE-0059482). |
| 3 | Verify the total operating hours of the robot according to the [maintenance plan](D-SE-0059471.html#D-SE-0059471) and [replace the slide films if necessary](D-SE-0059482.html#D-SE-0059482). |
