# Replacing the Fans

## Procedure Overview

Perform the following procedures to replace the fans:

* [Removing the fans](#D-SE-0066449__D-SE-0066449.3)
* [Mounting the new fans](#D-SE-0066449__D-SE-0066449.4)

## Removing the Fans

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Remove the maintenance cover and the motor cover as described in [*Opening the* Robot](D-SE-0059431.html#D-SE-0059431). |
| 2 | Disconnect the fan wires from the internal strip. |
| 3 | Remove the four fastening clips by putting them out from main motor side. |
| 4 | Remove the fans from the robot housing. |

## Mounting the New Fans

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Insert the fans into the robot housing.  For further information about the position of the fans, refer to the detail drawings in [*Mechanical and Electrical Data*](D-SE-0056649.html#D-SE-0056649). |
| 2 | Fasten the fastening clips to the robot housing. |
| 3 | Connect the fan wires to the internal strip.  For further information, refer to the cabling definition in [*Electrical Installation*](D-SE-0059433.html#D-SE-0059433). |
| 4 | Mount the maintenance cover and motor cover as described in [*Closing the* Robot](D-SE-0059443.html#D-SE-0059443). |
