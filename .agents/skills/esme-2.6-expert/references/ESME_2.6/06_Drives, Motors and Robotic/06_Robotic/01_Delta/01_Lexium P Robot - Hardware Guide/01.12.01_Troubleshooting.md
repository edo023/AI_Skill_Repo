# Troubleshooting

## Overview

| Malfunction | Probable cause | Solution |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Trouble with components. | End of component life cycle. | Note the [maintenance plan](D-SE-0059471.html#D-SE-0059471). |
| Squeaking. | Ball sockets are contaminated. | [Clean the ball sockets dry](D-SE-0059478.html#D-SE-0059478). |
| Ball sockets are worn out. | [Replace the ball sockets](D-SE-0059485.html#D-SE-0059485). |
| Spring roll is worn out. | [Replace the spring roll](D-SE-0059487.html#D-SE-0059487). |
| Rattling noise. | A spring is absent and/or damaged.  NOTE: Verify the upper arm regarding mechanical attachment. | [Replace the springs](D-SE-0059486.html#D-SE-0059486). |
| Oil beads up at new gearbox. | Initial oozing of new gearboxes. | [Clean the gearbox dry](D-SE-0059478.html#D-SE-0059478). |
| Grease at new ball bearing. | Initial oozing of new bearings. | [Clean the ball bearing dry](D-SE-0059478.html#D-SE-0059478). |
| Extensive position deviation after motor replacement. | The robot is not calibrated. | [Calibrate the robot](D-SE-0059492.html#D-SE-0059492). |
| Incorrect motor replacement. | [Mount the motor correctly](D-SE-0059490.html#D-SE-0059490). |
