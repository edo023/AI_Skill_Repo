# Replacing the Lower Arms

| WARNING | |
| --- | --- |
|  | FALLING HEAVY LOAD  Secure in place the parallel plate and lower arm when loosening and removing the screws.  Failure to follow these instructions can result in death, serious injury, or equipment damage. |

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Carefully remove the four screws (2) and the four lock washers (3) from the lower half shell at the lower arm (1) and then remove the shell (4).    NOTE: Take care that the indexing bolts (5) of the lower arm are located inside the holes of the shaft of the upper arm. |
| 2 | Carefully remove the four screws (10) and the four lock washers (11) from the upper half shell (8) at the lower arm (1) and then remove the shell.    NOTE: Take care about the indexing bolts (6) of the lower arm. |
| 3 | Replace the lower arm and mount the shells in reverse order.  For further information, refer to [*Mounting the Lower Arms with Parallel Plate*](D-SE-0103687.html#D-SE-0103687). |
