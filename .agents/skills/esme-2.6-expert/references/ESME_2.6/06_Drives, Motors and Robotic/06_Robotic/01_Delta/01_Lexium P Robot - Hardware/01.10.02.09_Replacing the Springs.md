# Replacing the Springs

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | [Remove the lower arms](D-SE-0059484.html#D-SE-0059484). |
| 2 | Push the springs off the spring brackets until the eyes have been unhooked. |
| 3 | Place the lower arms side by side. |
| 4 | With the open side of the eye, join the spring to the respective spring bracket. |
| 5 | Push the eyes of the springs into the spring brackets to completely embed the springs into the spring brackets. |
