# Replacement Equipment Inventory

## Overview

Keeping a stock of important components helps ensure the availability of your machine. Only exchange devices with identical types to help ensure compatibility.

Indicate the following information on the replacement equipment order, which can be found on the logistic [type plate](D-SE-0065761.html#D-SE-0065761):

| Parameter | Position on type plate | Example value |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Item name | First line | Robot T5m-F-NC-35-1500 |
| Item reference (type code) | ID-No | VRKT5M0FNC00000 |
| Hardware revision | HW | S00 |
