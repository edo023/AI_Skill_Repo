# Maintaining the Fans

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Verify that the fans are working correctly.  NOTE: The fans take air from the central area of the housing and blow it out through the slit in the motor covers. |
| 2 | If the fans do not work correctly, [replace the fans](D-SE-0066449.html#D-SE-0066449). |
