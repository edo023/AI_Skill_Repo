# Maintenance Plan

## Overview

Regular maintenance verifications are a form of preventive maintenance to keep the robot in good working condition and to help prevent malfunctions. It must be performed by trained maintenance personnel.

The maintenance intervals may have to be adapted to the operational hours depending on the application.

The maintenance intervals must take into account the natural wear and tear based on how you operate the Lexium SCARA.
