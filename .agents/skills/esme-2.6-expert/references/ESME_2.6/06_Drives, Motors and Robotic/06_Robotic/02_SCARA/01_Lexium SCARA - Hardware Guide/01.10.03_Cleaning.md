# Cleaning

## Overview

Only use wipes wetted with a cleaner with 70% Isopropyl alcohol (IPA70).

For painted parts, you can use the suggested cleaner if you wipe softly and without long application. Nevertheless, the repetitive mechanical action of wiping may damage the brightness of the paint.

Highly corrosive detergents are not suitable for cleaning the robot. Do not immerse anodized parts in cleaning solution.
