# Service Life

## Overview

The service life of the Lexium™ MC12 multi carrier is a function of the mean forces and torques that act in the system.

For detailed information about the service life of the system, refer to [Maintenance Plan](TPC_MLS-HWG_Maintenance_Plan-864670BC.html#TPC_MLS-HWG_Maintenance_Plan-864670BC).
