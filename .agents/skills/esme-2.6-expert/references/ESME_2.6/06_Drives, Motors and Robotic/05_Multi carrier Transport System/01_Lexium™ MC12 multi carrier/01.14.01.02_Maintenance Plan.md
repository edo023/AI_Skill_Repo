# Maintenance Plan

## Overview

The data given in the maintenance schedule are indicative only. The data have to be adapted depending on the application and the environmental conditions.

For the maintenance procedures, refer to:

[Cleaning](TPC_MLS-HWG_Cleaning-86471E69.html#TPC_MLS-HWG_Cleaning-86471E69)

[Replacing Parts](TPC_MLS-HWG_Replacing_Parts-864A2787.html#TPC_MLS-HWG_Replacing_Parts-864A2787)

[Lubrication](TPC_MLS-HWG_Lubrication-864D1754.html#TPC_MLS-HWG_Lubrication-864D1754)

NOTE: For information specific to the Heavy-Duty system, refer to [Maintenance Schedule](MaintenanceSchedule-F8F6FC28.html).

## Maintenance Schedule

Perform the following maintenance tasks after delivery or long storage:

| Interval(1) | Action |
| --- | --- |
| After delivery(2) | Fill the lubrication reservoirs of the carriers. Refer to [Lubrication](TPC_MLS-HWG_Lubrication-864D1754.html#TPC_MLS-HWG_Lubrication-864D1754). |
| After delivery, every 1000 km (621 miles) or within the first two months of service life | Verify the Lexium™ MC12 multi carrier by visual inspection for any damage or missing parts, especially for moving parts and parts at risk for collisions such as mounting elements. |
| After ≥ two years of storage | Verify the lubrication pads of the carriers and replace if necessary. Refer to [Replacing Parts](TPC_MLS-HWG_Replacing_Parts-864A2787.html#TPC_MLS-HWG_Replacing_Parts-864A2787). |
| **(1)** The distances referred to are for one carrier. The performance of your system may differ.  **(2)** Filling the lubrication reservoirs of the carriers helps to ensure an even lubrication of the carrier rollers and the rails. | |

Perform the following maintenance tasks within the stated intervals.

NOTE: The intervals in the following table are guidelines. The intervals can be adjusted depending on your application and the environmental conditions (track length, number of carriers, motion profile, temperature, and so on).

| Interval(1) | Action |
| --- | --- |
| Every 1000 km (621 miles) of operation | * Clean the Lexium™ MC12 multi carrier if necessary. Refer to [Cleaning](TPC_MLS-HWG_Cleaning-86471E69.html#TPC_MLS-HWG_Cleaning-86471E69). * Verify that the floating bearing of the lower carrier rollers moves freely in axial direction. Clean if necessary and make the rollers run smoothly. Refer to [Cleaning the Carrier Rollers](TPC_MLS-HWG_Cleaning-86471E69.html#TPC_MLS-HWG_Cleaning-86471E69__CleaningTheCarrierRollers-D984BF37). * Verify the lubrication pads of the carriers for wear and replace them if necessary. Refer to [Replacing Parts](TPC_MLS-HWG_Replacing_Parts-864A2787.html#TPC_MLS-HWG_Replacing_Parts-864A2787). |
| Every 1000 km (621 miles)... 8000 km (4970 miles)(2) | Lubricate the carriers depending on the application. Refer to [Lubrication](TPC_MLS-HWG_Lubrication-864D1754.html#TPC_MLS-HWG_Lubrication-864D1754). As examples, two test cases were conducted that required refilling the lubricant reservoirs.   * Test application for 1000 km (621 miles): 1.5 m (59 in.) track length, 1 carrier, laboratory environment at 35 °C (95 °F) * Test application for 8000 km (4970 miles): 5.2 m (205 in.) track length, 5 carriers, laboratory environment at 25 °C (77 °F) |
| Every 4000 km (2485 miles) of operation | * Clean the Lexium™ MC12 multi carrier. Refer to [Cleaning](TPC_MLS-HWG_Cleaning-86471E69.html#TPC_MLS-HWG_Cleaning-86471E69).   + Verify the covers of the segments and the drive magnets of the carriers for dirt and magnetic abrasion. Clean the covers and the magnets and remove the magnetic abrasion if necessary. Refer to [Removing Particles from the Magnets](TPC_MLS-HWG_Cleaning-86471E69.html#TPC_MLS-HWG_Cleaning-86471E69__RemovingMagneticAbrasionFromTheMagn-8CEED4F9).   + Verify the carrier rollers for dirt and clean if necessary.   + Verify the area of encoder magnets of the carriers for dirt, and clean if necessary.   + Verify the rails for dirt, and clean if necessary. * Verify the Lexium™ MC12 multi carrier by visual inspection for any damage or missing parts, especially moving parts and parts at risk for collisions such as mounting elements.    + Verify the drive magnets of the carriers for damage. The drive magnets cannot be replaced individually. Replace the plate with the two magnets as a whole if necessary. Refer to [Replacing the Drive Magnets of the Carrier](ReplacePermanentMagnets-A62E97D3.html#ReplacePermanentMagnets-A62E97D3).   + Verify the rails for wear or damage and replace them if necessary. |
| Every 20,000 km (12,427 miles) of operation | * Verify the carrier rollers for wear and replace the carrier rollers if necessary. Refer to [Replacing the Rollers of a Carrier](TPC_MLS-HWG_Replacing_Carrier_Rollers-86C0EA42.html#TPC_MLS-HWG_Replacing_Carrier_Rollers-86C0EA42). |
| **(1)** The distances referred to are dependent on your system and usage. More frequent usage may require more frequent lubrication applications.  **(2)** Not required for Lexium™ MC12 multi carrier tracks with automated lubrication. For a track with automated lubrication, ensure a sufficient supply of lubricant to the lubricant outlets. | |

Perform the following maintenance tasks if your carriers have exceptional movement profiles:

| Movement profile | Action |
| --- | --- |
| Reversing movements in which the carriers travel a distance of less than 200 mm (7.88 in). | * If a distance of less than 200 mm (7.88 in) is travelled, perform a lubrication run after 20,000 traverse cycles or earlier.   NOTE: Sufficient lubrication during reversing movements can only be reached if the carriers travel a distance of at least 200 mm (7.88 in). |
