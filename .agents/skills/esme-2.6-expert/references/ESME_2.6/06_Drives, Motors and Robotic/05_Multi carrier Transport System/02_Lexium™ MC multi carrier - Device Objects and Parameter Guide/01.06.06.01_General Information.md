# General Information

Before the first Sercos phase-up, you must define the geometry parameters of the Lexium MC Segment with the parameters [ConfiguredLength](ConfigLength-9BF3B372.html#ConfigLength-9BF3B372), [ConfiguredAngleFractionNumerator](ConfigAngle-9D769B32.html#ConfigAngle-9D769B32) and [ConfiguredAngleFractionDenominator](ConfigAngleDen-B31893E4.html#ConfigAngleDen-B31893E4).

During phase 2 of the Sercos phase-up, the values from the segment are read via Sercos and compared to the corresponding configured values. If a value read does not correspond to the configured value, the Sercos phase-up cannot be executed.

**Example:**  
The ConfiguredLength of a segment is 300 mm (11.81 in). The value read from the segment is 400 mm (15.75 in). So, the Sercos phase-up is not possible.
