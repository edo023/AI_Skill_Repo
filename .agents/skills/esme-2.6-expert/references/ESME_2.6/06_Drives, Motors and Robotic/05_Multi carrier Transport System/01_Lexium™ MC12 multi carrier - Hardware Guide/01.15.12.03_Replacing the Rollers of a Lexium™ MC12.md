# Replacing the Rollers of a Lexium™ MC12 Heavy-Duty Carrier

## Overview

A Heavy-Duty carrier has six rollers which must be replaced at the same time.

Refer to the [Maintenance Schedule](MaintenanceSchedule-F8F6FC28.html#MaintenanceSchedule-F8F6FC28) and the [Information About Replacing Parts of the Lexium™ MC12 Heavy-Duty Carrier](InformationAboutReplacingPartsOfThe-4CEEAC48.html#InformationAboutReplacingPartsOfThe-4CEEAC48).

## Replacing the Rollers

NOTE: Exposed or uninstalled carriers must have the protective cover of the drive magnets installed at all times. The cover is only removed at the time of carrier installation.

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Install the protective cover (7) of the drive magnets by sliding it onto the magnets if it is not already installed. Refer to [Removing the Protective Cover of the Drive Magnets](MountingCarrier-B69855A0.html#MountingCarrier-B69855A0__RemovingTheProtectiveCoverOfTheDriv-09755CCE).  NOTE: The rollers are available in two versions:  * Version 1: Upper rollers are mounted with fixed bearings (1 to 4). * Version 2: Lower rollers are mounted with loose bearings (5 and 6). |
| 2 | To replace the rollers, the rotatable arms have to be dismounted (as explained here from step 2 to 5): First, dismount the encoder support (**a**) by removing the four screws of the support. |
| 3 | Remove the cover plate (**b**) at the top and the bottom of the Heavy-Duty carrier by removing the six screws at each plate. |
| 4 | Under the cover plate (**b**) you can find the heads of the hex bolts (**c**) which hold the rotatable arms (**d**). Unscrew the two hex bolts (**c**) to dismount the rotatable arms (**d**). |
| 5 | The rollers (**f**) are mounted to the rotatable arms: loosen and remove the nuts (**e**) and dismount the rollers. |
| 6 | Replace the rollers (**f**) on both rotatable arms. |
| 7 | Before tightening the rollers again, refer to [Instructions for Secured Screw Connections](InformationAboutReplacingPartsOfThe-4CEEAC48.html#InformationAboutReplacingPartsOfThe-4CEEAC48__InstructionsForGluedScrewConnection-FA3043C8).  Place in the wedge-lock washers under the nuts and tighten the nuts.  (Upper rollers with 8 Nm (70.81 lbf-in), lower rollers with 3 Nm (26.55 lbf-in)). |
| 8 | Verify the lubrication pads of the Heavy-Duty carriers for wear and replace them if necessary. Refer to [Replacing the Lubrication Pads of a Lexium™ MC12 Heavy-Duty Carrier](ReplaceHDLubricationPads-F8F59F99.html#ReplaceHDLubricationPads-F8F59F99). |
| 9 | Before mounting the hex bolts to the rotatable arms, refer to [Instructions for Secured Screw Connections](InformationAboutReplacingPartsOfThe-4CEEAC48.html#InformationAboutReplacingPartsOfThe-4CEEAC48__InstructionsForGluedScrewConnection-FA3043C8).  NOTE: For the rotatable arms, apply thread-locking agent to the bolts and the threads of the arms.  Place the rotatable arms in position and push the hex bolts through the bearings (g) and fasten the hex bolts loosely in the threads of the rotatable arms. |
| 10 | Tighten the hex bolts (**c**) in the threads of the rotatable arms with 5 Nm (44.3 lbf-in). |
| 11 | Repeat step 4 to 10 to replace the lower rollers. |
| 12 | Before mounting the cover plates, refer to [Instructions for Secured Screw Connections](InformationAboutReplacingPartsOfThe-4CEEAC48.html#InformationAboutReplacingPartsOfThe-4CEEAC48__InstructionsForGluedScrewConnection-FA3043C8).  Place the cover plates at the top and the bottom of the Heavy-Duty carrier and mount them with their six screws. Tighten the screws with 2.5 Nm (22.1 lbf-in). |
| 13 | Before mounting the encoder support, refer to [Instructions for Secured Screw Connections](InformationAboutReplacingPartsOfThe-4CEEAC48.html#InformationAboutReplacingPartsOfThe-4CEEAC48__InstructionsForGluedScrewConnection-FA3043C8).  Mount the encoder support with its four screws. Tighten the screws with 1.0 Nm (8.9 lbf-in). |
