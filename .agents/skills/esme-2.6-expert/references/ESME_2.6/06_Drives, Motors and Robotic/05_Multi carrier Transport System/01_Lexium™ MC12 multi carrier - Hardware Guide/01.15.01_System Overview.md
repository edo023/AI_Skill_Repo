# System Overview

## Overview

The Lexium™ MC12 Heavy-Duty guide system is an additional offer to allow to operate the Lexium™ MC12 multi carrier system with greater payloads.

Due to different setup and dimensions of the Heavy-Duty carriers, the carriers require also a dedicated guidance with specific components like guide rails and spacers.

The Lexium™ MC12 Heavy-Duty guide system with the dedicated carriers, guide rails and spacers – detailed in this chapter – is a customized solution with some limitations compared to the standard Lexium™ MC12 guide system.

For further details on the differences to the standard Lexium™ MC12 guide system, contact your local Schneider Electric representative.

## **Components Overview**
