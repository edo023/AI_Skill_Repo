# Physical Environment

## Overview

The system is not protected against physical or chemical sources of damage by any design features, such as:

* Toxic
* Explosive
* Corrosive
* Highly reactive
* Inflammable types

The system has been designed to operate outside of any hazardous location. Only install the system in zones known to be free of a hazardous atmosphere.
