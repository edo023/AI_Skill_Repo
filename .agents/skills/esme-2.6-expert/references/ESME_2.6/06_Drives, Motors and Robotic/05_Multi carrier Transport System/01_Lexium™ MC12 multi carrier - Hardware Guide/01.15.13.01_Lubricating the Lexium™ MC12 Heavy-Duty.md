# Lubricating the Lexium™ MC12 Heavy-Duty Carrier

## Overview

The running surfaces of the four upper and two lower rollers of the Heavy-Duty carrier and the running surfaces of the Heavy-Duty guide rails must be lubricated. This is done by four upper and two lower lubrication pads. The Heavy-Duty carrier pads are delivered filled with lubricant and must be filled regularly.

The Heavy-Duty guide rails are lubricated via the Heavy-Duty carriers. Execute a Heavy-Duty carrier lubrication run on the Heavy-Duty guide rails after cleaning the rails.

NOTE: For a track with automated lubrication, ensure a sufficient supply of lubricant to the lubricant outlets.

For more information, refer to [Information About Lubrication](TPC_MLS-HWG_Info_Lubrication-864DAC04.html#TPC_MLS-HWG_Info_Lubrication-864DAC04).

Incorrect lubricants may damage the Heavy-Duty carriers and the Heavy-Duty guide rails. Insufficient lubrication or incorrect lubricants increase wear and reduce the service life.

## Filling the Lubrication Pads

The Heavy-Duty carrier can stay on the track during lubrication.

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | In the housing, a Heavy-Duty carrier has four lubrication nipples (1 to 4) to fill the four lubrication pads. Each lubrication pad has its own lubrication nipple.    Press the nozzle of a single-hand lubrication gun in a right angle against one of the lubrication nipples and inject the lubricant. |
|  |
| 2 | To fill the lubrication pads use one stroke of the lubrication gun.  NOTE: The lubrication process has to be done slowly. Use approximately one minute for the stroke to be sure that the oil has soaked up by the lubrication pad. |
| 3 | Proceed in this way to fill the two upper and the two lower lubrication pads. |
| 4 | On the rotatable upper arms, there are also lubrication pads (b). Loosen the two screws on each rotatable upper arm. Then, dismount the brackets with the lubrication pads. |
| 5 | To fill the lubrication pads use one stroke of the lubrication gun directly on the pad.  NOTE: The lubrication process has to be done slowly. Ensure that the oil has been soaked up by the lubrication pad. |
| 6 | Before mounting the brackets, refer to [Instructions for Secured Screw Connections](InformationAboutReplacingPartsOfThe-4CEEAC48.html#InformationAboutReplacingPartsOfThe-4CEEAC48__InstructionsForGluedScrewConnection-FA3043C8).  Mount the two brackets with the lubrication pads again and insert the 4 screws. Tighten the screws with 1 Nm (8.85 lbf-in). |
