# Replacing the Drive Magnets of the Carrier

## Overview

A Lexium™ MC12 Basic Carrier has two drive magnets which are firmly mounted on a supporting plate. The drive magnets cannot be replaced individually. The supporting plate with the two magnets can only be replaced as a whole.

NOTE: Also refer to [Information About Replacing Parts of the Carrier](TPC_MLS-HWG_Info_Replacing_Parts_Carrier-8738A367.html#TPC_MLS-HWG_Info_Replacing_Parts_Carrier-8738A367).

However, on a Smart Carrier, you cannot replace the drive magnets.

| NOTICE | |
| --- | --- |
|  | INOPERABLE EQUIPMENT  * Do not disassemble the Smart Carrier to replace either the drive magnets or to service the electronics. * Replace the complete Smart Carrier or send the Smart Carrier for servicing to Schneider Electric.  Failure to follow these instructions can result in equipment damage. |

For servicing a Smart Carrier, contact your local Schneider Electric service representative.

## Replacing the Drive Magnets of the Carrier

NOTE: Exposed or uninstalled carriers must have the protective cover of the drive magnets installed at all times. The cover is only removed at the time of carrier installation.

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | NOTE: A Lexium™ MC12 carrier has two drive magnets (**2+3**) which are firmly mounted on a supporting plate (**1**).  First install the protective cover (**4**) of the drive magnets by sliding it onto the magnets if it is not already installed. Refer to [Removing the Protective Cover of the Drive Magnets](MountingCarrier-B69855A0.html#MountingCarrier-B69855A0__RemovingTheProtectiveCoverOfTheDriv-09755CCE). |
| 2 | Loosen and remove the three screws (M4x16 class 8.8) (**a**) with which the supporting plate is fixed, and the associated washers. |
| 3 | Remove the supporting plate (**b**) with the two drive magnets. |
| 4 | Insert a new supporting plate and fix the supporting plate with the associated screws. Make sure to add the associated washers as well. |
| 5 | Tighten the screws with a torque of 3 Nm (26.56 lbf-in). |
