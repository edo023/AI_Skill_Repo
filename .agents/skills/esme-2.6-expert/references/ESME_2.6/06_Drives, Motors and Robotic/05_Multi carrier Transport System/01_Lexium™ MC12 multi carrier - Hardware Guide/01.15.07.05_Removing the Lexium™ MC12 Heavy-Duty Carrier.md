# Removing the Lexium™ MC12 Heavy-Duty Carrier from the Lexium™ MC12 Heavy-Duty Guide Rail

Proceed as follows, to remove the Heavy-Duty carrier from the Heavy-Duty guide rail:

NOTE: Exposed or uninstalled carriers must have the protective cover of the drive magnets installed at all times. The cover is only removed at the time of carrier installation.

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Make sure the top guide rails are not closed. If the track is closed, dismount one curved top guide rail and replace it with a Heavy-Duty mounting rail or a top Heavy-Duty straight guide rail so that you will be able to remove the carriers from the track. |
| 2 | Grab the carrier with one hand at the top and with one hand at the bottom and do not place your fingers between the carrier and the segments. |
| 3 | Push the carrier off the track.  NOTE: To push the carrier off the track, you have to overcome the magnetic force. |
| 4 | When the carrier is off the track, install the protective cover on the drive magnets. |
