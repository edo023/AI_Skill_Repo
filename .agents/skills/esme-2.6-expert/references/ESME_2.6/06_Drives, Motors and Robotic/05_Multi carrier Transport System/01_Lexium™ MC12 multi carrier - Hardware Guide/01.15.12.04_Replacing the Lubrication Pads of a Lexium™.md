# Replacing the Lubrication Pads of a Lexium™ MC12 Heavy-Duty Carrier

## Overview

A Heavy-Duty carrier has six lubrication pads which must be replaced at the same time.

Refer to the [Maintenance Schedule](MaintenanceSchedule-F8F6FC28.html#MaintenanceSchedule-F8F6FC28) and the [Information About Replacing Parts of the Lexium™ MC12 Heavy-Duty Carrier](InformationAboutReplacingPartsOfThe-4CEEAC48.html#InformationAboutReplacingPartsOfThe-4CEEAC48).

## Replacing the Lubrication Pads

NOTE: Exposed or uninstalled carriers must have the protective cover of the drive magnets installed at all times. The cover is only removed at the time of carrier installation.

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Install the protective cover (7) of the drive magnets by sliding it onto the magnets if it is not already installed. Refer to [Removing the Protective Cover of the Drive Magnets](MountingCarrier-B69855A0.html#MountingCarrier-B69855A0__RemovingTheProtectiveCoverOfTheDriv-09755CCE).  NOTE: A Heavy-Duty carrier has six lubrication pads which must be replaced at the same time. Four are installed in the main body (1 to 4) and two are installed at the end of the top rotatable arms (5 and 6). |
| 2 | To replace the lubrication pads in the main body, the rotatable arms have to be dismounted (as explained here from step 2 to 5): First, dismount the encoder support (**a**) by removing the four screws of the support. |
| 3 | Remove the cover plate (b) at the top and the bottom of the Heavy-Duty carrier by removing the six screws at each plate. |
| 4 | Under the cover plate (**b**) you can find the heads of the hex bolts (**c**) which hold the rotatable arms (**d**). Unscrew the two hex bolts (**c**) to dismount the rotatable arms (d). |
| 5 | The lubrication pads in the main body are sitting behind the mounting plate (**e**). Loosen and remove the six screws at the mounting plate and dismount the mounting plate (**e**). |
| 6 | Replace the lubrication pads (**f**) on the mounting plate. |
| 7 | Before mounting the mounting plate with the lubrication pads, refer to [Instructions for Secured Screw Connections](InformationAboutReplacingPartsOfThe-4CEEAC48.html#InformationAboutReplacingPartsOfThe-4CEEAC48__InstructionsForGluedScrewConnection-FA3043C8).  Screw the mounting plate (**e**) together with the lubrication pads to the main body with 1 Nm (8.9 lbf-in). |
| 8 | For the change of the lubrication pads that are mounted at the end of the rotatable arms, loosen the two screws at each bracket (**g**). |
| 9 | Remove the bracket (**g**) together with the lubrication pad. |
| 10 | Replace the lubrication pads from the brackets. |
| 11 | Before mounting the brackets, refer to [Instructions for Secured Screw Connections](InformationAboutReplacingPartsOfThe-4CEEAC48.html#InformationAboutReplacingPartsOfThe-4CEEAC48__InstructionsForGluedScrewConnection-FA3043C8).  Screw the brackets (**g**) again to the rotatable arms (**d**) with 1 Nm (8.9 lbf-in). |
| 12 | Before mounting the hex bolts to the rotatable arms, refer to [Instructions for Secured Screw Connections](InformationAboutReplacingPartsOfThe-4CEEAC48.html#InformationAboutReplacingPartsOfThe-4CEEAC48__InstructionsForGluedScrewConnection-FA3043C8).  Place the rotatable arms in position and push the hex bolts through the bearings (**h**) and fasten the hex bolts loosely in the threads of the rotatable arms. |
| 13 | Tighten the hex bolts (**c**) in the threads of the rotatable arms with 5 Nm (44.3 lbf-in). |
| 14 | Repeat step 4 to 7 and 12 to 13 to replace the lubrication pads in the lower main body. |
| 15 | Before mounting the cover plates, refer to [Instructions for Secured Screw Connections](InformationAboutReplacingPartsOfThe-4CEEAC48.html#InformationAboutReplacingPartsOfThe-4CEEAC48__InstructionsForGluedScrewConnection-FA3043C8).  Place the cover plates (**b**) at the top and the bottom of the Heavy-Duty carrier and mount them with their six screws. Tighten the screws with 2.5 Nm (22.1 lbf-in). |
| 16 | Lubricate the Heavy-Duty carrier. Refer to [Lubricating the Lexium™ MC12 Heavy-Duty Carrier](LubricatingHDCarrier-F8F68D40.html#LubricatingHDCarrier-F8F68D40). |
| 17 | Before mounting the encoder support, refer to [Instructions for Secured Screw Connections](InformationAboutReplacingPartsOfThe-4CEEAC48.html#InformationAboutReplacingPartsOfThe-4CEEAC48__InstructionsForGluedScrewConnection-FA3043C8).  Mount the encoder support with its four screws. Tighten the screws with 1.0 Nm (8.9 lbf-in). |
