# Maintenance

## Overview

The SFO (Safe Force Off) function has been designed for a defined lifetime that does not require to verify the safety-related function, nor any specific maintenance requirements. For the defined lifetime, refer to [Safety Standards](SafetyStandards-A2896F3B.html#SafetyStandards-A2896F3B).

After this lifetime has elapsed, a statement about the SFO function cannot be made due to the aging of the component. If you want to ensure the functional safety after this period, you need to replace the device that includes the safety function.

NOTE: Subject the product to a complete function test after replacement.
