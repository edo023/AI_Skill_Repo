# Maintenance Schedule

Perform the following maintenance tasks after delivery or long storage.

| Interval(1) | Action |
| --- | --- |
| After delivery, every 1000 km (621 miles) or within the first two months of service life | Verify the Heavy-Duty carriers by visual inspection for any damage or missing parts, especially for moving parts and parts at risk for collisions such as mounting elements |
| After ≥ two years of storage | Verify the lubrication pads of the Heavy-Duty carriers and replace them if necessary. Refer to [Replacing Parts](ReplacingParts-F6F95615.html#ReplacingParts-F6F95615). |
| (1) The distances referred to are for one Heavy-Duty carrier. The performance of your system may differ. | |

Perform the following maintenance tasks within the stated intervals.

NOTE: The intervals in the following table are guidelines. The intervals can be adjusted depending on your application and the environmental conditions (track length, number of Heavy-Duty carriers, motion profile, temperature, etc.).

| Interval(1) | Action |
| --- | --- |
| Every 1000 km (621 miles) of operation | * Clean the Lexium™ MC12 Heavy-Duty guide system if necessary. Refer to [Cleaning](TPC_MLS-HWG_Cleaning-86471E69.html#TPC_MLS-HWG_Cleaning-86471E69). * Verify that the floating bearing of the lower rollers of the Heavy-Duty carrier moves freely in axial direction. Clean if necessary and make the rollers run smoothly. Refer to [Cleaning the Carrier Rollers](TPC_MLS-HWG_Cleaning-86471E69.html#TPC_MLS-HWG_Cleaning-86471E69__CleaningTheCarrierRollers-D984BF37). * Verify the lubrication pads of the Heavy-Duty carriers for wear and replace them if necessary. Refer to [Replacing Parts](ReplacingParts-F6F95615.html#ReplacingParts-F6F95615). |
| Every 1000 km (621 miles)... 8000 km (4970 miles)(2) | Lubricate the Heavy-Duty carriers depending on the application. Refer to [Lubricating the Lexium™ MC12 Heavy-Duty Carrier](LubricatingHDCarrier-F8F68D40.html#LubricatingHDCarrier-F8F68D40). As examples, two test cases were conducted that required refilling the lubricant reservoirs.   * Test application for 1000 km (621 miles): 1.5 m (59 in.) track length, 1 carrier, laboratory environment at 35 °C (95 °F) * Test application for 8000 km (4970 miles): 5.2 m (205 in.) track length, 5 carriers, laboratory environment at 25 °C (77 °F) |
| Every 4000 km (2485 miles) of operation | * Clean the Lexium™ MC12 Heavy-Duty guide system if necessary. Refer to [Cleaning](TPC_MLS-HWG_Cleaning-86471E69.html#TPC_MLS-HWG_Cleaning-86471E69).   + Verify the covers of the segments and the drive magnets of the Heavy-Duty carriers for dirt and magnetic abrasion. Clean the covers and the magnets and remove the magnetic abrasion if necessary. Refer to [Removing Particles from the Magnets](TPC_MLS-HWG_Cleaning-86471E69.html#TPC_MLS-HWG_Cleaning-86471E69__RemovingMagneticAbrasionFromTheMagn-8CEED4F9).   + Verify the Heavy-Duty carrier rollers for dirt and clean if necessary.   + Verify the area of encoder magnets of the Heavy-Duty carriers for dirt, and clean if necessary.   + Verify the rails for dirt and clean if necessary. * Remove the Lexium™ MC12 Heavy-Duty carriers from the guide rail, refer to [Removing the Lexium™ MC12 Heavy-Duty Carrier from the Lexium™ MC12 Heavy-Duty Guide Rail](RemoveHDCarrierFromRail-F8F41462.html#RemoveHDCarrierFromRail-F8F41462). Verify it by visual inspection for any damage or missing parts, especially moving parts and parts at risk for collisions such as mounting elements.   + Verify the drive magnets of the Heavy-Duty carriers for damage. The drive magnets cannot be replaced individually. Replace the plate with the two magnets as a whole if necessary. Refer to [Replacing the Drive Magnets of the Carrier](ReplacePermanentMagnets-A62E97D3.html#ReplacePermanentMagnets-A62E97D3).   + Verify the rails for wear or damage and replace if necessary. * Reinstall the Heavy-Duty carriers and the guide rails and mount them according to the descriptions in [Mechanical Installation](MechanicalInstallation-F6F899A6.html#MechanicalInstallation-F6F899A6). |
| Every 10,000 km (6214 miles) of operation | Verify the Heavy-Duty carrier rollers for wear and replace the Heavy-Duty carrier rollers if necessary. Refer to [Replacing Parts](ReplacingParts-F6F95615.html#ReplacingParts-F6F95615). |
| (1) The distances referred to are for one Heavy-Duty carrier. The performance of your system may differ.  (2) Not required for Lexium™ MC12 Heavy-Duty tracks with automated lubrication. For a track with automated lubrication, ensure a sufficient supply of lubricant to the lubricant outlets. | |

Perform the following maintenance tasks if your Heavy-Duty carriers have exceptional movement profiles:

| Movement profile | Action |
| --- | --- |
| Reversing movements in which the Heavy-Duty carriers travel a distance of less than 200 mm (7.88 in). | If a distance of less than 200 mm (7.88 in) is travelled, perform a lubrication run after 20,000 traverse cycles or earlier. |
