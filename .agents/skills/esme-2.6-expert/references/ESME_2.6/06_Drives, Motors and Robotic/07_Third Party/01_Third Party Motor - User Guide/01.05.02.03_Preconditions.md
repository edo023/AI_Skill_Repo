Preconditions

Overview

As of PacDrive 3 V3.1 SP1 the following linear axis objects are available in EcoStruxure Machine Expert for Lexium 52 and Lexium 62:

oLexium 52 Linear Drive

oLexium 62 Linear Drive

The period length of the sine-cosine signals of the encoder must not exceed 65.5 mm (2.57 in.). For programming the electronic motor nameplate, at least Version V1.0.6.x of the EcoStruxure Machine Expert library UserMotorTypePlate has to be used.
