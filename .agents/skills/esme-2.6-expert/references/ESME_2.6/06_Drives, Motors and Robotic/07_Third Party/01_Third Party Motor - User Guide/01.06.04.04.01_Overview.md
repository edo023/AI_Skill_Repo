To do so, use the MTP.FC\_MotorDataFileCreate function from the library UserMotorTypePlate:

MTP.FC\_MotorDataFileCreate(  
    i\_sFilename:= 'ide0:/SEW\_ DRS71S4.mdx',  
    i\_stUserMotorData:= stUserMotorData, );

The file is saved on the flash disk / SD card of the controller.
