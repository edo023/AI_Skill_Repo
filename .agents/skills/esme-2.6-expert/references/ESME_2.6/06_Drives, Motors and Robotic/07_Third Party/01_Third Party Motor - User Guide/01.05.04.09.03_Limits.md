# Limits

Limits

The following limits are to be seen as the maximum values that can be processed by the system:

oThe maximum velocity of linear motors is limited to 12 m/s (39.37 ft/s).

oThe maximum distance for linear motors is limited to 130 m (426.50 ft.).

oThe maximum encoder period length (see UserMotorTypePlate parameter uiPeriodLength) is 40000 μm.
