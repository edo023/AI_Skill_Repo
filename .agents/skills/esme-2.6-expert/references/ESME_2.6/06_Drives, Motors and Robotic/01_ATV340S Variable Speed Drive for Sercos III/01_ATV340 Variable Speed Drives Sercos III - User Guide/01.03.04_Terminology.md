# Terminology

Terminology

The technical terms, terminology, and the corresponding descriptions in this manual normally use the terms or definitions in the relevant standards.

In the area of drive systems this includes, but is not limited to, terms such as error, error message, failure, fault, fault reset, protection, safe state, safety function, warning, warning message, and so on.

Among others, these standards include:

oIEC 61800 series: Adjustable speed electrical power drive systems

oIEC 61508 Ed.2 series: Functional safety of electrical/electronic/programmable electronic safety-related

oEN 954-1 Safety of machinery - Safety related parts of control systems

oISO 13849-1 & 2 Safety of machinery - Safety related parts of control systems

oIEC 61158 series: Industrial communication networks - Fieldbus specifications

oIEC 61784 series: Industrial communication networks - Profiles

oIEC 60204-1: Safety of machinery - Electrical equipment of machines – Part 1: General requirements

In addition, the term zone of operation is used in conjunction with the description of specific hazards, and is defined as it is for a hazard zone or danger zone in the EC Machinery Directive (2006/42/EC) and in ISO 12100-1.

Also see the glossary at the end of this manual.
