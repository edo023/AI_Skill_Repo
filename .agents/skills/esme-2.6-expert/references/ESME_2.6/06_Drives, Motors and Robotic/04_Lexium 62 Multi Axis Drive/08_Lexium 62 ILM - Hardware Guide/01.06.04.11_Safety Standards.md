# Safety Standards

## Overview

The designated safety functions have been designed and tested for functional safety according to the following standards

* IEC 61508:2010
* IEC 61800-5-2:2016
* EN ISO 13849-1:2015
* IEC 62061:2005

An independent assessment was performed by TÜV Nord.

According to the above listed standards, the figures for the devices for using the Inverter Enable function are as follows:
