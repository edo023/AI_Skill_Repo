# Cleaning

## Cleaning the Lexium 62 ILM Integrated Servo Drive

Care must be taken with cleaning products as some active agents may have deleterious effects on plastics and stainless steel welds.

| NOTICE | |
| --- | --- |
|  | CORROSION CAUSED BY CLEANING AGENTS  * Before using a cleaning agent, carry out a compatibility test in relation to the cleaning agent and the component affected. * Do not use alkaline detergent. * Do not use any chloride-containing cleaning agents. * Do not use any sulphuric acid containing detergent.  Failure to follow these instructions can result in equipment damage. |

For more information on the material properties of your component, refer to [*Technical Data*](D-SE-0049402.html#D-SE-0049402).

NOTE: The standard cooling method of the motor is by natural convection. Therefore, keep the motor surfaces free from dirt.
