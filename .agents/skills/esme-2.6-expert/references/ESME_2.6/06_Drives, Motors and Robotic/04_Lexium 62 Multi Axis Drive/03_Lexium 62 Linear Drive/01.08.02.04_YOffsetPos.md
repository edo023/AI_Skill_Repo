Functional Description

The parameter represents the position to which the [YOffsetGenerator](../YOffsetGenerator_2/YOffsetGenerator_2-1.htm#XREF_D_SE_0071847_1) adds to the [reference position](RefActualValues-2.htm#XREF_D_SE_0071490_1) of the motion generators.
