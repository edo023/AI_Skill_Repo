# YOffset Generator

## General

Position generator for easily generating Y offsets for an axis. For example, this is required to perform axis correction using *[Touchprobe](../../../../../api/crossBook?lang=en-US&virtualBookName=PD.Lib.SystemInterface&topicID=D_SE_0085588)* (measured input). This generator works independently of other [motion generators](../../../../../api/crossBook?lang=en-US&virtualBookName=D-SE-0071489.html#D-SE-0071489) on the axis. For instance, while a *[MultiCam()](../../PD.Lib.PacDriveLib&topicID=D_SE_0087312)* function block is running, a correction is being performed simultaneously through the YOffset generator.

| CAUTION | |
| --- | --- |
|  | INVALID AXIS POSITION  Disable the YOffset Generator in the program initialization if you are using absolute encoder homing (with or without retain).  Failure to follow these instructions can result in injury or equipment damage. |
