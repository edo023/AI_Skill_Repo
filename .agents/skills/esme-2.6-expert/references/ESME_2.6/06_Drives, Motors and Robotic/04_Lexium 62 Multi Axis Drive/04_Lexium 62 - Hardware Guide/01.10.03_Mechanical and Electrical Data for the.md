NOTE: Due to the higher pulse energy of the braking resistor in hardware revision RS 02, the precharge of the system may be delayed up to 50 seconds, depending on the load state or the estimated worst-case load state of the braking resistor. In particular, this longer delay time can be noticed in situations where the load of the braking resistor is undefined to the system (for example after turning on the 24 V supply of the device or after a forced discharge of the DC Bus).
