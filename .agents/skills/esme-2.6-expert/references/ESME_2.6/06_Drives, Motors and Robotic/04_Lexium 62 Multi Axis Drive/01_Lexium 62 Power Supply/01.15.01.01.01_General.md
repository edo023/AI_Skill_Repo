# General

## Use

With the help of the logger, the log entries of drives can be collected and displayed at a central location and saved in the Logic Builder project at a later time. The log entries are displayed in the register card Logger of the respective drive.

* The register card Logger is displayed for every drive between the register cards Configuration and Information.
* The Logger is activated via a button ([Load logger]) on the register card Logger or via the corresponding [Entry in the context menu](D-SE-0072003.html#D-SE-0072003). During the loading procedure, a progress bar shows the status bar and the status information.
* The individual log entries are [color-coded](D-SE-0071999.html#D-SE-0071999) according to their respective [diagnostic class](D-SE-0071999.html#D-SE-0071999).
* The online help is opened via [F1] or the corresponding [entry in the context menu](D-SE-0072006.html#D-SE-0072006). If a log entry is selected, then the corresponding online help is displayed, based on the diagnostic number. If no log entry is selected, then the general summary of the logger is displayed in the online help.
* The Logger file can be [saved](D-SE-0072005.html#D-SE-0072005) in the context menu of the file system via entries or [loaded](D-SE-0072004.html#D-SE-0072004) from the file system.
