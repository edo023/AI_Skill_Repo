# GroupReady

## General

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| Type | AD |
| Devices supporting the parameter | Lexium LXM62 Power Supply |
| Traceable | Yes |

## Functional Description

Indicates whether the group formed by the power supply and the connected servo drives is ready for task processing.

| Value | Data type | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- |
| no / 0 | BOOL | The group formed by the power supply and the connected drives is not ready for process tasks. |
| yes / 1 | BOOL | The group formed by the power supply and the connected drives is ready for process tasks.   * The parameter *[AxisReady](../../../../../api/crossBook?lang=en-US&virtualBookName=PD.Parameter.LXM62Drive&topicID=D_SE_0071454)* of the connected servo drives is yes / 1. * At least one real servo drive is connected to the power supply. * GroupState = Group\_Active |
