| Failure to follow these instructions can result in death, serious injury, or equipment damage. |

The Rack iPC unit is designed to use M4 screws. These screws are not for mounting the Rack iPC.They are to prevent the Rack iPC from sliding out of the cabinet:

![G-SE-0030747.1.gif-high.gif](images/G-SE-0030747.1.gif-high.gif)

Important Mounting Information

o[Environmental Characteristics](../iPC_-_Characteristics/iPC_-_Characteristics-3.htm#XREF_D_SE_0033369_1).

oThe Rack iPC is permitted for operation in closed rooms only.

oThe Rack iPC cannot be situated in direct sunlight.

oThe Rack iPC vent holes must not be covered.

oWhen mounting the Rack iPC, adhere to the allowable mounting angle.

oBe sure that the wall or switching cabinet can support a minimum 4 times the total weight of the Rack iPC.

oWhen connecting certain cable types (DVI, USB, and so on), keep the flex radius of the cable in mind.

Mounting Angle

The Rack iPC unit must be mounted on 19” rack.
