# Introduction

Introduction

When installing PCI/PCIe cards on board, the operating temperature is limited to 45 °C (113 °F). When installing a single PCI/PCIe card, the maximum power consumption is 10 W. When installing two PCI/PCIe cards, the maximum power consumption is 12 W as the sum of the two cards (however, the maximum power consumption per card is 10 W). Either when installing one card or two cards, if the total power consumption exceeds 6 W, the fan kit (HMIYBFKT4BM11) is required.

The fan kit (HMIYBFKT4BM11) is mounted on the Box iPC 4-Slot only.

Before installing a fan kit, shut down Windows in an orderly fashion and remove all power from the device.
