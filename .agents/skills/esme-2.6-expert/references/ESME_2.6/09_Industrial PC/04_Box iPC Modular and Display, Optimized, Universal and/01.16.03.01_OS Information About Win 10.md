# OS Information About Win 10

OS Information About Win 10

Windows 10 have two SKU (stock-keeping unit):

oHMI SKU (Standard System monitor).

o IIoT SKU (Pre-install Node-Red instead of Standard System monitor). Refer to [System Monitor](../iPC_-_System_Monitor_and_IIoT_Monitor/iPC_-_System_Monitor_and_IIoT_Monitor-1.htm#XREF_D_SE_0082782_1) or [IIoT and Cyber Security](../Box_PC_-_IIoT_Box_Project_Cyber_Security/Box_PC_-_IIoT_Box_Project_Cyber_Security-1.htm#XREF_D_SE_0067651_1) for more detail function. If you replace from HMI version to IIoT version, you can get the Node-Red installer from Schneider Electric Web side ([www.schneider-electric.com](www.schneider-electric.com .)).
