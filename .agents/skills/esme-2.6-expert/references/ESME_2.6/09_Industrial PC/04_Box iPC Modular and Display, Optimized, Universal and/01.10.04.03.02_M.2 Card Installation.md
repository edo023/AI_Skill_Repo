# M.2 Card Installation

M.2 Card Installation

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Disconnect the power cord to the Box iPC. |
| 2 | Touch the housing or ground connection (not the power supply) to discharge any electrostatic charge from your body. |
| 3 | Unscrew the four screws from the cover:  G-SE-0057251.2.gif-high.gif |
| 4 | Insert the M.2 card into the expansion card connector and fasten it with one screw:  G-SE-0058196.1.gif-high.gif    1   Expansion card connector  2   M.2 card  3   Screw size M2 (included in accessory Box iPC)  NOTE: The recommended torque to tighten these screws is 0.5 Nm (4.5 lb-in). |
| 5 | Replace the cover and fasten it with four screws. |
