# Connections

Connections

This interface is used to connect Box iPC to remote equipment, via a cable. The connector is a D-Sub 9-pin plug connector.

If you use a long PLC cable to connect to the Box iPC, the cable can be at an electrical potential that is different from the electrical potential of the panel, even if both are connected to ground.

The table shows the D-Sub 9-pin assignments:

| Pin | Assignment | Description | D-Sub 9-pin plug female connector |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | – | – | G-SE-0063956.1.gif-high.gif |
| 2 | – | – |
| 3 | RxD/TxD-P | Receive/Send Data-P  connection B plug |
| 4 | – | – |
| 5 | GND | Reference potential |
| 6 | VP | Positive supply voltage |
| 7 | – | – |
| 8 | RxD/TxD-N | Receive/Send Data-N  connection A plug |
| 9 | – | – |
