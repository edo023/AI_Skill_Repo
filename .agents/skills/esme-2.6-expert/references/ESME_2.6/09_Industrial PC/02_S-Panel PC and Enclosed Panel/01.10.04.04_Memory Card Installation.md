Memory Card Installation

Introduction

The Panel PC controller operating system views the CFast card as a hard disk. Proper handling and care of the CFast card helps extend the life of the card. Familiarize yourself with the card before attempting insertion or removal of the card.

Before installing or removing a card, shut down Windows® in an orderly fashion and remove all power from the device.

| --- | --- |
| 1 | Disconnect the power cord to the S-Panel PC. |
| 2 | Touch the housing or ground connection (not the power supply) to discharge any electrostatic charge from your body. |
