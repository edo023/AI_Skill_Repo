# Installation of the S-Panel PC

Installation of the S-Panel PC

The installation gasket and the installation fasteners are required for the easy installation of the S-Panel PC. The panel mounting process of the easy installation can be completed by one person.

NOTE: For easy installation of the S-Panel PC, the suggested mounting panel thickness can be up to 2 mm (0.079 in).

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Check that the gasket is correctly attached to the S-Panel PC.  NOTE: When checking the gasket, avoid contact with the sharp edges of the S-Panel PC frame, and insert the gasket completely into its groove. |
| 2 | Release the 2 screws at the bottom of the S-Panel PC:  G-SE-0042004.1.gif-high.gif |
| 3 | Loosen the 2 cross-slotted screws from the top of the S-Panel PC to raise the snap hook:  G-SE-0042003.1.gif-high.gif      Note:  o1 snap hook for the W10”  o2 snap hook for the W15” |
| 4 | Install the S-Panel PC in the panel opening and push it into the wall. The snap hook holds the S-Panel PC to the wall:  G-SE-0038233.3.gif-high.gif |
| 5 | Insert the installation fasteners securely into the slots at the top, bottom, left, and right side of the S-Panel PC:  G-SE-0041985.1.gif-high.gif      Note:  o8 installation fasteners for the W10”  o10 installation fasteners for the W15” |
| 6 | Insert each fastener in its corresponding slot and pull the fastener back until it is flush with the rear of the fastener hole:  G-SE-0038736.1.gif-high.gif      G-SE-0038737.1.gif-high.gif |
| 7 | Tighten each of the cross-slotted fastener screws, and fasten the S-Panel PC in place:  G-SE-0038738.1.gif-high.gif      NOTE: To ensure a high degree of moisture resistance, use a torque of 0.5 Nm (4.5 lb-in). |
| 8 | The angle of the unit is tilted no more than the amount allowed by the mounting orientation requirements. |
