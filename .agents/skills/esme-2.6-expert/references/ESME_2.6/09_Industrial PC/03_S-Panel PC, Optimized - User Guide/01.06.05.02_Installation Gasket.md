# Installation Gasket

Installation Gasket

The gasket is required to meet the protection ratings (IP••/Type 4X indoor) of the S-Panel PC. It provides additional protection from vibration.

NOTE: IP••/Type 4X indoor or Type 4 is not part of UL certification.
