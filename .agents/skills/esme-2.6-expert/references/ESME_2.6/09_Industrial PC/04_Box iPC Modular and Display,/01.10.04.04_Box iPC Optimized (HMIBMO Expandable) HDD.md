# Box iPC Optimized (HMIBMO Expandable) HDD/SSD Drive Installation

![](images/logo.png)

Box iPC Optimized (HMIBMO Expandable) HDD/SSD Drive Installation

Overview

The Box iPC supports three types of SATA devices and two SATA ports.The table shows the SATA device configuration:

| SATA port | SATA device | SATA speed |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Port 1 | HDD/SSD | 6 Gb/s; 3 Gb/s; 1.5 Gb/s |
| Port 2 | M.2 |

NOTE: Remove all power before attempting this procedure.

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Disconnect the power cord to the Box iPC. |
| 2 | Touch the housing or ground connection (not the power supply) to discharge any electrostatic charge from your body. |
| 3 | Remove the four screws of the cover and remove it:  G-SE-0058397.2.gif-high.gif |
| 4 | Connect the SATA cables to the Box iPC:  G-SE-0057252.1.gif-high.gif    1   HDD/SSD adapter  2   SATA power cable  3   SATA signal cable  NOTE: The SATA signal cable is soft to avoid cable push up issue after it is connected. |
| 5 | Screw the HDD/SSD adapter (HMIYBADHDDBMO1) on the Box iPC (the screws are in the accessory):  G-SE-0057253.2.gif-high.gif      NOTE: The shock absorbers protect against hard drive vibrations. |
| 6 | Fix the HDD/SSD with the HDD/SSD case and screw it to fix:  G-SE-0058371.1.gif-high.gif |
| 7 | Slide the HDD/SSD case into the HDD/SSD adapter and screw it to fix:  G-SE-0057254.2.gif-high.gif |
| 8 | Replace the cover. Secure the cover using the four screws.  NOTE: The recommended torque to tighten these screws is 0.5 Nm (4.5 lb-in). |
