# Fan and Filter Cover

Fan and Filter Cover

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Disconnect the power supply to the Magelis Rack iPC. |
| 2 | Touch the housing or ground connection (not the power supply) to discharge any electrostatic charge from your body. |
| 3 | Loosen two screws on the rear of the top cover for the Rack iPC Performance and Universal.  Loosen five screws on the rear and both sides of the top cover for the Rack iPC Optimized. |
| 4 | Slide the top cover backwards and then lift it up Rack iPC Optimized:  G-SE-0030844.1.gif-high.gif      Slide the top cover backwards and then lift it up for the Rack iPC Performance and Universal:  G-SE-0032115.1.gif-high.gif |
| 5 | Press in the marked latching mechanism as you pull out the filter cover for the Rack iPC Optimized and Universal:  G-SE-0032129.1.gif-high.gif      1   Take out the fan bracket  2   Replacing system fan  3   Removing the filters |
| 6 | Replacing the internal fan for the Rack iPC Universal:  G-SE-0032130.1.gif-high.gif      1   Replacing the fan behind the SAS/SATA HDD backplane  2   Remove the fan from the plastic bracket |
| 7 | Replacing the system cooling fan for the Rack iPC Performance:  G-SE-0032128.1.gif-high.gif      1   Replacing the system cooling fan (without removing the top cover)  2   Replacing HDD fan  3   Removing the filters |
| 8 | Reinstall the top cover and tighten the screws. |
