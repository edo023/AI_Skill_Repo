USB Ports (USB12, USB56, USB34, USB78, USB910, USB1112, USB13, USB14)

Rack iPC Performance provides up 14 x USB ports. USB5...14 are USB 2.0 ports supporting transmission rates up to 480 MB/s. USB1...4 are USB 3.0 ports supporting transmission rate up to 5 Gbps.These ports support plug & play and hot swapping for up to 127 external devices and are able to be disabled in BIOS menu. If all USB ports are used, switch the USB power to 5 V instead of five VSB.
