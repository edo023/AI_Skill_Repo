# Auxiliary 8-Pin Power Connector (ATX8P_P1)

Auxiliary 8-Pin Power Connector (ATX8P\_P1)

The ATX8P\_P1 is an 8-pin connector. For a fully configured system, use a power supply unit (PSU) that complies with ATX 12 V specification 2.0 (or later version). Do not forget to connect the 8-pin EATX12 V power plug; otherwise, the system does not restart.
