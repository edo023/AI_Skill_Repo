# USB Power Switch

USB Power Switch

The Rack iPC contains a jumper that can support USB/KBMS power source from 5 Vsb or 5 V. The default setting is 1-2 closed which is supporting USB stand-by power under S5. When the jumper is 2-3 closed, the USB/KBMS power source is switched to 5 V. If you want to disable USB stand-by power under S5, and under 2-3 closed, it does not support S3 and S4 mode.
