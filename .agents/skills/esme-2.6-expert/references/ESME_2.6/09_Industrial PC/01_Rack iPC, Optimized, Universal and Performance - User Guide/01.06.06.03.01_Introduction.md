# Introduction

Introduction

In certain cases, it may be necessary to reinstall the operating system.

Precautions to take:

oKeep static-producing materials (plastic, upholstery, carpeting) out of the immediate workpace.

oDo not remove ESD-sensitive components from their anti-static bags until you are ready to install them.

oWhen handling static-sensitive components, wear a properly grounded wrist strap (or equivalent).

oAvoid contact with exposed conductors and component leads.
