Compliance Standards

oUnited States:

oFederal Communications Commission, FCC Part 15, Class  A

oEurope: CE

o2014/35/EU Low Voltage Directive, based on IEC 60950 or IEC 61010-2-201

o2014/30/EU EMC Directive, class A, based on IEC 61006-2 and IEC 61006-4

oAustralia:

oStandard AS/NZS CISPR11
