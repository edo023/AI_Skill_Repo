# HDD/SSD Drive Installation

HDD/SSD Drive Installation

|  |
| --- |
| NOTICE |
| ELECTROSTATIC DISCHARGE |
| Take the necessary protective measures against electrostatic discharge before attempting to remove the Magelis Industrial PC cover. |
| Failure to follow these instructions can result in equipment damage. |

|  |
| --- |
| Caution_Color.gifCAUTION |
| OVERTORQUE AND LOOSE HARDWARE |
| oDo not exert more than 0.5 Nm (4.5 lb-in) of torque when tightening the installation fastener, enclosure, accessory, or terminal block screws. Tightening the screws with excessive force can damage the installation fastener.  oWhen fastening or removing screws, ensure that they do not fall inside the Magelis Industrial PC chassis. |
| Failure to follow these instructions can result in injury or equipment damage. |

NOTE: Be sure to remove the power before attempting this procedure.

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Disconnect the power cord of the S-Panel PC. |
| 2 | Touch the housing or ground connection (not the power supply) to discharge any electrostatic charge from your body. |
| 3 | Remove the 2 screws from the rear cover of the S-Panel PC:  G-SE-0038584.1.gif-high.gif |
| 4 | Install the 2.5" SATA HDD/SSD on the HDD/SSD bracket. Fasten the 4 screws on the side of HDD/SSD bracket (the screws are in the accessory box):  G-SE-0038796.2.gif-high.gif |
| 5 | Install the dampers in the brackets. Connect the HDD/SSD into the SATA connector. Install it in the S-Panel PC and fasten it with the 4 screws:  G-SE-0038798.2.gif-high.gif |
| 6 | Replace the rear cover and fasten with the 2 screws for the S-Panel PC:  G-SE-0038584.1.gif-high.gif      NOTE: The recommended torque to tighten these screws is 0.5 Nm (4.5 lb-in). |

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Disconnect the power cord of the Enclosed PC. |
| 2 | Touch the housing or ground connection (not the power supply) to discharge any electrostatic charge from your body. |
| 3 | Remove the 4 screws from the rear cover of the Enclosed PC:  G-SE-0042619.1.gif-high.gif |
| 4 | Remove the HDD/SSD bracket from the Enclosed PC:  G-SE-0045843.1.gif-high.gif |
| 5 | Install the 2.5" SATA HDD/SSD on the HDD/SSD bracket. Fasten the 4 screws on the side of HDD/SSD bracket (the screws are in the accessory box):  G-SE-0045842.1.gif-high.gif |
| 6 | Connect the HDD/SSD into the SATA connector. Install the dampers in the brackets. Install the HDD/SSD in the Enclosed PC and fasten it with the 4 screws:  G-SE-0042618.1.gif-high.gif |
| 7 | Replace the rear cover and fasten with the 4 screws for the Enclosed PC:  G-SE-0042619.1.gif-high.gif      NOTE: The recommended torque to tighten these screws is 0.5 Nm (4.5 lb-in). |
