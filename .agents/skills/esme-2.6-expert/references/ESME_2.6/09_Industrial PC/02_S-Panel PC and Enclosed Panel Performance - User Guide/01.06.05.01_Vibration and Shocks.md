# Vibration and Shocks

Vibration and Shocks

Take extra care with respect to vibration levels when installing or moving the S-Panel PC and the Enclosed PC. If you move the S-Panel PC and the Enclosed PC while it is installed in a rack equipped with caster wheels, it may undergo excessive shock and vibration.
