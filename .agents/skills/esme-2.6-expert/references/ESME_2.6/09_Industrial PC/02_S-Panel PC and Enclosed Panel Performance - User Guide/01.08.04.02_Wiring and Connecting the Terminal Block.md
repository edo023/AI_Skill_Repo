# Wiring and Connecting the Terminal Block

Wiring and Connecting the Terminal Block

The table below describes how to connect the power cord to the DC terminal block of the S-Panel PC:

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Remove all power from the S-Panel PC and confirm that the DC power supply has been disconnected from its power source. |
| 2 | Remove the terminal block from the power connector and connect the power cord to the terminal block as shown below:  The figure shows the power cord to the DC terminal block of the S-Panel PC:  G-SE-0038794.4.gif-high.gif      Use copper wire rated for 75 °C (167 °F) with a section of 0.75 to 2.5 mm2 (AWG 18 to AWG 14) and use 2.5 mm2 wire to make the ground connection. |
| 3 | Place the terminal block in the power connector and tighten the screws.  The figure shows the power cord to the DC terminal block of the S-Panel PC:  G-SE-0038795.2.gif-high.gif      NOTE: The recommended torque to tighten these screws is 0.5 Nm (4.5 lb-in). |
