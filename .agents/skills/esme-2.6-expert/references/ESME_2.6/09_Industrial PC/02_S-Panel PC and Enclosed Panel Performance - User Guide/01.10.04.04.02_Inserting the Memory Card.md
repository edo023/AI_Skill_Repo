# Inserting the Memory Card

Inserting the Memory Card

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Disconnect the power cord to the S-Panel PC. |
| 2 | Touch the housing or ground connection (not the power supply) to discharge any electrostatic charge from your body. |
| 3 | Remove the two screws from the rear cover and remove it:  G-SE-0038584.1.gif-high.gif |
| 4 | Insert the CFast card firmly into the card slot:  G-SE-0043250.1.gif-high.gif |
| 5 | Replace the rear cover and fasten it with the screws:  G-SE-0038584.1.gif-high.gif      NOTE: The recommended torque to tighten these screws is 0.5 Nm (4.5 lb-in). |
