# Installing the AUX Connector

Installing the AUX Connector

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Remove 7 mm (.28 in.) of the vinyl cover of each of the wires. |
| 2 | If using stranded wire, twist the ends. Tinning the ends with solder reduces risk of fraying and ensures good electrical transfer. |
| 3 | Insert a driver into the square-shaped hole. |
| 4 | Insert the cable into the circular-shaped hole and pull out the driver. The cable is then fixed.  G-SA-0036615.3.gif-high.gif |
| 5 | Insert the AUX connector into the AUX input/output and sound output interface. |
