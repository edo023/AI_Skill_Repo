# Introduction

Introduction

This interface is used to connect the XBT GT2000/4000/5000/6000/7000 and XBT GK series to the remote equipment, via RS-485 cable. The connector used is a RJ45-8-pin type connector.

The unit’s Serial Port is not isolated. The SG (signal ground) and FG (frame ground) terminals are connected insde the unit.
