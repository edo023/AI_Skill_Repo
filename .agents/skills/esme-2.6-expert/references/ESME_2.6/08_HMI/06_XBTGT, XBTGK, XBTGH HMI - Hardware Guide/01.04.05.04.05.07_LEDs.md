# LEDs

LEDs

Each function key has a corresponding LED. The behavior of the LEDs are programmed at the application level. For example, a LED can start blinking to highlight possible choices or indicate ongoing action as determined by the PLC.

LEDs can light up in one of three colors: orange, red, and green.

LEDs can be in one of four states: off, steady on, blinking (about 1 Hz), fast blinking (>1 Hz).
