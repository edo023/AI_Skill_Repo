# Compliance Standards for XBT GT and XBT GK Series

Compliance Standards for XBT GT and XBT GK Series

Europe:

oDirective 2006/95/EC (Low voltage)

oDirective 2004/108/EC (EMC).

The units are CE marked. For detailed information, contact your local distributor.

oProgrammable Controllers: EN/IEC 61131-2

oEMI: EN 55011 (Group 1, Class A)

oEMC: EN 61000-6-2

oAustralia: C-Tick N998, standard AS/NZS CISPR11
