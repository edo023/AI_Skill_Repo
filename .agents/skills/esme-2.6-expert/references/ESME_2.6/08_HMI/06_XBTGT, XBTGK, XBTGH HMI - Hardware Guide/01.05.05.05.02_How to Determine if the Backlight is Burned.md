# How to Determine if the Backlight is Burned Out

How to Determine if the Backlight is Burned Out

If your unit's backlight suddenly turns OFF, use the following steps to determine if the backlight is actually burned out:

oIf the unit's Backlight Control is not set and the screen has gone blank, your backlight is burned out.

oIf the unit's Backlight Control is set to standby mode, the screen has gone blank, and touching the screen or performing another input operation does not cause the display to reappear, your backlight is burned out.
