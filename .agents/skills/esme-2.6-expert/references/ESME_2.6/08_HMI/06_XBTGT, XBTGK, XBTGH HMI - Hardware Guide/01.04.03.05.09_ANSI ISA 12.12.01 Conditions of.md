# ANSI/ISA 12.12.01 Conditions of Acceptability and Handling Considerations for XBT GT and XBT GK

ANSI/ISA 12.12.01 Conditions of Acceptability and Handling Considerations for XBT GT and XBT GK Series

The XBT GT and XBT GK series have been designed to operate in hazardous locations in accordance with Class 1, Division 2 standards\*. All relevant local, state, and regional codes must be followed.

\* Except for XBT GT2930.
