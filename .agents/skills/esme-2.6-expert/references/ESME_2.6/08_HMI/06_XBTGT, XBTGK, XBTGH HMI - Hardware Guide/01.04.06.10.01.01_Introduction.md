# Introduction

Introduction

Install the emergency switch guard to a XBT GH unit to prevent the emergency switch from being accidentally turned on, for example, if the unit is dropped or placed upside down on a desk. The emergency switch guard complies with Safety Category 1 (ISO 13840-1).
