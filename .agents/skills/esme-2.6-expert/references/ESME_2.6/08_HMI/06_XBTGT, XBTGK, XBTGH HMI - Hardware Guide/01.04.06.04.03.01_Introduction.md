# Introduction

Introduction

Follow these instructions when supplying power to the unit.

NOTE:

oWhen the frame ground (FG) terminal is connected, be sure the wire is grounded. Not grounding the unit can result in excessive Electromagnetic Interference (EMI). Grounding is required to meet EMC level immunity.

oThe shield ground (SG) and FG terminals are connected internally in the unit.

NOTE:

oRemove power before wiring to the power terminals of the unit.

oThe unit uses only 24 Vdc power. Using any other level of power can damage both the power supply and the unit.

oSince the unit is not equipped with a power switch, be sure to connect a power switch to the unit’s power supply.

oBe sure to ground the unit's FG terminal.
