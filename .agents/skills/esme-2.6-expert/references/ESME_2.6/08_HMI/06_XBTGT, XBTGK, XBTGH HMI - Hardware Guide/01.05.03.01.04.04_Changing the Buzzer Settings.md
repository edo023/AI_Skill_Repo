# Changing the Buzzer Settings

Changing the Buzzer Settings

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | In the Settings menu, touch the Offline tab. |
| 2 | Touch the Buzzer icon. |
| 3 | Touch the desired buzzer mode. The factory setting is When Press Touch Object.  oNone: Selecting this will turn the buzzer OFF.  oWhen Press Touch Object: The buzzer will only sound when a Touch Object is touched. |
