# Introduction

Introduction

The following units have a USB port:

oXBT GT1005 series

oXBT GT2000 series

oXBT GT4000 series

oXBT GT5000 series

oXBT GT6000 series

oXBT GT7000 series

oXBT GK series

oXBT GH series

Data transfer cable (XBT ZG935) can be attached to the USB port to allow transferring of data from the computer to the unit.
