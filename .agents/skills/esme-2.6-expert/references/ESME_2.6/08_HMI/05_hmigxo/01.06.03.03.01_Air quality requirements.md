# Air quality requirements

Air quality requirements

Do not operate or store the panel where any of the following chemicals may evaporate or where these chemicals are present in the air:

oCorrosive chemicals such as acids, alkalines and liquids containing salt.

oFlammable chemicals such as organic solvents.
