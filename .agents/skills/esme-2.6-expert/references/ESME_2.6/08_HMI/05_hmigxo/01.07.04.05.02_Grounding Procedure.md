# Grounding Procedure

Grounding Procedure

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Check that the grounding resistance is less than 0.1 Ω (1). |
| 2 | The FG wire should have a cross sectional area greater than 2 mm (1). Create the connection point as close to the panel as possible, and make the wire as short as possible. When using a long grounding wire, replace the thin wire with a thicker wire, and place it in a duct. |
| 3 | If the equipment does not function properly when grounded, disconnect the ground wire from the FG terminal. |
| (1) Observe local codes and standards. Ensure the ground connection has a resistance of less than 0.1 Ω and that the ground wire has a cross-section of at least 2 mm2 or AWG 14. | |
