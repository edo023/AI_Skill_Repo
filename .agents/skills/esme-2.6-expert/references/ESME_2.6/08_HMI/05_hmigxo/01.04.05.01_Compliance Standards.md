# Compliance Standards

Compliance Standards

Schneider-Electric submitted this product for independent testing and qualification by third party listing agencies.

Schneider-Electric and these agencies have certified this product as meeting the following standards:

oDirective 2006/95/EC (Low voltage)

oDirective 2004/108/EC (EMC).

The panels are CE marked.

oEMI: EN61000-6-4

oEMS: EN61000-6-2

oEMS: EN61131-2

oUL50 Type 4 indoor only

oIP65 (front face) - IP20 (rear)
