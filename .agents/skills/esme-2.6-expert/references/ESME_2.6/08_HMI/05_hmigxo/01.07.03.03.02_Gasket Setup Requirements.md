# Gasket Setup Requirements

Gasket Setup Requirements

The gasket helps maintain the protection ratings (IP65, IP20) of the panel, and provides additional protection from vibration.

| Stage | Description |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Before installing the panel into a cabinet, check that the installation gasket is securely attached to the panel. |
| 2 | A gasket which has been used for a long period may have scratches or dirt on its surface, and could have lost much of its dust and drip resistance. Change the gasket once a year or when scratches or dirt become visible. |
| 3 | Make sure that the gasket is inserted into the panel bottom face. |
