# Touch Panel

![](images/logo.png)

Touch Panel

|  | HMIDT35X/HMIDT65X/HMIDT75X |
| --- | --- |
| Touch panel type | Analog resistive |
| Touch panel resolution | 1,024 x 1,024 |
| Touch panel service life | 1 million times or more |

The touch panel does not support multi-touch (two point touch / multiple point touch). If you touch multiple points on the touch panel, it may operate as if you touched the center-point of the multiple touches. For example, if you touch two or more points on the touch panel and at the center of the touches is a switch for a drive system, even though you did not directly touch that switch, it may function as if you did.
