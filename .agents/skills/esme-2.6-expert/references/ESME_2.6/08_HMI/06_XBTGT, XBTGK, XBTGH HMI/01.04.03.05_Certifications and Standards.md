Agency Certifications

Schneider Electric submitted this product for independent testing and qualification by third-party listing agencies. These agencies have certified this product as meeting the following standards.

Agency Certifications for XBTGT and XBTGK Series

North America: XBT GT and XBT GK series certified by Underwriters Laboratories Inc. and/or Canadian Standard Association against:

oUL508, Industrial Control Equipment

oCSA-C22.2, No. 142-M1987 - Standard for Process Control Equipment

oANSI/ISA - 12.12.01(1) Electrical Equipment for Use in Class I Division 2 Hazardous Locations

oCAN/CSA-C22.2, No.14, No.213(1) - For Hazardous Locations

(1) Except for XBT GT2930.

Some XBT GT terminals are type approved by Marine Agencies(2):

oRMRS Russia

oRINA Italy

oLR United Kingdom

oGL Germany

oDNV Norway

oBV France

oABS United States of America

For detailed information, contact your local distributor.

(2) Except for XBT GT2430/2930/5430.

Hazardous Substances

The XBT GK, XBT GT, and XBT GH Series are designed for compliance with:

oWEEE, Directive 2002/96/EC

This product is compliant with:

oRoHS, Directive 2011/65/EU

oRoHS China, Standard SJ/T 11363-2006

ANSI/ISA 12.12.01 Conditions of Acceptability and Handling Considerations for XBT GT and XBT GK Series

The XBT GT and XBT GK series have been designed to operate in hazardous locations in accordance with Class 1, Division 2 standards\*. All relevant local, state, and regional codes must be followed.

\* Except for XBT GT2930.
