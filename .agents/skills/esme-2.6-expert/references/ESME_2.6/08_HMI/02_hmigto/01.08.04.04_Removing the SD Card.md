# Removing the SD Card

![](images/logo.png)

Removing the SD Card

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Make sure the SD Card Access LED is off.  G-SE-0015006.1.gif-high.gif    1   SD Card Access LED |
| 2 | Push down the tab on the SD Card cover, then pull open the cover.  G-SE-0015008.1.gif-high.gif    1   Tab |
| 3 | Push the SD Card once to release, and pull out the card. After removing the card, close the cover.  NOTE: After using the SD Card, store the SD Card in its case or other safe location. |
