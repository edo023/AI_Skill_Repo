Memory, Clock, Touch Panel, and Function Switches

Introduction

NOTE:

oWhen the message indicating that the battery level is low is displayed, supply power to the panel and fully charge the battery. In 24 hours the battery charges to a level that allows backup operation. Completing a full charge requires about 120 hours (five days).

oThe lifetime of the rechargeable lithium battery is 10 years when the battery’s ambient temperature is 40 °C (104 °F) or less, 4.1 years when the battery’s ambient temperature is 50 °C (122 °F) or less, and 1.5 years when the battery’s ambient temperature is 60 °C (140 °F) or less.

When used for backup:

oApproximately 100 days with a fully charged battery.

oApproximately 6 days with a half-charged battery.
