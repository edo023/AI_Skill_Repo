# Introduction

Introduction

The serial interface is not isolated. The SG (signal ground) and the FG (functional ground) terminals are connected inside this product.

Use only the SELV (Safety Extra-Low Voltage) circuit to connect the COM, USB, and LAN interfaces.

NOTE: For information on how to connect controllers and other types of equipment, refer to the corresponding device driver manual of your screen editing software.
