# Precautions

Precautions

oCheck that the grounding resistance is 100 Ω or less.\*1

oThe FG wire should have a cross sectional area greater than 2 mm2 (AWG14)\*1. Create the connection point as close to this product as possible, and make the wire as short as possible. When using a long grounding wire, replace the thin wire with a thicker wire, and place it in a duct.

oThe SG (signal ground) and FG (functional ground) terminals are connected internally in this product. When connecting the SG line to another device, be sure that no ground loop is formed.

\*1 Observe local codes and standards.
