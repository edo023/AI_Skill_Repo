Introduction

This product is designed for use on flat surfaces of Type 1, Type 4X (indoor use only), Type 13 Enclosure, or IP65.

Be aware of the following when building this product into an end-use product:

oThe rear face of this product is not approved as an enclosure. When building this product into an end-use product, be sure to use an enclosure that satisfies standards as the end-use product’s overall enclosure.

oInstall this product in an enclosure with mechanical rigidity.

oThis product is not designed for outdoor use. UL certification obtained is for indoor use only.

oInstall and operate this product with its front panel facing outward.

NOTE: IP65 is not part of the UL certification.
