# TMC4 Cartridges

## Overview

You can expand the number of I/Os of your Modicon M241 Logic Controller by adding TMC4 cartridges.

For more information, refer to the [TMC4 Cartridges Hardware Guide](../../../../../api/crossBook?lang=en-US&virtualBookName=tmc4hw&topicID=D_SE_0035769).

## TMC4 Standard Cartridges

The following table presents the general-purpose TMC4 [cartridges](../../../../../api/crossBook?lang=en-US&virtualBookName=tmc4hw&topicID=D_SE_0035771) with the corresponding channel type, voltage/current range, and terminal type:

| Reference | Channels | Channel Type | Voltage  Current | Terminal Type |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| [TMC4AI2](../../../../../api/crossBook?lang=en-US&virtualBookName=tmc4hw&topicID=D_SE_0036454) | 2 | Analog inputs (voltage or current) | 0...10 Vdc  0...20 mA or 4...20 mA | 3.81 mm (0.15 in.) pitch, removable spring terminal block |
| [TMC4TI2](../../../../../api/crossBook?lang=en-US&virtualBookName=tmc4hw&topicID=D_SE_0036482) | 2 | Analog temperature inputs | Thermocouple type K, J, R, S, B, E, T, N,C  3 wires RTD type Pt100, Pt1000, Ni100, Ni1000 | 3.81 mm (0.15 in.) pitch, removable spring terminal block |
| [TMC4AQ2](../../../../../api/crossBook?lang=en-US&virtualBookName=tmc4hw&topicID=D_SE_0036486) | 2 | Analog outputs (voltage or current) | 0...10 Vdc  4...20 mA | 3.81 mm (0.15 in.) pitch, removable spring terminal block |
