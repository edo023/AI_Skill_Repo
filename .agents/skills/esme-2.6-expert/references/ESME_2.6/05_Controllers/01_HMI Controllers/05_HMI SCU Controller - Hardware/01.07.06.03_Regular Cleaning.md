Regular Cleaning

Cleaning the Display

|  |
| --- |
| NOTICE |
| EQUIPMENT DAMAGE |
| oPower off the unit before cleaning it.  oDo not use hard or pointed objects to operate the touch panel since it can damage the panel surface.  oDo not use paint thinner, organic solvents, or a strong acid compound to clean the unit. |
| Failure to follow these instructions can result in equipment damage. |

When the surface or the frame of the display gets dirty, soak a soft cloth in water with a neutral detergent, wring the cloth tightly, and wipe the display.
