Mounting Positions

Correct Mounting Position

If the display module is mounted separately, the rear module must be mounted vertically:

![G-SE-0023367.1.gif-high.gif](images/G-SE-0023367.1.gif-high.gif)

NOTE: Keep adequate spacing for proper ventilation to maintain an ambient temperature between 0...50 °C (32...122 °F).

Incorrect Mounting Position

The rear module should only be positioned as shown in the correct mounting position figure to help ensure adequate air flow through the product.
