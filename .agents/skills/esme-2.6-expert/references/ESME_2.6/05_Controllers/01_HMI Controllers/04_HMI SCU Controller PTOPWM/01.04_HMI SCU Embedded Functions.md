# HMI SCU Embedded Functions

![](images/logo.png)

HMI SCU Embedded Functions

Overview

This part describes the embedded functions of the Magelis SCU HMI Controller.

Each embedded function uses inputs and outputs.

The Magelis SCU HMI Controller has:

oUp to 16 digital inputs, including 2 fast inputs

oUp to 10 outputs, including 8 relay outputs and 2 fast outputs
