CAN Bus Configuration

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | In the Devices tree, double-click CAN. |
| 2 | Configure the baudrate (by default: 250000 bits/s):  G-SE-0002013.4.gif-high.gif      NOTE: The Online Bus Access option allows you to block SDO, [DTM](../glossary/glossary.htm#XREF_D_SE_0024697_680), and [NMT](../glossary/glossary.htm#XREF_D_SE_0024697_153) sending through the status screen. |

When connecting a DTM to a device using the network, the DTM communicates in parallel with the running application. The overall performance of the system is impacted and can cause overload on the network.
