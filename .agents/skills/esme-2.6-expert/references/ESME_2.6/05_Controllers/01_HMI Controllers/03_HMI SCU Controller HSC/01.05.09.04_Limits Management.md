# Limits Management

![](images/logo.png)

Limits Management

Overview

When the counter limit is reached, the counter behaves as Rollover.

Rollover

In the case of overflow or underflow of the counter, the current counter value goes automatically to the opposite limit value. Modulo\_Flag output is set to TRUE.
