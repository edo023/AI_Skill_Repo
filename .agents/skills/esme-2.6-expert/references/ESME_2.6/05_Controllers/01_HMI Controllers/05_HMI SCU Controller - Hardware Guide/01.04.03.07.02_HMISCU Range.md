The HMISAC and HMISBC are replacement rear modules. The HMIS65 and HMIS85 are screen replacement modules. The table shows for equivalence:

| Replacement Rear Module | | Replacement Screen | | Equivalent Full Module |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| HMISAC | + | HMIS65 (3.5 inch) | = | HMISCU6A5 |
| HMISAC | + | HMIS85 (5.7 inch) | = | HMISCU8A5 |
| HMISBC | + | HMIS65 (3.5 inch) | = | HMISCU6B5 |
| HMISBC | + | HMIS85 (5.7 inch) | = | HMISCU8B5 |
