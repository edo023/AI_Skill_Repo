# Dedicated Outputs

Dedicated Outputs

Outputs used by the Pulse Train Output, Pulse Width Modulation, and High Speed Counters can only be accessed through the function block. They cannot be read or written directly within the [application](../glossary/glossary.htm#XREF_D_SE_0024697_626).
