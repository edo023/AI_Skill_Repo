# Introduction

## Description

Schneider Electric provides a one cable solution that allows you to find the Schneider Electric controllers in a C2C network, including the slave controllers below the C2C Master controller.

This means that if you have a running C2C network, you can connect your Ethernet cable to any of the Schneider Electric controllers (Master or Slaves). This controller acts as a gateway and allows you to communicate with the other Schneider Electric controllers in your C2C network avoiding a direct cable connection.

So you can access controllers you cannot connect directly, for example, if the controller is installed in an inaccessible area of your plant or machine.

NOTE: As you use the bandwidth of the Sercos bus, keep in mind that transmission times are higher than for direct connection. This can influence the present communication with the controller that is used as a gateway in between to the slave.

You can use the one cable connection:

* [With EcoStruxure Machine Expert](D-SE-0088167.html#D-SE-0088167)
* [With the Diagnostics tool](D-SE-0088168.html#D-SE-0088168)
* [With the Controller Assistant tool](D-SE-0088169.html#D-SE-0088169)
