# C2C Master Project

## Description

The following main points are performed in the C2C Master project:

* A real axis (upper motor at TTS1 with the label "Axis 1") performs a motion (triggered by the *[YOffsetGenerator](../../../../../api/crossBook?lang=en-US&virtualBookName=PD.Parameter.LXM62Drive&topicID=D_SE_0071847)*). The motion of this axis is transmitted to the **C2C Encoder Input** of the C2C Slave (TTS2) by the **C2C Encoder Output** of the C2C Master.
* The values of the data structure ST\_MyData (produced by the C2C Slave) are written in the **C2C Data Input** of the C2C Master.
