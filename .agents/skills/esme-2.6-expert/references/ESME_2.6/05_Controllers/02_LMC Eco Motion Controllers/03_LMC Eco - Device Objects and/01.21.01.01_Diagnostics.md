# Diagnostics

## General

The access of the CANopen master to diagnoses of the CANopen slave is executed by means of the function GET\_STATE.

Ensure that the configured value `Net + 1` has to be transferred in the input parameter NETWORK.

NOTE: Diagnostic parameters may be traced in all tasks **except for** the SystemMotionTask since this will cause a delay of the retrieval of the diagnostic from the NetC component and thus of the RTP.
