## Adjust Real-Time Clock

The real-time clock is not adjusted at the time of delivery of the device. Summer and winter time is considered by the device. As of LMC firmware V1.64.x.x, the time zone information is supported. `SysTimeRtcSetTimeZone` allows the configuration (preconfigured for CET). LMC parameter `RealTimeClock` can be set with `SetRealTimeClock` or `SysTimeRtcSet`. If the real-time clock is not adjusted, the time and date specifications in the message logger will not be correct. Make certain that the real-time clock is adjusted correctly.
