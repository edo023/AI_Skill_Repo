M660 controllers provide two PCI Express Mini Card slots and two associated openings in the housing that allow you to install PCI Express Mini Card modules that feature external interfaces. Originally, the openings are covered with blind plates as long as no PCI Express Mini Card modules are installed.

Two types of PCI Express Mini Cards are available:

1. PCI Express Mini Card modules with external interfaces, for example, RJ45 connectors. They consist of the following components:

   * PCI Express Mini card: This card is inserted into the PCI Express Mini Card slot.
   * Connector board: This component consists of a board with the external interfaces, for example, RJ45 connectors.
   * Ribbon cable: The ribbon cable connects the PCI Express Mini Card and the connector board.
   * Screws: The screws are used to hold the PCI Express Mini Card.
2. PCI Express Mini Cards without external interfaces, for example, AI accelerator cards. They consist of the following components:

   * PCI Express Mini Card: This card is inserted into the PCI Express Mini Card slot.
   * Screws: The screws are used to hold the PCI Express Mini Card.

A PCI Express Mini Card slot provides a maximum power of 3.6 W at a voltage of 3.3 V. For technical data, refer to [PCI Express Mini Card Slots](PCIExpressMiniSlots-DFC6BDBE.html). For information on the input power increase with installed PCI Express Mini Cards, refer to [Input Power](ElectricalData-E70A295D.html).

PCI Express Mini Cards that are recognized by the firmware of the controller are made available under VxWorks (motion control part). If you install a PCI Express Mini Card that is not recognized by the firmware of the controller, it is made available under Windows (industrial PC part, MN660P controllers only).

## Installing a PCI Express Mini Card Module or Card

If you want to install a PCI Express Mini Card module or card in a controller already installed and commissioned, disconnect all wiring from the controller and remove it from the control cabinet prior to starting the installation procedure. Label the cables and wires to facilitate re-installation.

PCI Express Mini Cards that are recognized by the firmware of the controller are made available under VxWorks (motion control part). If you install a PCI Express Mini Card that is not recognized by the firmware of the controller, it is made available under Windows (industrial PC part, MN660P controllers only).
