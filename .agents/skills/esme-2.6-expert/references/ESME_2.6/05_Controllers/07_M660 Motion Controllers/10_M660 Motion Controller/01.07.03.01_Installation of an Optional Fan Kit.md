The fan kit is available as an accessory (commercial reference MNAH6AFS01 for MN660•••••••1••• controllers, refer to [Accessories and Spare Parts](Accessories-E6F36BC8.html)). It allows you to improve the heat dissipation in the controller.

The fan kit consists of a fan plate with two fans attached to it. This fan plate replaces the bottom plate of the controller.

## Installing an Optional Fan Kit

Procedure:

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Remove power from the controller and from all connected equipment. |
| 2 | Touch the housing or ground connection of the controller (not the power supply) to discharge electrostatic charge from your body. |
| 3 | If the housing has been sealed via the sealing lug, cut the sealing wire and remove the seal. |
| 4 | Remove the two knurled screws at the bottom plate of the controller. |
| 5 | Remove the bottom plate. |
| 6 | Push the connectors of the fans into the white connector sockets on the printed circuit board. No specific order is required because both fans are switched simultaneously. |
| 7 | Fit the fan plate into position. |
| 8 | Fasten the fan plate with the two knurled screws. |
| 9 | Mount a new housing seal, if required. |
| 10 | Verify correct operation of all equipment. |

Fan monitoring is not enabled by default for controllers that are not factory-equipped with a fan kit. After installing the fan kit, enable fan monitoring in EcoStruxure Machine Expert. Refer to the [Modicon M660 Motion Controller Programming Guide](AboutTheBook-E6EB8FB0.html#AboutTheBook-E6EB8FB0__M660ProgrammingGuide-3CE60EE1) for details. Do not enable fan monitoring if no fan kit is installed.

## Uninstalling and Reinstalling a Fan Kit

Certain activities such as installing PCI Express cards or PCI Express Mini Card modules or cards require you to temporarily uninstall the fan kit for access to the controller housing and to reinstall it to complete the installation.

Procedure:

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Remove power from the controller and from all connected equipment. |
| 2 | Touch the housing or ground connection of the controller (not the power supply) to discharge electrostatic charge from your body. |
| 3 | If the housing has been sealed via the sealing lug, cut the sealing wire and remove the seal. |
| 4 | Remove the two (MN660•••••••1•••) or four (MN660•••••••2•••••) knurled screws at the bottom of the controller. |
| 5 | Remove the fan plate with the fan assembly and disconnect the two fan cables. |
| 6 | Perform the steps required to install the equipment in the PCI Express Mini Card slots or the PCI Express Card slots. For details, refer to [Installation of PCI Express Mini Card Module or Card](InstallingModules-15BB7327.html) and [Installation of PCI Express Cards](InstallationOfPCIExpressCards-DDD5D13D.html). |
| 7 | Reconnect the two fan cables. Ensure the connectors are securely inserted in their connectors. |
| 8 | Refit and tighten the two (MN660•••••••1•••) or four (MN660•••••••2•••••) knurled screws at the bottom of the controller. |
| 9 | Mount a new housing seal, if required. |
| 10 | Verify correct operation of all equipment. |
