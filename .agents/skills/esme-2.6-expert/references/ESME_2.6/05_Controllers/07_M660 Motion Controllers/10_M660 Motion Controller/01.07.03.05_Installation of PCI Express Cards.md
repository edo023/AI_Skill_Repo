A PCI Express Card slot provides a maximum power of 8 W at a voltage of 3.3 V and of 12 V. For technical data, refer to [PCI Express Card Slots](PCIExpressSlots-DFC70C18.html). For information on the input power increase with installed PCI Express Cards, refer to [Input Power](ElectricalData-E70A295D.html).

PCI Express Cards that are recognized by the firmware of the controller are made available under VxWorks (motion control part). If you install a PCI Express Card that is not recognized by the firmware of the controller, it is made available under Windows (industrial PC part).

## Installing a PCI Express Card

If you want to install a PCI Express Card in a controller already installed and commissioned, disconnect all wiring from the controller and remove it from the control cabinet prior to starting the installation procedure. Label the cables and wires to facilitate re-installation.

The following description presents the installation of a PCI Express Card in the lower slot (the slot closest to the heat sink). It is a best practice to install the first PCI Express card in the lower slot. If you want to install a second PCI Express card at a later point in time, you can access the upper slot (without having to remove the first PCI Express card).

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Remove the fan kit from your controller. For details, refer to [Uninstalling and Reinstalling a Fan Kit](InstallationOfAFanKit-3ADB63FA.html#InstallationOfAFanKit-3ADB63FA__UninstallingAndRe-InstallingAFanKit-DFD759B9). |
| 2 | Remove the mounting plate. For details, refer to [Preparing the Controller for Mounting](Mounting-E7061508.html#Mounting-E7061508__PreparingTheControllerForMounting-B150C980). Invert the sequence of steps of the procedure described. |
| 3 | At the bottom of the controller, remove the grounding screw (**1**).  Remove the screws (**2**), (**3**) and (**4**). |
| 4 | At the typeplate side, remove the screws (**1**), (**2**), (**3**) and (**4**). |
| 5 | At the backside, remove the screws (**1**), (**2**) and (**3**). |
| 6 | At the top of the controller, remove the screws (**1**), (**2**), (**3**) and (**4**). |
| 7 | Lift the U-shaped cover off of the controller housing. You can now access the PCI Express Card lower slot (**1**) and upper slot (**2**). |
| 8 | Insert a screwdriver (TX10) through the hole (**1**) and remove the screw (**2**).  Remove the blind plate (**3**). |
| 9 | Loosen the screws (**1**), (**2**), (**3**) and (**4**) and slide the PCI Express Card supports (**5**) and (**6**) to the left. |
| 10 | Insert the PCI Express Card (**1**) into the lower slot. |
| 11 | Refit the screw (**2**) with a screwdriver (TX10) through the hole (**1**) shown in [step 8](#InstallationOfPCIExpressCards-DDD5D13D__Step8_1224114812-DDDA46FB) and tighten it with a tightening torque of 0.82 Nm (7.26 lb.in). |
| 12 | Slide the support (**5**) to the right until it holds the PCI Express card and re-tighten the screws (**1**) and (**2**) loosened in [step 9](#InstallationOfPCIExpressCards-DDD5D13D__Step9_1224114912-DDDA4DAE) with a tightening torque of 0.82 Nm (7.26 lb.in).  Re-tighten the screws (**3**) and (**4**) for the support (**6**) loosened in [step 9](#InstallationOfPCIExpressCards-DDD5D13D__Step9_1224114912-DDDA4DAE) with a tightening torque of 0.82 Nm (7.26 lb.in). |
| 13 | Refit the U-shaped cover of the controller housing removed in [step 7](#InstallationOfPCIExpressCards-DDD5D13D__Step7_1224114712-DDDA3E23). |
| 14 | Refit the screws removed in [step 6](#InstallationOfPCIExpressCards-DDD5D13D__Step6_1224114612-DDDA37E7). Tightening torques:   * Screws **1**, **3**, **4**: 0.82 Nm (7.26 lb.in) * Screw **2**: 0.43 Nm (0.38 lb.in) |
| 15 | Refit the screws **1**, **2** and **3** removed in removed in [step 5](#InstallationOfPCIExpressCards-DDD5D13D__Step5_1224114512-DDDA2F14) (tightening torque 0.82 Nm (7.26 lb.in)). |
| 16 | Refit the screws **1**, **2**, **3** and **4** removed in [step 4](#InstallationOfPCIExpressCards-DDD5D13D__Step4_1224114411-DDDA27BB) (tightening torque 0.82 Nm (7.26 lb.in)). |
| 17 | Refit the screws **2**, **3**, and **4** removed in [step 3](#InstallationOfPCIExpressCards-DDD5D13D__Step3_1224114311-DDDA20E3) (tightening torque 0.82 Nm (7.26 lb.in)). |
| 18 | Refit the grounding screw **1** also removed in [step 3](#InstallationOfPCIExpressCards-DDD5D13D__Step3_1224114311-DDDA20E3). For further details, refer to [Grounding Procedure](Grounding-E7057D7B.html#Grounding-E7057D7B__GroundingProcedure-1761F08A). |
| 19 | Refit the mounting plate removed in [step 2](#InstallationOfPCIExpressCards-DDD5D13D__Step2_1224114211-DDDA18D2). For further details, refer to [Preparing the Controller for Mounting](Mounting-E7061508.html#Mounting-E7061508__PreparingTheControllerForMounting-B150C980). |
| 20 | Refit the fan kit removed in [step 1](#InstallationOfPCIExpressCards-DDD5D13D__Step1_1224114111-DDDA12A4). For further details, refer to [Uninstalling and Reinstalling a Fan Kit](InstallationOfAFanKit-3ADB63FA.html#InstallationOfAFanKit-3ADB63FA__UninstallingAndRe-InstallingAFanKit-DFD759B9). |

After having installed the PCI Express Card, install and commission the controller. For details, refer to [Installation](Installation-3CE89AFC.html) and [Commissioning](Commissioning-E6EE327D.html).

The firmware of the controller automatically detects that a new PCI Express Card has been installed.
