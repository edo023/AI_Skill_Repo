# PCI Express Card Slots

Controllers of type MN660P••••••2••••• provide two PCI Express Card slots. Refer to [Type Code](TypeCode-E6EB6EB6.html) for details on M660 controller types.

| Characteristic | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Number of slots | 2 |
| Number of lanes | PCIE Gen3 x1 |
| Type of socket | Open-ended (can accommodate cards with more lanes) |
| Maximum card size | Half size cards:   * Height: 111.15 mm (4.37 in) * Length: 167.65 mm (6.60 in) |
| Maximum power | * 8 W at 3.3 V * 8 W at 12 V   NOTE: Installing cards in the PCI Express slots increases the input current of your controller. For details, refer to [Input Power](ElectricalData-E70A295D.html). |
| Assignment to operating system | * VxWorks (motion control part) if the card is recognized by the controller firmware * Windows (industrial PC part) if the card is not recognized by the controller firmware |
