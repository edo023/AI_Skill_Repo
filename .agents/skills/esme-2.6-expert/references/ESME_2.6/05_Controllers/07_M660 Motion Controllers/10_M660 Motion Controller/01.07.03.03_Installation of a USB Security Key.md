# Installation of a USB Security Key

## General Information

M660 controllers are equipped with an internal USB 2.0 Type-A port (refer to [Technical Data - USB Ports](USBPorts-E70A9E6A.html) for details).

The USB 2.0 Type-A port is available after you have removed the bottom plate of the controller. It is intended for security or licensing keys such as such as CodeMeter from Wibu under Windows for the industrial PC of an MN660P motion controller. Such keys allow you to implement security or software licensing features on the industrial PC of the controller.

The maximum length of a USB key that can be connected to the internal USB 2.0 Type-A port is 35.5 mm (1.39 in). Connect the USB key directly to the internal USB 2.0 Type-A port without using a cable. Do not use the USB 2.0 Type-A port for other purposes.

The USB 2.0 Type-A is not available under VxWorks.

Procedure:

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Remove power from the controller and from all connected equipment. |
| 2 | Touch the housing or ground connection of the controller (not the power supply) to discharge electrostatic charge from your body. |
| 3 | If the housing has been sealed via the sealing lug, cut the sealing wire and remove the seal. |
| 4 | Remove the two (MN660•••••••1•••) or four (MN660•••••••2•••••) knurled screws at the bottom of the controller. |
| 5 | Controller without fan kit:   * Remove the bottom plate.   Controller with fan kit:   * Remove the fan plate with the fan assembly and disconnect the two fan cables. |
| 6 | Insert the USB key into the USB 2.0 Type-A port (do not use a USB cable). |
| 7 | Controller without fan kit:   * Refit the bottom plate into position.   Controller with fan kit:   * Reconnect the two fan cables. Ensure the connectors are securely inserted in their connectors. * Refit the fan plate with the fan assembly into position. |
| 8 | Refit and tighten the two (MN660•••••••1•••) or four (MN660•••••••2•••••) knurled screws at the bottom of the controller. |
| 9 | Mount a new housing seal, if required. |
