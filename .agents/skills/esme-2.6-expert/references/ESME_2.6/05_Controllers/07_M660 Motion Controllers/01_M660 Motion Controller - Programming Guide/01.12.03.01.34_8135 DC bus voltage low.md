# 8135 DC bus voltage low

Diagnostic class: 2

| Device reactions | Default reaction | Minimum reaction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Drive | D | E |
| Power supply | D | E |

For drives: An attempt is being made to start the drive (ControllerEnable = True), but the DC bus voltage is too low.

For power supplies: An attempt is being made to enable the power supply (PowerSupplyCheck = on / 1), but the DC bus voltage is too low.

| Device | Threshold |
| --- | --- |
| Lexium 62 | * 420 Vdc in 400 Vac operation * 220 Vdc in 230 Vac operation |
| Lexium 62 Power Supply | * 440 Vdc in 400 Vac operation * 240 Vdc in 230 Vac operation |
| Lexium 62 ILM | * 420 Vdc in 400 Vac operation * 220 Vdc in 230 Vac operation |

| Cause | Solution |
| --- | --- |
| ControllerEnable is TRUE, but the mains voltage is too low. | Verify and correct if necessary the mains voltage (see the technical data on the device); the mains contactor may not be connected. |
| If DC buses are connected in parallel, an error has occurred in a servo amplifier. | * Locate servo amplifier with error. * Eliminate error. |
