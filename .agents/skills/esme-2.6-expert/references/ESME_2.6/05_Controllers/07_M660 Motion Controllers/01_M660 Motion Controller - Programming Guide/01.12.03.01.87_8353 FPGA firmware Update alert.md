# 8353 FPGA firmware: Update alert

Diagnostic class: 2

An alert was detected in the update of the FPGA firmware of a communication device. The FPGA firmware was not updated. The affected communication device remains operative, but it may not use the latest FPGA firmware.

The ext. diagnosis indicates the affected communication device.

| Cause | Remedy |
| --- | --- |
| No matching FPGA firmware was found for a given communication device. | Restart the controller. The FPGA firmware update is repeated. Verify that the FPGA firmware update has completed (diagnostic message [8351 FPGA firmware: Update completed](FPGAFirmwareUpdateCompleted-4C961008.html)).  If the condition persists, contact your local Schneider Electric representative. |
