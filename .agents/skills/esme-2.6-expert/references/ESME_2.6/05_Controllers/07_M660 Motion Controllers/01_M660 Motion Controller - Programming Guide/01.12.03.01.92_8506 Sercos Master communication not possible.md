# 8506 Sercos: Master communication not possible

Diagnostic class: 3

Sercos communication interrupted because no link is detected on either port.

| Cause | Solution |
| --- | --- |
| Device disconnected from Sercos bus. | Verify that the device is properly connected to the Sercos bus. |
| Device not energized. | Energize the device. |
| Device inoperative. | * Power-cycle the device. * Replace the device. |

In two consecutive cycles, an error is detected on the Sercos bus while receiving data. The Sercos bus is shut down.

Acknowledging a diagnostic message does not remove the cause of the detected error.
