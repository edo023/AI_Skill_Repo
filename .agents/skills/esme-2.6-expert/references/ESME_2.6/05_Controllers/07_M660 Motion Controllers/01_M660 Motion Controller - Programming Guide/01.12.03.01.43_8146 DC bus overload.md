# 8146 DC bus overload

Diagnostic class: 3

| Device reactions | Default reaction | Minimum reaction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Drive | - | - |
| Power supply | BP1 | BP1 |

The DC bus is overloaded. In this case, unlike diagnostic message '[8107 Overcurrent](TPC_M660_Diagnostics_8107Overcurren-03B0EDE7.html)', switch off does not occur as soon as the current threshold has been exceeded. The thermal overload of the device is observed for a specific time.

| Cause | Solution |
| --- | --- |
| Too many loads are connected to the DC bus. | * It may be that the existing device constellation is sufficient by adapting the application (for example, less acceleration). * Use another power supply and distribute supply of loads. |
