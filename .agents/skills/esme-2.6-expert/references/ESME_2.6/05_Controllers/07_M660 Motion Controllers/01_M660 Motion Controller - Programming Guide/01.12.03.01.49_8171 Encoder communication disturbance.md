# 8171 Encoder communication disturbance detected

Diagnostic class: 2

| Device reactions | Default reaction | Minimum reaction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Drive | D | E |
| Power supply | - | - |

Alert concerning data transmission from the motor encoder over the serial interface. The drive continues to operate, but if the condition persists, it may be disabled with the diagnostics message '[8958 Encoder communication not possible](EncoderCommunicationNotPossible-0444B561.html)'.

| Cause | Solution |
| --- | --- |
| Incorrect encoder cable or incorrect wiring. | Verify correct wiring of the encoder with the correct encoder cable. |
| The 24 Vdc control voltage is too low. | Verify correct control voltage. |
| Encoder inoperative. | Replace the motor or the encoder. |

The following diagnostics messages provide additional information on the effects of an interruption of the encoder communication:

* [8958 Encoder communication not possible](EncoderCommunicationNotPossible-0444B561.html)
* [8176 Encoder connection break](EncoderConnectionBreak-03C40691.html)
* [8105 Encoder position invalid](TPC_M660_Diagnostics_8105EncoderSig-03B03323.html)
* [8175 Extended message for encoder disturbances](EncoderCommunicationDisturbanceDete-03C2A51C.html) (from drive logger)
