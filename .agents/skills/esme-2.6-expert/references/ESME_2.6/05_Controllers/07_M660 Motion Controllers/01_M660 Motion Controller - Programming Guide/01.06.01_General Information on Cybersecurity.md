# General Information on Cybersecurity

The controller and the programming environment offer a number of cybersecurity features that you need to configure according to the requirements of your application and your infrastructure. In addition, take all measures identified in your cybersecurity risk assessment or equivalent risk assessment to help protect your infrastructure.

The following table provides an overview of the minimum cybersecurity measures you must take:

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Identify the cybersecurity risks to your infrastructure and implement all necessary measures. |
| 2 | Implement the measures described in the EcoStruxure Machine Expert [Programming Guide, Cybersecurity](../../../../../api/crossBook?lang=en-US&virtualBookName=SoMProg&topicID=D_SE_0108413) . |
| 3 | Verify the settings for the M600 firewall that is activated by default. If necessary, [configure the firewall](Firewall-01B95A89.html). |
