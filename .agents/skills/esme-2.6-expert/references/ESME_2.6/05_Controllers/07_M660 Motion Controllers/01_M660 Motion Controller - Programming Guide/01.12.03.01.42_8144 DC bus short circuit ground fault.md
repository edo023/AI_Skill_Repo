# 8144 DC bus short circuit / ground fault

Diagnostic class: 3

| Device reactions | Default reaction | Minimum reaction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Drive | - | - |
| Power supply | AP | AP |

A short-circuit has occurred in the DC bus.

| Cause | Solution |
| --- | --- |
| Detected wiring error. The connection cables from the power supply to the servo drives through the distribution boxes are not connected properly or have a short circuit. | Verify the cables and replace if necessary. |
