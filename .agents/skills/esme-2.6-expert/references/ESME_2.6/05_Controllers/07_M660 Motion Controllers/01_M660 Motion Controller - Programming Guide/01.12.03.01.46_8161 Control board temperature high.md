# 8161 Control board temperature high

Diagnostic class: 2

| Device reactions | Default reaction | Minimum reaction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Drive | D | E |
| Power supply | D | E |

* The temperature of the power board inside the housing is too high. If the temperature continues to increase, the diagnostic message '8142 Control board overtemperature' is triggered.
