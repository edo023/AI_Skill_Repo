# 8517 Sercos: Addressing not unique

Diagnostic class: 3

A Sercos device in the configuration could not be uniquely assigned to a physical device on the Sercos bus.

| Cause | Solution |
| --- | --- |
| At least two Sercos devices with identical identification mode and addressing were detected. | Verify the setting of the parameter [IdentificationMode](M660_UserFunct_SercosIdentification-44A406D8.html#M660_UserFunct_SercosIdentification-44A406D8__M660_Param_IdentificationMode-44A4FEE1) and the settings in the corresponding parameters of the user function [SercosIdentification](M660_UserFunct_SercosIdentification-44A406D8.html#M660_UserFunct_SercosIdentification-44A406D8). |
