# 8352 FPGA firmware: Update error detected

Diagnostic class: 3

An error was detected in the update of the FPGA firmware of a communication device. The affected communication device may be inoperative.

The ext. diagnosis indicates the affected communication device.

| Cause | Remedy |
| --- | --- |
| The controller was powered off during an FPGA firmware update. | Restart the controller. The FPGA firmware update is repeated. Verify that the FPGA firmware update has completed (diagnostic message [8351 FPGA firmware: Update completed](FPGAFirmwareUpdateCompleted-4C961008.html)).  If the condition persists, contact your local Schneider Electric representative. |
