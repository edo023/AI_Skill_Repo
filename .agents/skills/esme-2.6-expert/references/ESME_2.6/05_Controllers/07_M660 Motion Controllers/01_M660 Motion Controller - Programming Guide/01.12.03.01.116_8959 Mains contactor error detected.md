# 8959 Mains contactor error detected

Diagnostic class: 3

| Device reactions | Default reaction | Minimum reaction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Drive | - | - |
| Power supply | BP1 | BP1 |

The DC bus is charged before the ready contact is closed by the device.

| Cause | Solution |
| --- | --- |
| The mains contactor is not affected by the ready contact of the device. | Switch mains contactor using the ready contact of the device. |
