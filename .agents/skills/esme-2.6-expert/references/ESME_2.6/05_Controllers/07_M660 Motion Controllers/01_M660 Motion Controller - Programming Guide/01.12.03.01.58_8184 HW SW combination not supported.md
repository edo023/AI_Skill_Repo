# 8184 HW/SW combination not supported

Diagnostic class: 3

| Device reactions | Default reaction | Minimum reaction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Drive | AD | AD |
| Power supply | AP | AP |

The used firmware does not match the used hardware or FPGA version.

| Cause | Solution |
| --- | --- |
| A firmware was transferred on the device that is not compatible to the hardware. | Transfer firmware that is compatible to the hardware. |
| An FPGA version was transferred on the device that is not compatible to the firmware. | Transfer FPGA version that is compatible to the firmware. |
