# 8129 Power stage load high

Diagnostic class: 2

| Device reactions | Default reaction | Minimum reaction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Drive | D | E |
| Power supply | - | - |

The power stage is overloaded. The overload level has been increased up to 80 %. This may cause an error '[8120 Power stage overload](PowerStageOverload-03BD265A.html)' if the load of the power stage is not reduced.
