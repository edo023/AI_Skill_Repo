# 8976 Mains phases wiring not connected

Diagnostic class: 3

| Device reactions | Default reaction | Minimum reaction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Drive | - | - |
| Power supply | BP1 | BP1 |

The mains phases were not connected to the device correctly.

| Cause | Solution |
| --- | --- |
| The wiring of the mains phases is not correct (for example, only one individual phase was connected to the device in the three-phase operating mode). | Verify and correct if necessary the wiring of the mains phases. |
