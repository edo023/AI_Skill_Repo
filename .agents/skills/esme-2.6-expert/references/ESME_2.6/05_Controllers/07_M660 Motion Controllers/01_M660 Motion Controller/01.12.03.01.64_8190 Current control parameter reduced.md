# 8190 Current control parameter reduced

Diagnostic class: 1

| Device reactions | Default reaction | Minimum reaction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Drive (Lexium 62 Standard) | E | E |
| Power supply | - | - |

For Lexium 62 Standard and Lexium 62 ILM used with the motors of the types SH3040 and BSH040 it is necessary to reduce the internal current control loop parameters to get a stable control behavior. This reduction is indicated by this diagnostic message.

EIO0000005527.01
