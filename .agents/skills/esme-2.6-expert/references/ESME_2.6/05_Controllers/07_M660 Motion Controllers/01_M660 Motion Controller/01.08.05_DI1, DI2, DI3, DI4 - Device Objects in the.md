The controller provides four onboard digital inputs through the connector **CN2**. The device objects DI1, DI2, DI3 and DI4 represent the pins of the digital input of connector **CN2** of the controller.

The four device objects for the digital inputs are child objects of the device object [DigitalInputGroup\_CN2](DigitalInputGroup_CN2-DeviceObjectI-067DF051.html).

Assignment of the device objects to the pins of connector **CN2**:

* DI1 represents pin **P6**
* DI2 represents pin **P7**
* DI3 represents pin **P8**
* DI4 represents pin **P9**.
