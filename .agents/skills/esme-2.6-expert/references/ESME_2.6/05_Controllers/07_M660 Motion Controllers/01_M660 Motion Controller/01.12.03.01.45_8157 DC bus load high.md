# 8157 DC bus load high

Diagnostic class: 2

| Device reactions | Default reaction | Minimum reaction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Drive | - | - |
| Power supply | D | E |

The overload of the DC bus is at 80 %. This is indicated by the parameter DC\_BusOverload. If the overload increases to 100 %, then the diagnostic message '[8146 DC bus overload](DCBusOverload-03C16F24.html)' is triggered.
