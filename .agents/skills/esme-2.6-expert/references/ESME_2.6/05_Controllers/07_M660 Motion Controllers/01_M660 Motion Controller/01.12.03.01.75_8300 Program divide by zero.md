# 8300 Program divide by zero

Diagnostic class: 4

A division by zero is to be executed in the program.

NOTE: The division by **zero** is defined for REAL and LREAL and hence does not generate the 8300 Program divide by zero diagnostic message. In this case, a non-numeric value is returned.

| Cause | Solution |
| --- | --- |
| The divisor was zero in a division function. | Verify divisors and avoid division by zero in the program. |
