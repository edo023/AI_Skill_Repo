# 8114 Device typeplate not readable

Diagnostic class: 3

| Device reactions | Default reaction | Minimum reaction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Drive | AD | AD |
| Power supply | AP | AP |

The electronic device type plate cannot be read or contains invalid data.

The nameplate contains for example the device type, serial number, alignment values, and so on.

| Cause | Solution |
| --- | --- |
| Detected hardware error: The device is inoperable. | * Contact your local Schneider Electric service representative. * Replace the servo amplifier. |
