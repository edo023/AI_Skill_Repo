# 8175 Extended message for encoder disturbance

Diagnostic class: 1

| Device reactions | Default reaction | Minimum reaction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Drive | E | E |
| Power supply | - | - |

This diagnostic message indicates potential issues with the Hiperface DSL Encoder.

This diagnostic message is only available for Lexium 62 Standard Plus and Lexium 62 Advanced Plus.

By default, this diagnostic message is only recorded in the drive logger.

EIO0000005527.01
