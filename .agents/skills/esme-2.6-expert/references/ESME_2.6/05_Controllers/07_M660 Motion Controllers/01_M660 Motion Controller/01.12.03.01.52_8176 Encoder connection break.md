# 8176 Encoder connection break

Diagnostic class: 2

| Device reactions | Default reaction | Minimum reaction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Drive | D | E |
| Power supply | - | - |

The physical connection to the encoder is interrupted.

| Cause | Solution |
| --- | --- |
| Physical connection to the encoder interrupted. | Verify correct physical connection to the encoder. |

The following diagnostic messages provide additional information on the effects of the physical connection interruption to the encoder communication:

* [8171 Encoder communication disturbance detected](EncoderCommunicationDisturbanceDete-03C2A51C.html)
* [8958 Encoder communication not possible](EncoderCommunicationNotPossible-0444B561.html)
* [8105 Encoder signal out of range](TPC_M660_Diagnostics_8105EncoderSig-03B03323.html)
* [8175 Extended message for encoder disturbance](ExtendedMessageForEncoderDisturbanc-03C383B5.html) (from drive logger)
