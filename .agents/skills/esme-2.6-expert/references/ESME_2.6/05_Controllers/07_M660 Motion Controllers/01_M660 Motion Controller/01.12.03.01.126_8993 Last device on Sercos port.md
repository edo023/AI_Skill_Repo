# 8993 Last device on Sercos port

Diagnostic class: 1

After a topology change to double line, this message is generated in addition to the diagnostic message '[8957 Sercos bus topology changed](SERCOSBusTopologyChanged-04428951.html)'.

`Px TAddr y`

`x` is the Port number and `y` is the TopologicalAddress
