# 8518 Sercos: Too many physical devices

Diagnostic class: 3

The number of physical drives on the Sercos bus exceeds the limit.

Ext. diagnosis: **HW <maximum number of Sercos slaves on the controller>/<Number of configured Sercos slaves>**

| Cause | Solution |
| --- | --- |
| Limited by the controller type:  The number of configured servo drives with the [WorkingMode](M660_UserFunct_GroupWorkingMode-44A54DB0.html#M660_UserFunct_GroupWorkingMode-44A54DB0) Real exceeds the maximum number for the controller type. | Reduce the number of servo drives with the [WorkingMode](M660_UserFunct_GroupWorkingMode-44A54DB0.html#M660_UserFunct_GroupWorkingMode-44A54DB0) Real to the limit supported by the controller by setting excess servo drives to the WorkingMode Virtual or by deleting them altogether. |
