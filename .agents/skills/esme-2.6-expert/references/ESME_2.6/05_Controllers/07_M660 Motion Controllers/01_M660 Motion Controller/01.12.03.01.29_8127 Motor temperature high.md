# 8127 Motor temperature high

Diagnostic class: 2

| Device reactions | Default reaction | Minimum reaction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Drive | D | E |
| Power supply | - | - |

* This diagnostic message is triggered as soon as the motor temperature reaches a value that is 5 degrees below the triggering threshold of diagnostic message '[8102 Motor overtemperature](TPC_M660_Diagnostics_8102MotorOvert-03AEF6AE.html)'.

  The motor temperature is measured either by a temperature sensor or by a thermal model.

  The MotorTemperatureMonitoring parameter is used to determine how the motor temperature is measured.
