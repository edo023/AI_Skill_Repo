# 8193 Machine encoder temperature high

Diagnostic class: 2

| Device reactions | Default reaction | Minimum reaction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Drive | D | E |
| Power supply | - | - |

The temperature inside the machine encoder is too high.

| Cause | Solution |
| --- | --- |
| Insufficient ventilation or ambient temperature too high. | Verify and correct or replace if necessary the device fans and ventilation slots (if available). |
| Verify ventilation in switching cabinet. | If installed, ensure that the air conditioning unit is functioning properly. |
| Detected hardware error: The temperature sensor is inoperable. | Replace the device. |
