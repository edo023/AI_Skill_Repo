# 8958 Encoder communication not possible

Diagnostic class: 3

| Device reactions | Default reaction | Minimum reaction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Drive | BD2 | CD |
| Power supply | - | - |

It has not been possible to read encoder data over the serial interface for 10 seconds. The drive is disabled.

| Cause | Solution |
| --- | --- |
| Incorrect encoder cable or incorrect wiring. | Verify correct wiring of the encoder with the correct encoder cable. |
| The 24 Vdc control voltage is too low. | Verify correct control voltage. |
| Encoder inoperative. | Replace the motor or the encoder. |

Diagnostics message '8171 Encoder comm. disturbance detected' is triggered as an alert before this error is detected.

The following diagnostics messages provide additional information on the effects of an interruption of the encoder communication:

* 8176 Encoder connection break
* 8105 Encoder position invalid
* 8175 Extended message for encoder disturbances (from drive logger)
