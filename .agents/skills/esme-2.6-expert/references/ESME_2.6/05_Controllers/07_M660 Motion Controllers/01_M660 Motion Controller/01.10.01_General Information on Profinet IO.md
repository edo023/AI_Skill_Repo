# General Information on Profinet IO

## PROFINET IO Controller Implementation

The PROFINET IO controller implementation for M660  controllers is based on the CODESYS PROFINET controller stack. For details, refer to the [CODESYS PROFINET online help](https://product-help.se.com/ESME/2.6/VLP_Software/VLP_Logic_Builder/VLP_Communication/VLP_Device_Editors/en-US/CODESYS_PROFINET?t=CODESYS_PROFINET%2Fmodules%2F662ea641f8505ddbe6aca34154daeecb.html&theme=Help).

refer to the CODESYS PROFINET online help.

Multiple-port connectivity is not available. The PROFINET IO controller requires a direct connection to a switch that needs to be configured as an external switch.

If the Profinet IO controller is connected to one of the dual-port connectors **CN20/CN21** or **CN22/CN23** of a double option card (**CN20**, **CN21**, **CN22** and **CN23**), do not connect PROFINET devices to the other dual-port connector. If you do so, the topology identification may detect errors.

For details on adding a PROFINET IO controller, refer to [Adding a PROFINET IO Controller](AddingAProfinetIOController-4CF960D6.html).

For details on configuring a PROFINET IO controller, refer to [CODESYS PROFINET, Configuration](https://product-help.se.com/ESME/2.6/VLP_Software/VLP_Logic_Builder/VLP_Communication/VLP_Device_Editors/en-US/CODESYS_PROFINET?t=CODESYS_PROFINET%2Fmodules%2F_pnio_f_master_configuration.html&theme=Help).
