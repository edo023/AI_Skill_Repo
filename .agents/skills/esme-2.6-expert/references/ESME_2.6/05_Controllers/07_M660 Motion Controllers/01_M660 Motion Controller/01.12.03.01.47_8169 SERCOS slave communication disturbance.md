# 8169 SERCOS slave communication disturbance detected

Diagnostic class: 2

| Device reactions | Default reaction | Minimum reaction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Drive | D | E |
| Power supply | D | E |

A temporary signal error on the Sercos bus has been detected. The MDT data may be incorrect due to this detected error. In this case, the data of the last cycle is reused.

Temporary detected errors can have the following causes:

* Error detected in the master synchronous telegram (MST)
* Error detected in the master data telegram (MDT)
* Error detected in the frame check sequence (FCS; checksum of the Ethernet frame)

| For the motor/drive system: | |
| --- | --- |
| Cause | Solution |
| Insufficient EMC. | * Verify the wiring. * Verify that neither the controller nor the connected equipment is subject to electromagnetic interference. |
