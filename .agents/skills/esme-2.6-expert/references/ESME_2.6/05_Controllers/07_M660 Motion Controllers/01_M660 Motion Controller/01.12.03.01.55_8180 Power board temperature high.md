# 8180 Power board temperature high

Diagnostic class: 2

| Device reactions | Default reaction | Minimum reaction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Drive | - | - |
| Power supply | D | E |

The temperature of the power board inside the housing is too high. If the temperature continues to rise, the diagnostic message '[8177 Power board overtemperature](PowerBoardOvertemperature-03C44524.html)' is triggered.

EIO0000005527.01
