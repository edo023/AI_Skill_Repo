# Fan Monitoring

## Activating Fan Monitoring

The function FC\_ActivateFanSupervision of the library M6xxPLCSystem allows you to enable monitoring of the fan of an M660 controller.

Fan monitoring of M660 controllers is not enabled by default. Use this function to enable fan monitoring of M660 controllers that are factory-equipped with a fan, or after you have installed a fan kit (commercial references MNAH6AFS01 and MNAH6AFS02).

If fan monitoring is enabled, an error is detected ([8075 "Controller fan out of range"](ControllerFanOutOfRange-56946041.html)) if the speed of rotation of a fan is not within the permissible range.

Do not disable fan monitoring of an M660 controller that you have equipped with an optional fan kit unless you have previously uninstalled the fan kit.

Do not disable fan monitoring of M660 controllers that are factory-equipped with a fan.

For details, refer to the M6xxPLCSystem library guide, [FC\_ActivateFanSupervision](../../../../../api/crossBook?lang=en-US&virtualBookName=M6xxPLCSystem&topicID=FC_ActivateFanSupervision_B51AD4CC).
