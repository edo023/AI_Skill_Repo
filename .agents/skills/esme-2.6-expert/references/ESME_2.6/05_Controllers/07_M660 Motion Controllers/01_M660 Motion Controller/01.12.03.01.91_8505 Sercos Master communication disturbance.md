# 8505 Sercos: Master communication disturbance detected

Diagnostic class: 2

A checksum error on the Sercos bus was detected. The data (target position and actual position) of the last Sercos cycle are used again.

| Cause | Solution |
| --- | --- |
| Insufficient EMC. | * Verify the wiring. * Verify that neither the controller nor the connected equipment is subject to electromagnetic interference. |
