# 8979 STO\_A and STO\_B different levels

Diagnostic class: 3

| Device reactions | Default reaction | Minimum reaction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Drive | AD | AD |
| Power supply | - | - |

This diagnostic message is triggered if the input `STO_A` and `STO_B` have different levels. Both inputs always have to have identical levels.

| Cause | Solution |
| --- | --- |
| The wiring of the inputs is incorrect. | Verify and correct if necessary the wiring of the inputs. |
