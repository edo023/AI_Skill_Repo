# Overview

## General

The **C2C Online View** is a tab of the **C2C Master** device editor.

The **C2C Online View** displays the C2C network configuration retrieved from the PacDrive LMC.

The displayed information is read-only, but can be filtered and printed.

NOTE: The **C2C Slave** device does not provide the **C2C Online View**.
