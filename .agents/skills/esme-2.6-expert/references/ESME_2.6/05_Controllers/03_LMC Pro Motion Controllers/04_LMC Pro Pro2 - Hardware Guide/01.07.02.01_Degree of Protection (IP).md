# Degree of Protection (IP)

## Overview

Install components such that a degree of protection corresponding to the actual operational environment is set up.

For more information on the degree of protection of the equipment, refer to [*Ambient Conditions*](D-SE-0051396.html#D-SE-0051396).

The following conditions may damage the components:

* Oil
* Moisture
* Electromagnetic interference
* Ambient temperature
* Metal dust deposits

| WARNING | |
| --- | --- |
|  | UNINTENDED EQUIPMENT OPERATION  * Observe and conform to ambient temperatures, storage temperatures and transport temperatures of the individual components as specified in the operating manuals of the components. * Prevent the formation of moisture during the operation, storage and transport of individual components. * Conform to the vibration and shock requirements specified for the equipment when operating, storing and transporting system equipment.  Failure to follow these instructions can result in death, serious injury, or equipment damage. |
