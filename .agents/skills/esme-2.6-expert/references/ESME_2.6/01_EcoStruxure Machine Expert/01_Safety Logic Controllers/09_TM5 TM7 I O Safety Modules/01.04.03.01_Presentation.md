# Presentation

## General Information

Digital and analog I/O modules convert process signals into the internal signal level required by the controller.

The TM5/TM7 Safety I/O modules can be used in safety-related applications according to:

* EN ISO 13849, up to PL e
* IEC 62061, up to SIL 3
* IEC 61508, up to SIL 3
