# Training

## Introduction

As stated in the IEC 61508, Part 1, App. B, all persons involved in a Safety Lifecycle activity must have the appropriate training, technical knowledge, experience, and qualifications relevant apply the products specified in the present document. Training, technical knowledge, experience, and qualifications should be assessed in relation to each particular application.

NOTE: Make sure you possess all information and skills required to install, run, and maintain Safety-Related Systems correctly.
