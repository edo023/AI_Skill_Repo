# bus base

A bus base is a mounting device that is designed to seat an electronic module on a DIN rail and connect it to the TM5 bus for Safe Logic Controllers. Each bus base extends the TM5 data and the power buses and the 24 Vdc I/O power segment. The electronic modules are added to the TM5 system through their insertion on the base bus. The bus base also supplies the articulation point for the terminal blocks.
