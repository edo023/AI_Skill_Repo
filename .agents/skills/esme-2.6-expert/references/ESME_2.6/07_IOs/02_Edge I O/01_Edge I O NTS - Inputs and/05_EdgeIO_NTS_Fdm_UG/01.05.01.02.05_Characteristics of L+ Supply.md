# Characteristics of L+ Supply

The following table describes the characteristics of L+ supply of the NTSFIO0400 module:

| Characteristics | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Maximum current | * Channel 0 : 250 mA * Channel 1 : 250 mA * Channel 2 : 200 mA * Channel 3 : 200 mA |
| Voltage | Depending on the power supply |
| Short circuit protection | Yes |

NOTE: L+ and L- form the 24 Vdc power supply for the IO-Link device.
