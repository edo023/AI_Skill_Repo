# General Overview

The range of Modicon Edge I/O NTS discrete modules includes:

* [Discrete Input Modules](#GeneralOverview-E0CB536A__DiscreteInputModules-E0CCF25A)
* [Discrete Output Modules](#GeneralOverview-E0CB536A__DiscreteOutputModules-E0CCF63A)

NOTE: References with an H are hardened devices, suitable for harsh environments.

NOTE: The terminal blocks are purchased separately. The compatible terminal block references is printed on the front of the module.
