# General Overview

The range of Modicon Edge I/O NTS analog modules includes:

* [Input modules](#GeneralOverview-B8066275__AnalogInputModules-DD1448B1)
* [Temperature Input modules](#GeneralOverview-B8066275__TemperatureInputModules-DD1448B1)
* [Output modules](#GeneralOverview-B8066275__AnalogOutputModules-DD144E53)

NOTE: References with an H are hardened devices, suitable for harsh environments.

NOTE: The terminal blocks are purchased separately. The compatible terminal block reference is printed on the front of the module.
