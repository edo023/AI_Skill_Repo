# 0x8702C0: Rotary Switches Changed During Run-time

Criticality level: Error module

MainFunction: IPAddressing

SubFunction: Rotary Switch

## Principle and Potential Causes

The rotary switches position was changed while in operation.

## Possible Solutions

Set the rotary switches to their initial positions.

## How to Clear the Error Code

Reboot your cluster.
