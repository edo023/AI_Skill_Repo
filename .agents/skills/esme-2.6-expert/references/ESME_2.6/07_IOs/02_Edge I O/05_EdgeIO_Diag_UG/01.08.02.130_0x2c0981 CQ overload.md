# 0x2C0981: CQ overload

Criticality level: Error channel

MainFunction: FDM

SubFunction: CQ

## Principle and Potential Causes

There is an overload on the CQ pin.

## Possible Solutions

Verify the device connected to the CQ pin.

## How to Clear the Error Code

Unplug and replug the device connected to the CQ pin.
