# 0x1D0384: Internal Field Power Error Detected

Criticality level: Error channel

MainFunction: IOM\_DIO

SubFunction: Q

## Principle and Potential Causes

An error is detected on the internal field power supply.

The internal field power supply is not present.

## Possible Solutions

Verify the internal field power supply and its connection.

## How to Clear the Error Code

The diagnostic ID automatically clears when the cause is resolved.
