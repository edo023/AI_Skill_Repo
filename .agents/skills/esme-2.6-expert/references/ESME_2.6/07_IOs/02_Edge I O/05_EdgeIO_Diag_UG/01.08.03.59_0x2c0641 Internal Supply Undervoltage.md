# 0x2C0641: Internal Supply Undervoltage Advisory

Criticality level: Advisory

MainFunction: FDM

SubFunction: Supply

## Principle and Potential Causes

The internal power supply voltage is below 18 V.

Potential causes:

* There is an undervoltage or an overload of the internal power supply.
* An internal error is detected.

## Possible Solutions

Unplug/plug the terminal.

If the advisory persists, contact your local Schneider Electric representative.

## How to Clear the Advisory Code

Not applicable.
