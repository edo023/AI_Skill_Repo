# 0x2C0741: Driver Over Temperature Advisory

Criticality level: Advisory

MainFunction: FDM

SubFunction: Temperature

## Principle and Potential Causes

The temperature of the transceiver is over 135 °C (275 °F).

Potential causes:

* Overtemperature
* Overload

## Possible Solutions

* Verify the ambient temperature and take appropriate action.
* Verify the load and take appropriate action.

## How to Clear the Advisory Code

The diagnostic ID automatically clears when the cause is resolved.
