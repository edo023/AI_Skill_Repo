# 0x1A0385: Hardware Error Detected

Criticality level: Error channel

MainFunction: IOM\_AIO

SubFunction: Q

## Principle and Potential Causes

A hardware error is detected on the indicated channel.

This can occur due to electromagnetic interferences.

## Possible Solutions

Clear the electromagnetic interference.

If the error persists, contact your local Schneider Electric representative.
