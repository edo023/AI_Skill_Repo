# 0x1B058F: SinCos Synchro Error

Criticality level: Error channel

MainFunction: IOM\_MOTION

SubFunction: Channel

## Principle and Potential Causes

The position cannot be calculated because there is a synchronization issue between the number of periods and the angle in the period.

The encoder frequency is over 400 kHz.

## Possible Solutions

Do not exceed 400 kHz encoder frequency.

## How to Clear the Error Code

Disable and enable the expert function.
