# 0x8101C0: Invalid Cybersecurity Rotary Switch Value Detected

Criticality level: Error module

MainFunction: Startup

SubFunction: RotarySwitch

## Principle and Potential Cause

The position of the cybersecurity switch is invalid.

## Possible Solutions

Turn the cybersecurity switch to a valid position.

If the error persists, contact your local Schneider Electric representative.

## How to Clear the Error Code

Reboot your cluster.
