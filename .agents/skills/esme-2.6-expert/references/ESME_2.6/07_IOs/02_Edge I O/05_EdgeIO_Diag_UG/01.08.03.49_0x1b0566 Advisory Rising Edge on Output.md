# 0x1B0566: Advisory Rising Edge on Output

Criticality level: Advisory

MainFunction: IOM\_MOTION

SubFunction: ExpertQ

## Principle and Potential Causes

A configured rising edge is not generated.

A configured rising edge, that was not generated, is overwritten.

## Possible Solutions

Ensure that the rising edge is generated before overwriting the rising edge value.

## How to Clear the Advisory Code

Disable and enable the expert function.
