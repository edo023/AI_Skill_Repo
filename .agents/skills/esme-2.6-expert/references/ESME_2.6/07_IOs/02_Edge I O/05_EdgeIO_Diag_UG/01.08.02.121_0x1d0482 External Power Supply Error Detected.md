# 0x1D0482: External Power Supply Error Detected

Criticality level: Error channel

MainFunction: IOM\_DIO

SubFunction: I

## Principle and Potential Causes

An error is detected on the external power supply.

The external power supply is not present.

## Possible Solutions

Verify the external power supply and its connection.

## How to Clear the Error Code

The diagnostic ID automatically clears when the cause is resolved.
