# 0x1B0559: Advisory Low Vpp

Criticality level: Advisory

MainFunction: IOM\_MOTION

SubFunction: Channel

## Principle and Potential Causes

The signals from the encoder are incorrect.

Potential causes:

* The wiring between the module and the encoder is incorrect or disconnected.
* The encoder is not supplied.
* The encoder is missing.

## Possible Solutions

* Verify the wiring between the module and the encoder.
* Verify if the encoder is connected and supplied.

## How to Clear the Advisory Code

The advisory is cleared on the rising edge of the WarningAck parameter.
