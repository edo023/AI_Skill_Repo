# 0x2C07C4: Module Temperature Above Critical Level (De-Energizing Outputs)

Criticality level: Error module

MainFunction: FDM

SubFunction: Temperature

## Principle and Potential Causes

The temperature of the microcontroller exceeds 95 °C (203 °F).

The module is in overload.

## Possible Solutions

Verify the ambient temperature and take appropriate action.

## How to Clear the Error Code

Not applicable.
