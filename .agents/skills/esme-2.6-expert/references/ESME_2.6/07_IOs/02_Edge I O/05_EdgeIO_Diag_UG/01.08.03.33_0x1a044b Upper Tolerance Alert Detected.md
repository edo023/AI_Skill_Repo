# 0x1A044B: Upper Tolerance Alert Detected

Criticality level: Advisory

MainFunction: IOM\_AIO

SubFunction: I

## Principle and Potential Causes

A upper tolerance alert is detected on the indicated channel.

The channel input value is above the maximum Range mode parameter value.

## Possible Solutions

* Verify and adjust the Range mode parameter to better align with your sensor specifications.
* Verify your installation and take appropriate action.

## How to Clear the Advisory Code

The diagnostic ID automatically clears when the cause is resolved.
