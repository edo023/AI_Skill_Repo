# 0x1F0602: Tunneling Response Timeout

Criticality level: Information

MainFunction: IOM\_HART

SubFunction: HART\_Tunneling

## Principle and Potential Causes

The command from the selected channel timed out.

The command is either not supported or contains incorrect data.

## Possible Solutions

Ensure that the command and the requested data are correct.

## How to Clear the Information

The diagnostic ID automatically clears when the cause is resolved.
