# 0x1B0585: Quadrature Error

Criticality level: Error channel

MainFunction: IOM\_MOTION

SubFunction: Channel

## Principle and Potential Causes

Signals from the incremental encoder are incorrect.

Potential causes:

* The wiring between the module and the encoder is incorrect.
* The wiring between the module and the encoder is damaged.
* The power supply to the encoder is non existent or insufficient.

## Possible Solutions

* Verify the wiring between the module and the encoder.
* Verify if the encoder is connected and supplied.

## How to Clear the Error Code

Disable and enable the expert function.
