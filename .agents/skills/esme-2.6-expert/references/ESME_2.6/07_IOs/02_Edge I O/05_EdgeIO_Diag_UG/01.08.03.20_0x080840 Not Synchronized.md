# 0x080840: Not Synchronized

Criticality level: Advisory

MainFunction: IOBusSynchro

SubFunction: Process

## Principle and Potential Causes

There is no synchronization between the I/O bus and the I/O modules.

## Possible Solutions

Wait for the synchronisation completion.

## How to Clear the Advisory Code

The diagnostic ID automatically clears when the cause is resolved.
