# 0x1B058B: Endat Protocol Error

Criticality level: Error channel

MainFunction: IOM\_MOTION

SubFunction: Channel

## Principle and Potential Causes

An internal error is detected on the encoder.

The encoder specifications are not met.

## Possible Solutions

Verify the encoder status and take appropriate action.

## How to Clear the Error Code

Disable and enable the expert function.
