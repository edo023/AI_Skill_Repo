# 0x2C0782: Driver Over Temperature Alert

Criticality level: Error channel

MainFunction: FDM

SubFunction: Temperature

## Principle and Potential Causes

The transceiver is over 150 °C (302 °F).

Potential causes:

* Overtemperature
* Overload

## Possible Solutions

* Verify the ambient temperature and take appropriate action.
* Verify the load and take appropriate action.

## How to Clear the Error Code

The diagnostic ID automatically clears when the cause is resolved.
