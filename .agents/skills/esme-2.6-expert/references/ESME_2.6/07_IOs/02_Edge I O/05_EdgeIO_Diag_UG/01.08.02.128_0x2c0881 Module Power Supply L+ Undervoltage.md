# 0x2C0881: Module Power Supply L+ Undervoltage

Criticality level: Error channel

MainFunction: FDM

SubFunction: I

## Principle and Potential Causes

There is an undervoltage or an overload on the L+ pin.

## Possible Solutions

* Verify the field power supply and take appropriate action.
* Verify the load on the L+ pin and take appropriate action.

## How to Clear the Error Code

The diagnostic ID automatically clears when the cause is resolved.
