# 0x2C01C2: Unable To Register Method ID Callback.

Criticality level: Error module

MainFunction: FDM

SubFunction: CONF

## Principle and Potential Causes

A Standardized Master Interface (SMI) method cannot be used.

An internal error is detected.

## Possible Solutions

Contact your local Schneider Electric representative.

## How to Clear the Error Code

Not applicable.
