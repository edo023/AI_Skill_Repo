# 0x020844: Unrecoverable Error Restarted Device

Criticality level: Advisory

MainFunction: FirmwareUpdate

SubFunction: Process

## Principle and Potential Causes

A previously detected unrecoverable error caused a restart of the device.

Potential causes:

* An error is detected by the firmware.
* The watchdog timer is expired.
* A hardware error is detected.

## Possible Solutions

Contact your local Schneider Electric representative.

## How to Clear the Advisory Code

Reboot your cluster.
