# 0x1B0592: Internal Error

Criticality level: Error channel

MainFunction: IOM\_MOTION

SubFunction: Channel

## Principle and Potential Causes

An internal error is detected on the motion controller.

## Possible Solutions

Contact your local Schneider Electric representative.

## How to Clear the Error Code

Perform a PTO reset (AdminType = 1)
