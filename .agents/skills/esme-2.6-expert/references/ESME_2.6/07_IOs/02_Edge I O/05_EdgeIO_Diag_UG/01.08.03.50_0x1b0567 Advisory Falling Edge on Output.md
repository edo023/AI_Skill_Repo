# 0x1B0567: Advisory Falling Edge on Output

Criticality level: Advisory

MainFunction: IOM\_MOTION

SubFunction: ExpertQ

## Principle and Potential Causes

A configured falling edge is not generated.

A configured falling edge, that was not generated, is overwritten.

## Possible Solutions

Ensure that the falling edge is generated before overwriting the falling edge value.

## How to Clear the Advisory Code

Disable and enable the expert function.
