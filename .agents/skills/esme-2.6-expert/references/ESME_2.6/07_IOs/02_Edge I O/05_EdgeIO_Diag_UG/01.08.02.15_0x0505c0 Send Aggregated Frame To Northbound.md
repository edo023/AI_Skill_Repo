# 0x0505C0: Send Aggregated Frame To Northbound Unsuccessful

Criticality level: Error module

MainFunction: ImplicitCommWithCtr

SubFunction: LinkWithCtr

## Principle and Potential Causes

The bus extender cannot send the implicit data exchange frames on northbound.

## Possible Solutions

Contact your local Schneider Electric representative.

## How to Clear the Error Code

Not applicable.
