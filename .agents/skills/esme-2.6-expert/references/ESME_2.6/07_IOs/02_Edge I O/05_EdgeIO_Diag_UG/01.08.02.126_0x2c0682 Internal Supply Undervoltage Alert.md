# 0x2C0682: Internal Supply Undervoltage Alert

Criticality level: Error channel

MainFunction: FDM

SubFunction: Supply

## Principle and Potential Causes

The internal voltage is below 9V.

Undervoltage or overload of internal supply.

## Possible Solutions

Disconnect the terminal.

## How to Clear the Error Code

The diagnostic ID automatically clears when the cause is resolved.
