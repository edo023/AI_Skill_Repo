# 0x2C06C4: Supply Undervoltage Alert

Criticality level: Error module

MainFunction: FDM

SubFunction: Supply

## Principle and Potential Causes

The internal field power supply voltage is below the correct range.

The power supply energizing the field power segment is insufficient.

## Possible Solutions

Verify that the power supply energizing the field power segment meets the minimum power supply requirements.

## How to Clear the Error Code

Reboot the cluster.
