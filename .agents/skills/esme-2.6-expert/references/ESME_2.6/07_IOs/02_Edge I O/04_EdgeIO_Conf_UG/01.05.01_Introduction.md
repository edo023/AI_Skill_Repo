# Introduction

There are several ways to configure the Modicon Edge I/O island, two of these are the following:

* Modicon Edge I/O Configurator software.
* Modicon Edge I/O NTS Web Interface.

NOTE: You can also configure Modicon Edge I/O natively with EcoStruxure Automation Expert - Motion and EcoStruxure Automation Expert software for specific controllers such as Modicon M262 and Soft dPAC.

These applications allow you to:

* Create and modify configuration files of your Modicon Edge I/O island, refer to [FILE Page](FileMenu-05FB9A43.html).
* Manage your islands, refer to [ISLAND MANAGEMENT Page](ISLANDMANAGEMENTMenu-078D6AFD.html).
* Manage your user rights, refer to [ISLAND SETUP Page](TPC_IslandSetupPage-F5F4642C.html).
* Perform diagnostics operations, refer to [DIAGNOSTICS Page](DIAGNOSTICSMenu-078D03B2.html).
* Reboot your island, refer to [MAINTENANCE Page](MAINTENANCEMenu-080D8686.html).
