# Diagnostics Status LEDs

Modicon Edge I/O 24 Vdc bus status, 24 Vdc field power, I/O status and channel states are displayed in direct relationship to the channels or the function.

Various states are displayed in different ways; for example, green signifies “OK” while red indicates a detected error.

Refer to the user guides of the Modicon Edge I/O modules for status LEDs descriptions.
