# Shielded Cables Connections

Cables carrying the analog I/O, counting I/O, motion I/O, encoders, networks and fieldbus communication signals must be shielded. The shielding must be securely connected to ground:

* The analog I/O, counting I/O, motion I/O, encoders, networks and fieldbus shields may be connected either to the functional ground (FE) of your system using an EMC kit or to the protective ground (PE).
* The bus communication cable shields must be connected to the protective ground (PE) with a connecting clamp secured to the conductive backplane of your installation.

| WARNING | |
| --- | --- |
|  | ACCIDENTAL DISCONNECTION FROM PROTECTIVE GROUND (PE)  * Do not use the EMC kit to provide a protective ground (PE). * Use the EMC kit only to provide a functional ground (FE).  Failure to follow these instructions can result in death, serious injury, or equipment damage. |
