# Maintenance Plan

Prior to any type of work on the system, establish a maintenance plan matching the information, the precautions and the processes observed in this manual.

Include the following points in the maintenance plan of your machine:

* Inspect the product for pollution or damage at regular intervals.
* Inspect all connection cables and connectors regularly for damage. Replace damaged cables immediately.

* Verify that all input and output elements are firmly seated.
* Tighten all mechanical and electrical threaded connections to the specified torque.
