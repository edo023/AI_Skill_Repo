# Functional Ground (FE) on the DIN Rail

The DIN Rail for your Modicon Edge I/O NTS is common with the functional ground (FE) plane and must be mounted on a conductive backplane.

| WARNING | |
| --- | --- |
|  | UNINTENDED EQUIPMENT OPERATION  Connect the DIN rail to the protective ground (PE) of your installation.  Failure to follow these instructions can result in death, serious injury, or equipment damage. |

The connection between the functional ground (FE) and your Modicon Edge I/O NTS is made by the DIN Rail contacts on the back of the bus base of the expansion modules.

For more information about the installation of the DIN rail, refer to [DIN Rail Installation](DINRailInstallation-24D67EED.html).
