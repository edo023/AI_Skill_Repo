## Altitude

The Modicon Edge I/O NTS modules are designed to operate with rated characteristics (current, power) at altitudes up to 2,000 m (6,562 ft).

The following table presents the maximum operating altitude with application of the multiplication factor on design according to IEC 61010-2-201 Table 6 (Electrical Safety Rules):

| Module range | Maximum installation altitude |
| --- | --- |
| NTSDA•••, NTSDR••• | 3,000 m (9,843 ft) |
| NTSDD•••, NTSA•••, NTSEHC•••, NTSN•••, NTSF•••, NTSPF•••, NTSDMY•••, NTSPCM••• | 5,000 m (16,404 ft) |

A derating applies on the maximum operating ambient temperature for modules that operate above 2,000 m (6,562 ft) altitude according to IEC 61131-2.
