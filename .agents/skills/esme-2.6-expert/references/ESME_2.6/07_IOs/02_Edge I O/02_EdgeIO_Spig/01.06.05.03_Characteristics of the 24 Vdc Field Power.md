# Characteristics of the 24 Vdc Field Power Supply

To distribute current for the 24 Vdc Field Power according to the [power distribution system](PowerDistributionSystemImplementati-3659C673.html#PowerDistributionSystemImplementati-3659C673), the Power supply Field and Bus and the Power supply Field Distribution module connector CN1 are supplied by an external Safety Extra-Low Voltage (SELV) power supply with the following characteristics:

| Characteristic | Value / Description |
| --- | --- |
| Nominal current | 10.5 A |
| Nominal voltage | 24 Vdc |
| Power interruption hold-up time on bus | - |

Use a certified, external 1P or 2P 10.5 A 230 V or equivalent fuse.

| WARNING | |
| --- | --- |
|  | POTENTIAL OF OVERHEATING AND FIRE  * Do not connect the modules directly to line voltage. * Use only isolating SELV power supplies to supply power to the modules.  Failure to follow these instructions can result in death, serious injury, or equipment damage. |
