# Communication Cables and Connectors

The communication cables are STP (Shielded Twisted Pair) CAT5e electrical cables with shielded RJ45 connectors. The following table describes the CAT5 cables to connect the network interface modules to an Ethernet network:

| Standard | Description | Maximum length | Application | Data rate | Connectors to the Fieldbus Interface |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 100Base-TX | 24-gauge, twisted pair | 100 m (328 ft) | Data transmission | 100 Mbit/s | 8-pin male |
