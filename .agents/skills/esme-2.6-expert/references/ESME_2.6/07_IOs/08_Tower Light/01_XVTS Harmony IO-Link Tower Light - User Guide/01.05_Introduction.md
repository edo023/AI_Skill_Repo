# Introduction

Harmony Pro XVTS IO-Link tower lights are industrial devices which provide visual and audible signals. They can be used to monitor the operating status of a machine, a workstation, a moving machine, a production island, and so on.

They use the IO-Link communication protocol, which allows you to configure the devices connected to your IO-Link network, and monitor them remotely using data exchanged over the network.

This chapter describes the characteristics of these XVTS tower lights and their integration into the Schneider Electric products environment.
