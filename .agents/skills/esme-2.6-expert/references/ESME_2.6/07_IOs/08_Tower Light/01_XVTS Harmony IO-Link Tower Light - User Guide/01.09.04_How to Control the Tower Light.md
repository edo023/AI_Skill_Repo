# How to Control the Tower Light

## Overview

To control your XVTS tower light, you have to send to the IO-Link Master a process data.

For more details, refer to:

* [Optical](Optical-B981535F.html)
* [Audible](Audible-B98155A1.html)

The way to send the process data depends on the IO-Link Master used.
