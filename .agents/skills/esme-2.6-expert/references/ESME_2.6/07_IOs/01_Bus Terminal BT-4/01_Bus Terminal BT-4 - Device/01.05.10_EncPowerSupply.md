Functional Description

Sets the supply voltage of the connected encoders.

The connected encoders receive their power supply from the bus terminal BT-4/ENC1. Here are three power supply options:

o5 Vdc: Incremental encoder

o24 Vdc: Incremental encoder

o8 Vdc: SinCos encoder

The 5 V and the 8 V are generated in the module. The 24 V have to be supplied by using an additional supply voltage connector. To protect the modules, the power supply of each encoder is limited to 300 mA with a fuse.

| Value | Data type | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Internal / 0 | BOOL | The supply voltage for the encoder is generated in the power supply for the BT-4/ENC1 (connector X1). |
| External / 1 | BOOL | Power for the encoder comes directly through connector X5 without any adjustments. |
