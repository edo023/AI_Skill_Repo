|  |  |

Functional Description

The parameter determines at which value of [SignalQuality](../../../../../../api/crossBook?lang=en-US&virtualBookName=MasterEnc_SinCos-14.htm#XREF_D_SE_0076252_1) a track monitoring error [8601 Master encoder signal out of the range](../../../PD.Diagnostic&topicID=D_SE_0064023_1) is detected and issued.
