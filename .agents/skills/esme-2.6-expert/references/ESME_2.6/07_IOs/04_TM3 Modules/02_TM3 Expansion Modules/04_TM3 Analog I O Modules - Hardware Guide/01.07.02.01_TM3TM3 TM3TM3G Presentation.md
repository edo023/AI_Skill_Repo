# TM3TM3 / TM3TM3G Presentation

## Overview

TM3TM3 (screw) and TM3TM3G (spring) analog expansion modules:

* 2 input channels 16 bits (Voltage, current, thermocouple, 3-wire-RTD)
* 1 output channel 12 bits (Voltage, current)
* Removable screw or spring terminal block
