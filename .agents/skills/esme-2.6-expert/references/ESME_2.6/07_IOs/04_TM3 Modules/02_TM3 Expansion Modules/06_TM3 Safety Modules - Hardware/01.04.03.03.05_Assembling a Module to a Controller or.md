Assembling a Module to a Controller or Receiver Module

| Step | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Remove all power and dismount any existing controller I/O assembly from its DIN mounting. |
| 2 | Remove the [expansion connector](../glossary/glossary.htm#XREF_D_SE_0024697_696) sticker from the controller or the outermost installed expansion module. |
| 3 | Verify that the [locking device](../../../../../../api/crossBook?lang=en-US&virtualBookName=tm3trrvhw&topicID=D_SE_0025794_7) on the new module is in the upper position. |
| 4 | Align the internal bus connector on the left side of the module with the internal bus connector on the right side of the controller, Receiver module or expansion module. |
| 5 | Press the new module towards the controller, Receiver module or expansion module until it is securely in place. |
| 6 | Push down the [locking device](../../../../../../api/crossBook?lang=en-US&virtualBookName=tm3trrvhw&topicID=D_SE_0025794_7) on the top of the new module to lock it to the controller, Receiver module or previously installed expansion module. |
