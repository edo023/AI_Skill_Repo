# Power-On Condition

## Description

When applying power to the safety module, the outputs are activated only if these four conditions are fulfilled:

* The [start condition](D-SE-0038326.html#D-SE-0038326) is valid.
* The safety-related conditions (safety-related inputs) indicate to activate the outputs.
* The internal [enable](D-SE-0039068.html#D-SE-0039068) condition is valid.
