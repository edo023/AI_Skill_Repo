# TM3DQ32TK Wiring Diagram

## Introduction

These expansion modules have two built-in HE10 (MIL 20) connectors for the connection of outputs and power supply.

## Wiring Rules

See [Wiring Best Practices](D-SE-0026685.html#D-SE-0026685).
