# TM3DI8 / TM3DI8G Presentation

## Overview

TM3DI8 (screw) and TM3DI8G (spring) digital expansion module:

* 8 channels
* 24 Vdc digital input
* 1 common line
* Sink/source
* Removable screw or spring terminal block
