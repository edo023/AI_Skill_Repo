# General Description

## Introduction

The range of TM3 expert I/O expansion modules includes:

* TeSys modules
* High speed counting modules

TeSys modules are used to manage starters within TeSys system architectures.

High speed counting modules are used for counting functions and position detection. Events are only supported by the TM3XFHSC202 / TM3XFHSC202G modules.
