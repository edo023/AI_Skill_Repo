# TM3DQ8R / TM3DQ8RG Presentation

## Overview

TM3DQ8R (screw) and TM3DQ8RG (spring) digital expansion module:

* 8 channels
* 2 A relay outputs
* 1 common line
* Removable screw or spring terminal block
