# Dimensions of Top Hat Section Rail DIN Rail

Dimensions of Top Hat Section Rail DIN Rail

You can mount the controller or receiver and its expansions on a 35 mm (1.38 in.) top hat section rail (DIN rail). It can be attached to a smooth mounting surface or suspended from a [EIA rack](../glossary/glossary.htm#XREF_D_SE_0024697_685) or mounted in a [NEMA](../glossary/glossary.htm#XREF_D_SE_0024697_324) cabinet.
