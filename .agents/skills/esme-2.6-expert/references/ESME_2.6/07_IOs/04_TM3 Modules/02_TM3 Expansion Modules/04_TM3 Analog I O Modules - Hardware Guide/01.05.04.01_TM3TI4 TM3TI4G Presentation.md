# TM3TI4 / TM3TI4G Presentation

## Overview

TM3TI4 (screw) and TM3TI4G (spring) analog expansion modules:

* 4 channels 16 bits (Voltage, current, isolated thermocouple, 3-wire-RTD)
* Removable screw or spring terminal block
