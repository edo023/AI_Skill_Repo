# TM3DM16R Presentation

## Overview

TM3DM16R digital expansion module:

* 8 channel 24 Vdc sink/source inputs
* 1 common line for inputs
* 8 channel 2 A relay outputs
* 2 common lines for outputs
* Removable screw terminal block
