# TM3AM6 / TM3AM6G Presentation

## Overview

TM3AM6 (screw) and TM3AM6G (spring) analog expansion modules:

* 4 input channels 12 bits (Voltage, current)
* 2 output channels 12 bits (Voltage, current)
* Removable screw or spring terminal block

## Model Name

TM3AM6 / TM3AM6G expansion modules may present a **model name**, respectively TM3AM6–B / TM3AM6G–B.

NOTE: The **model name** depends on the production site and does not impact the module characteristics.
