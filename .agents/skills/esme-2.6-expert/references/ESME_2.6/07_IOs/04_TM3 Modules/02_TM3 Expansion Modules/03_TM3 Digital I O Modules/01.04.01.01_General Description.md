# General Description

## Introduction

The range of TM3 digital I/O expansion modules includes:

* Input modules
* Output modules
* Mixed input/output modules

TM3 digital I/O expansion modules are equipped with (depending on the reference):

* Removable screw terminal blocks
* Removable spring terminal blocks
* HE10 (MIL 20) connectors

For modules with HE10 (MIL 20) connectors, a group of products known as Telefast 2 are available that enable these modules to be connected to sensors and actuators.
