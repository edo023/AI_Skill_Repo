# TM3DM32R Presentation

## Overview

TM3DM32R digital expansion module:

* 16 channel 24 Vdc sink/source inputs
* 2 common lines for inputs
* 16 channel 2 A relay outputs
* 4 common lines for outputs
* Removable screw terminal block
