# TM3DI16 / TM3DI16G Presentation

## Overview

TM3DI16 (screw) and TM3DI16G (spring) digital expansion module:

* 16 channels
* 24 Vdc digital input
* 1 common line
* Sink/source
* Removable screw or spring terminal block
