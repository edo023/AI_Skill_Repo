# TM3DQ8T / TM3DQ8TG Presentation

## Overview

TM3DQ8T (screw) and TM3DQ8TG (spring) digital expansion module:

* 8 channels
* 0.5 A source outputs
* 1 common line
* Removable screw or spring terminal block
