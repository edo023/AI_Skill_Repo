# TM3TI8T / TM3TI8TG Presentation

## Overview

TM3TI8T (screw) and TM3TI8TG (spring) analog expansion modules:

* 8 channels 16 bits (thermocouple, thermistor, resistor)
* Removable screw or spring terminal block
