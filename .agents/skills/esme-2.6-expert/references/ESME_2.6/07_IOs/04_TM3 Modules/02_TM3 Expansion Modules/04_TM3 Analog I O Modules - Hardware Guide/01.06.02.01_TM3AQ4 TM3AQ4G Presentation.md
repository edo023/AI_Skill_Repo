# TM3AQ4 / TM3AQ4G Presentation

## Overview

TM3AQ4 (screw) and TM3AQ4G (spring) analog expansion modules:

* 4 channels 12 bits (Voltage, current)
* Removable screw or spring terminal block

## Model Name

TM3AQ4 / TM3AQ4G expansion modules may present a **model name**, respectively TM3AQ4–B / TM3AQ4G–B.

NOTE: The **model name** depends on the production site and does not impact the module characteristics.
