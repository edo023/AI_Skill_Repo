# TM3AM6 / TM3AM6G Characteristics

## Introduction

This section provides a description of the electrical, the input/output characteristics of the TM3AM6 / TM3AM6G expansion modules.

See also [Environmental Characteristics](D-SE-0025238.html#D-SE-0025238).
