# CANopen Port

## Overview

The TM3 CANopen bus coupler is equipped with two isolated RJ45 ports (**CN1**, **CN2**) enabling easy daisy-chain configuration, as shown in following graphic:

![](images/T001664341.gif)

**\*** You can connect a maximum of 64 CANopen devices, master inclusive, without use of a repeater. Both ends of the CAN bus must be terminated by terminating resistor.

## Characteristics

This table describes the CANopen characteristics:
