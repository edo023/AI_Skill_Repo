# Modicon TM3 Bus Couplers

## Introduction

The Modicon TM3 Bus Couplers are devices designed to create distributed architectures in association with EtherNet/IP, TM3 / TM2 I/O modules. They are managed by a controller, through fieldbus:

* TM3BCEIP for EtherNet/IP and Modbus TCP
* TM3BCSL for Modbus Serial Line
* TM3BCCO for CANopen

For more details, see [Modicon TM3 Bus Couplers - Hardware Guide](../../../../../api/crossBook?lang=en-US&virtualBookName=tm3bchw&topicID=D_SE_0084312).
