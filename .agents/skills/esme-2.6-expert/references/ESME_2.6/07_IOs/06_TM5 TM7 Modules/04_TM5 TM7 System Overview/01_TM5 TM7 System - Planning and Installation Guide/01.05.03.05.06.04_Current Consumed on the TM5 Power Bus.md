# Current Consumed on the TM5 Power Bus

Current Consumed on the TM5 Power Bus

The controller generates 400 mA on the TM5 power bus to supply expansion slices. The TM5 power bus begins with the TM5SAI4L expansion module and terminates with the TM5SDO2R expansion module.

The total current consumed on the TM5 power bus is 642 mA, and exceeds the 400 mA capacity of the segment.

You must supplement the TM5 power bus by substituting the TM5SPS1F PDM with a TM5SPS2F type PDM .

The CPDM and the TM5SPS2F PDM provide 1576 mA (400 mA + 1176 mA) of current to the TM5 power bus.
