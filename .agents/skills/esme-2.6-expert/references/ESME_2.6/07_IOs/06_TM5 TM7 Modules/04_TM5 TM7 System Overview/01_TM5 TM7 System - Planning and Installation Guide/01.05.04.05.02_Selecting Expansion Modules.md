# Selecting Expansion Modules

Selecting Expansion Modules

When you plan a TM5 System layout, you need to know the number and type of expansion electronic modules and their matching bus bases and terminal blocks.

NOTE: There are restrictions and regulations associated with certain module types. Refer to the hardware guides.

Once the number and type of modules has been established, it becomes possible to determine [power distribution requirements](../TM5_-_Initial_Planning_for_TM5/TM5_-_Initial_Planning_for_TM5-9.htm#XREF_D_SE_0002047_1), your external [power source requirements](../TM5_-_Initial_Planning_for_TM5/TM5_-_Initial_Planning_for_TM5-15.htm#XREF_D_SE_0002306_4) and the overall hardware design.
