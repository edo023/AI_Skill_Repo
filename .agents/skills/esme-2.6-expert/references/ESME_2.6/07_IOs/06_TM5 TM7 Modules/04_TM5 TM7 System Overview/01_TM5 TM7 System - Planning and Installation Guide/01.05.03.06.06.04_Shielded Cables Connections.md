# Shielded Cables Connections

Shielded Cables Connections

Cables carrying the fast I/O, analog I/O, network and field bus communication signals must be shielded. The shielding must be securely connected to ground. The fast I/O and analog I/O shields may be connected either to the functional ground (FE) of your system via the TM2XMTGB grounding plate or to the protective ground (PE). The field bus communication cable shields must be connected to the protective ground (PE) with a connecting clamp secured to the conductive backplane of your installation.
