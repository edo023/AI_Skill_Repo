TM5 / TM7 Distributed I/Os Architecture

Introduction

The TM5 / TM7 System is an open system and can operate with the following open field bus standards:

o[CANopen](../glossary/glossary.htm#XREF_D_SE_0024697_650)

o[sercos](../glossary/glossary.htm#XREF_D_SE_0024697_593)

oEtherNet/IP

TM5/TM7 Mixed Distributed I/Os

In addition to your distributed configuration you can place remote I/Os at a distance up to 100 m (328.1 ft) and create a TM5 / TM7 mixed distributed I/Os [configuration](../glossary/glossary.htm#XREF_D_SE_0024697_659).
