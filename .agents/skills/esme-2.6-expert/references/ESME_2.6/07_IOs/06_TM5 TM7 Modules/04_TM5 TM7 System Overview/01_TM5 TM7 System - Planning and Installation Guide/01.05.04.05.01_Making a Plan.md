# Making a Plan

Making a Plan

Before you begin to install your TM5 System, you need to establish a plan that identifies:

othe type of enclosure for the TM5 System

othe number and type of expansion modules on your TM5 System

othe order in which any TM5 expansion modules are assembled together to form the TM5 bus

othe power requirements of your TM5 System configuration

oa coding scheme that helps match the terminal blocks with their electronic modules

oa labeling plan

The local TM5 bus is constructed as a series of interconnected bus base units. The structure of the TM5 backplane is defined by the type and order of electronic modules that will reside in it. You will need to make these decisions in advance and the association table can help you. There is a [color coding system](../Intro_-_Description_of_the_TM5_and_TM7_System/Intro_-_Description_of_the_TM5_and_TM7_System-4.htm#XREF_D_SE_0000758_1) in the TM5 System. In addition to [labeling your TM5 System](TM5_-_Flexible_TM5_System_Installation-14.htm#XREF_D_SE_0001023_1), you can also follow a [coding system](TM5_-_Flexible_TM5_System_Installation-13.htm#XREF_D_SE_0000888_1) of the electronic modules and terminal blocks.

NOTE: The safety-related I/O slices do not support the coding of the electronic modules.
