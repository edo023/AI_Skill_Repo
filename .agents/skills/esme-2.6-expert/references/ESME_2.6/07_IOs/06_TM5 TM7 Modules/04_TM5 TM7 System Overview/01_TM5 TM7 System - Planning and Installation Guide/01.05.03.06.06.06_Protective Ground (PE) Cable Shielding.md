# Protective Ground (PE) Cable Shielding

Protective Ground (PE) Cable Shielding

To ground the shield of a cable via a grounding clamp:

| Step | Description | |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Strip the shielding for a length of 15 mm (0.59 in.) | G-SE-0004794.1.gif-high.gif |
| 2 | Attach the cable to the conductive backplane plate by attaching the grounding clamp to the stripped part of the shielding as close as possible to the TM5 System base. | G-SE-0003362.1.gif-high.gif |

NOTE: The shielding must be clamped securely to the conductive backplane to help ensure good contact.
