# Current Supplied and Consumption Tables on the TM7 Power Bus

![](images/logo.png)

Current Supplied and Consumption Tables on the TM7 Power Bus

Introduction

During the configuration procedure of your TM7 System, the [TM5 Manager](../../../../../../api/crossBook?lang=en-US&virtualBookName=tm7prg&topicID=D_SE_0005138_2) calculates the probable current consumption of the TM7 power bus according to your design.

The following tables give a synthesis of the current supplied and current consumed on the TM7 power bus by the following TM5 / TM7 components:

oTM5SBET7 Transmitter module,

oTM7 expansion blocks (Field bus interface I/O blocks, I/O blocks and Power Distribution Block).

TM5SBET7 Transmitter Module

For information on the current supplied to the TM7 power bus, refer to [Supplying the TM7 Power Bus](../TM7_Part_-_Initial_Planning_for_TM7_System/TM7_Part_-_Initial_Planning_for_TM7_System-5.htm#XREF_D_SE_0009310_25).
