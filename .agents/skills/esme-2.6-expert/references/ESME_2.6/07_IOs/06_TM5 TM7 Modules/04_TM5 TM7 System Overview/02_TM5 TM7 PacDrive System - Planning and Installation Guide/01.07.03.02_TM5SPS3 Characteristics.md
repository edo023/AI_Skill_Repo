## Temperature De-rating

The Interface Power Distribution Module (IPDM) TM5SPS3 is subject to temperature restrictions depending on the current consumption on the TM5 power bus:

* up to 500 mA: 0...60°C (32...140°F)
* over 500 mA: 0...55°C (32...131°F)
