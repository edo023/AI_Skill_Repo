# Grounding Function

Grounding Function

The [DIN](../glossary/glossary.htm#XREF_D_SE_0024697_675) rail must be attached to a conductive backplane that itself is connected to a [protective ground (PE)](../TM5_-_Initial_Planning_for_TM5/TM5_-_Initial_Planning_for_TM5-17.htm#XREF_D_SE_0002601_9).

In the mounting channel of each piece of the TM5 equipment is a metal spring contact. When properly mounted on a metal DIN rail, these contacts provide connection to the [functional ground (FE)](../TM5_-_Initial_Planning_for_TM5/TM5_-_Initial_Planning_for_TM5-17.htm#XREF_D_SE_0002601_8) for the entire TM5 System.
