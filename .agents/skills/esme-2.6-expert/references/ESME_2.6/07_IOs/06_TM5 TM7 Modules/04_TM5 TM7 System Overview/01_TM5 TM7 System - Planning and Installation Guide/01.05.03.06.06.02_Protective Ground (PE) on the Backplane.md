# Protective Ground (PE) on the Backplane

Protective Ground (PE) on the Backplane

The protective ground ([PE](../glossary/glossary.htm#XREF_D_SE_0024697_501)) is connected to the conductive backplane by a heavy-duty wire, usually a braided copper cable with a cross-section of 6 mm2 ([AWG](../glossary/glossary.htm#XREF_D_SE_0024697_636) 10) or larger.
