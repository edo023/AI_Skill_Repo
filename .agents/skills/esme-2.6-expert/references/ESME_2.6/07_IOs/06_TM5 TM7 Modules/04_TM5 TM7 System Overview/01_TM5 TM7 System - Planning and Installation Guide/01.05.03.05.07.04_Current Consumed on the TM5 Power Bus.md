# Current Consumed on the TM5 Power Bus

Current Consumed on the TM5 Power Bus

The TM5SBER2 Receiver module generates 1156 mA on the TM5 power bus to supply remote expansion I/Os. The TM5 power bus begins with the TM5SBER2 remote expansion module and terminates with the TM5SDO2R remote expansion module.

The total current consumed on the TM5 power bus is 544 mA, and does not exceed the 1156 mA capacity of the TM5 power bus.
