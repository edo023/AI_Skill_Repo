# Functional Ground (FE) on the DIN Rail

Functional Ground (FE) on the DIN Rail

The DIN Rail for your TM5 System is common with the functional ground ([FE](../glossary/glossary.htm#XREF_D_SE_0024697_704)) plane and must be mounted on a conductive backplane.

|  |
| --- |
| Warning_Color.gifWARNING |
| UNINTENDED EQUIPMENT OPERATION |
| Connect the DIN rail to the functional ground (FE) of your installation. |
| Failure to follow these instructions can result in death, serious injury, or equipment damage. |

The connection between the functional ground (FE) and your TM5 System is made by the [DIN Rail contacts](../SPIG_TM5_TM7_-_Basics_of_the_TM5_System/SPIG_TM5_TM7_-_Basics_of_the_TM5_System-5.htm#XREF_D_SE_0015379_5) on the back of the controller and the bus base of the expansion modules.
