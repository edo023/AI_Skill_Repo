# Current Supplied and Consumption Tables on the 24 Vdc I/O Power Segment

![](images/logo.png)

Current Supplied and Consumption Tables on the 24 Vdc I/O Power Segment

Introduction

During configuration procedure of your TM5 System, the [TM5 Manager](../../../../../../api/crossBook?lang=en-US&virtualBookName=tm5prg&topicID=D_SE_0005138_1) calculates the probable current consumption of each 24 Vdc power segment according to your design.

The following tables give a synthesis of the current supplied and current consumed on the 24 Vdc I/O power segment by the following TM5 components:

oControllers

oCompact I/O

oElectronic modules

The bus bases do not draw current from the 24 Vdc I/O power segment.
