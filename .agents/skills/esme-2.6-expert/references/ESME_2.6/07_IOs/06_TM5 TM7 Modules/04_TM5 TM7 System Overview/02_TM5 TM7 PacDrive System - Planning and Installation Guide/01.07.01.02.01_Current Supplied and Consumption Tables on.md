# Current Supplied and Consumption Tables on the 24 Vdc I/O Power Segment

## Introduction

The following tables give a synthesis of the current supplied and current consumed on the 24 Vdc I/O power segment by the following TM5 System components:

* Compact I/O
* Electronic modules

The bus bases do not draw current from the 24 Vdc I/O power segment.
