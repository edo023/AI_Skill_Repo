# Current Supplied and Consumption Tables on the 24 Vdc I/O Power Segment

## Introduction

The following tables give a synthesis of the current supplied and current consumed on the 24 Vdc I/O power segment by the TM7 System blocks.

NOTE: There is no current supplied or consumed by the TM7 Power Distribution Block (PDB) on the 24 Vdc I/O power segment.
