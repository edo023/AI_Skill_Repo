The following figure shows the wiring diagram for the TM5SPDG5D4F:

![G-SE-0003202.3.gif-high.gif](images/G-SE-0003202.3.gif-high.gif)

1   Internal electronics

2   24 Vdc I/O power segment integrated into the bus bases

3   Integrated fuse type T slow-blow 6.3 A 250 V exchangeable

4   PS1: External isolated power supplies 24 Vdc

NOTE: Connect the 0 Vdc power circuits together and to the functional ground (FE) of your system to meet the EMC requirements.
