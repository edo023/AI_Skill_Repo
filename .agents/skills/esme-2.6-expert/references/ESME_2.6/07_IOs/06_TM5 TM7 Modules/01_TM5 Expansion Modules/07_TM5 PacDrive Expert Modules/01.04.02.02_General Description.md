Introduction

The range of expert expansion [electronic modules](../glossary/glossary.htm#XREF_D_SE_0024697_686) includes the High Speed Counter ([HSC](../glossary/glossary.htm#XREF_D_SE_0024697_574)) electronic modules.

HSC Electronic Module Features

The HSC electronic modules are used for counting [functions](../glossary/glossary.htm#XREF_D_SE_0024697_714) and position detection.
