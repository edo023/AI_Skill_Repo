# Actuator Supply

Actuator Supply

The following table provides the actuator supply of the 4Out electronic module:

| supply | |
| --- | --- |
| Voltage | Power segment supply less voltage drop for internal protection. |
| Voltage drop for internal protection at 500 mA | 2 Vdc max |
| Actuator supply current (for all powered connected actuators) | 500 mA |
| Internal protection | Overload short circuit |
