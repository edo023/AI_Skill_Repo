# Definition

Definition

[Hot swapping](../glossary/glossary.htm#XREF_D_SE_0007605_258) is the ability to remove an I/O electronic module from its [bus base](../glossary/glossary.htm#XREF_D_SE_0007605_187) and then replace it with an identical [electronic module](../glossary/glossary.htm#XREF_D_SE_0007605_217) while the TM5 System is under power without disrupting the normal operations of the [controller](../glossary/glossary.htm#XREF_D_SE_0007605_125). When the electronic module is returned to its bus base or replaced with another electronic module with the same reference, it starts to operate again.
