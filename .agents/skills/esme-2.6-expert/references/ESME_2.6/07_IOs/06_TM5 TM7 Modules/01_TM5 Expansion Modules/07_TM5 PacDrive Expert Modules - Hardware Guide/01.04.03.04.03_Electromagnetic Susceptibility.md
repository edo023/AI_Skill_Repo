| Fast transients burst | IEC/EN 61000-4-4 | Power lines: 2 kV  [I/O](../glossary/glossary.htm#XREF_D_SE_0024697_469): 1 kV  Shielded cable: 1 kV  Repetition rate: 5(1) and 100 KHz |
