# Introduction

Introduction

The range of TM7 Digital I/O blocks includes:

• [Digital input](../glossary/glossary.htm#XREF_D_SE_0024697_674) blocks

• [Digital output](../glossary/glossary.htm#XREF_D_SE_0024697_674) blocks

• Digital mixed input/output blocks

The TM7 Digital I/O blocks need to be associated with IP67 power cables, TM7 bus cables and I/O cables.
