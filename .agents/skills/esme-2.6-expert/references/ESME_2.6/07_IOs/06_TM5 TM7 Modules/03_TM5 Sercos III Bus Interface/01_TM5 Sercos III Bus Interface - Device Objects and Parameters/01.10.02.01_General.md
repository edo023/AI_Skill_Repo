# General

## Description

Bus transmitter, supply feed 24 Vdc

Transmitter module 24 Vdc for data transmission on the TM5 data bus

## Configuration Register

| Register | Meaning | Default | Data type |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 0 | [Module status](D-SE-0070210.html#D-SE-0070210) | – | USINT |
| 2 | [Bus supply current](D-SE-0070211.html#D-SE-0070211) | – | USINT |
| 4 | [Bus supply voltage](D-SE-0070212.html#D-SE-0070212) | – | USINT |

For further information, see the TM5 Receiver and Transmitter Modules Hardware guide.
