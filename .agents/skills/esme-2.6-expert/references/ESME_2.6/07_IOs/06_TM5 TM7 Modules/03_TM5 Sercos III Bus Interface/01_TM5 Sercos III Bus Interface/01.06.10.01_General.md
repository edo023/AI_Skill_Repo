# General

## Description

Eight digital inputs 24 Vdc, sink; four outputs 24 Vdc / 0.5 A, source

Digital I/O-module 24 Vdc with 12 digital inputs (sink, 1-wire, type 1) and four digital outputs (source, 1-wire, transistor)

## Configuration Register

| Register | Meaning | Default | Data type |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 0 | [Digital inputs 1–8](D-SE-0070331.html#D-SE-0070331) | – | USINT |
| 2 | [Digital outputs 1–4](D-SE-0070332.html#D-SE-0070332) | – | USINT |
| 18 | [Digital input filter](D-SE-0070333.html#D-SE-0070333) | 20 | USINT |
| 30 | [Status of digital outputs 1–4](D-SE-0070334.html#D-SE-0070334) | 0 | – |

For further information, see the TM5 digital I/O modules hardware manual.
