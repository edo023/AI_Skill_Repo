# Regular Digital Inputs

## General

The regular digital input states of the configuration profile TM5SDM8DTS\_DIHFOO can be read from the [I/O mapping channel](D-SE-0069002.html#D-SE-0069002) DigitalInput01-08.

Each bit represents one physical channel on the module.

The following bits represent the values of the four inputs:

* Bit 0: physical channel 1
* Bit 1: physical channel 2
* Bit 4: physical channel 5
* Bit 5: physical channel 6

The bits 2, 3, 6 and 7 of the I/O mapping channel DigitalInput01-08 are reserved for system use.
