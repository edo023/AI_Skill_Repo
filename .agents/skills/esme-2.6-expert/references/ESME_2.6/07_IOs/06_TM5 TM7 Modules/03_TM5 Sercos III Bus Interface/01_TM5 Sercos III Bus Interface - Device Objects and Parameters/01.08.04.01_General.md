# General

## Description

4 DI; 4 DI; 4 DI; 4 DO; 4 DO

Compact I/O module 24 Vdc with 3x4 digital inputs and 2x4 digital outputs

The module consists of the following five submodules:

* Three submodules with four digital inputs
* Two submodules with four digital outputs
