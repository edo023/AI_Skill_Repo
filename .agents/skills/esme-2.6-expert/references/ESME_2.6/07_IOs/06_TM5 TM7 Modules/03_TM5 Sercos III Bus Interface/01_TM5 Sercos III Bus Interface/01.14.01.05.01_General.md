# General

## Description

Safety digital mixed module, 24 Vdc

* Six safety-related inputs
* Six safety-related test outputs
* Two safety-related transistor outputs with current monitoring, 24 Vdc, 500 mA
* Configurable input filter, 0...500 ms

## Configuration

| Tab | Meaning |
| --- | --- |

| [User Parameter](D-SE-0070429.html#D-SE-0070429) | User-defined parameters |
