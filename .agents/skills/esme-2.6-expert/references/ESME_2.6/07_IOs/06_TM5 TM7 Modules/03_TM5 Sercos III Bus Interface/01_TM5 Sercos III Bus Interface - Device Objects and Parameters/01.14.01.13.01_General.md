# General

## Description

Safety digital mixed module, 24 Vdc, M12 connectors, IP67

* Eight safety-related digital inputs, input filter configurable
* Eight safety-related test outputs
* Four safety-related digital transistor outputs, 24 Vdc, 2 A
* Integrated over-current protection and inductive load resistance
