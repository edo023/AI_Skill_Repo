# TM5 Compact I/O Modules

## Supported Submodules

Each of the compact modules consists of five submodules.

The following types of submodules can be used for this purpose:

* Submodule with four digital inputs
* Submodule with six digital inputs
* Submodule with 12 digital inputs
* Submodule with four digital outputs
* Submodule with six digital outputs
* Submodule with six digital relay outputs
* Submodule with four analog inputs
* Submodule with two analog outputs
