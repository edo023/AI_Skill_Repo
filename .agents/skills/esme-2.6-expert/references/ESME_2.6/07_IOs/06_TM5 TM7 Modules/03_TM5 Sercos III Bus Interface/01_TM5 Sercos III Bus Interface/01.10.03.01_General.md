# General

## Description

Bus transmitter, supply feed 24 Vdc, X67 supply

Transmitter module 24 Vdc for data transmission on the TM5 data bus with IP67 power supply 6 W

## Configuration Register

| Register | Meaning | Default | Data type |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 0 | [Module status](D-SE-0070214.html#D-SE-0070214) | – | USINT |
| 2 | [TM7 bus supply current](D-SE-0070215.html#D-SE-0070215) | – | USINT |
| 4 | [TM7 bus supply voltage](D-SE-0070216.html#D-SE-0070216) | – | USINT |

For further information, see the TM5 Receiver and Transmitter Modules Hardware guide.
