# General

## Description

12 DI; 12 DI; 6 RO; 6 RO

Compact I/O module 24 Vdc with 2x12 digital inputs and 2x6 digital relay outputs

The module consists of the following five submodules:

* Two submodules with 12 digital inputs
* Two submodules with six digital relay outputs
* One dummy submodule which cannot be configured
