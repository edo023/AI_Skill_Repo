# General

## Replacement for VBO04S00 PacNet Bus Terminal 16 DI / 16 DO (BT-4 DIO) Module

The TM5SDM8DTS module can be used as a functional substitute for the BT-4 DIO module and provides additional new functionalities.

NOTE: Aside from the flexibility afforded by the finer I/O count of the TM5SDM8DTS module, there may be some applicative differences between the TM5SDM8DTS and VBO04S00 modules. If you substitute one for the other, ensure that you validate your application.
