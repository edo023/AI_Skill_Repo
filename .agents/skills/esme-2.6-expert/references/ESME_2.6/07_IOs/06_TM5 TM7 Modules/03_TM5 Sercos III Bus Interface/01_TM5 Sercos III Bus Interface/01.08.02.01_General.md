# General

## Description

6 DI; 6 DI; 6 DO; 4 AI; 2 AO

Compact I/O module 24 Vdc with 2x6 digital inputs, 1x6 digital outputs, 1x4 analog inputs and 1x2 analog outputs

The module consists of the following five submodules:

* Two submodules with six digital inputs
* One submodule with six digital outputs
* One submodule with four analog inputs
* One submodule with two analog outputs
