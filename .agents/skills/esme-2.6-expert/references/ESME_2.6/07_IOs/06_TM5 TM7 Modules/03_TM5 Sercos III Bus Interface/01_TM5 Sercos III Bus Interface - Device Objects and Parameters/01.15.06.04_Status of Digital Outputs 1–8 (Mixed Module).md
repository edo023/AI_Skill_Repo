# Status of Digital Outputs 1–8 (Mixed Module)

## General

On the module, the output states of the outputs defined by the output mask are compared to the reference state. The control of an output driver is used as a reference state.

A change of the default state causes the reset of the monitoring of this output. The state of each single channel can be read out. A change of the monitoring state is sent actively as a detected error message.

| --- | --- |
