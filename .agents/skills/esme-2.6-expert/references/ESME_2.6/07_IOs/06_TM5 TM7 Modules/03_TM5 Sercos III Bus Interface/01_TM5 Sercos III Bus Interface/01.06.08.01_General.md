# General

## Description

12 digital inputs 24 Vdc, sink, IEC 61131-2, type 1

Digital input module 24 Vdc with 12 digital inputs (sink, 1-wire, according to IEC 61131-2 type 1)

## Configuration Register

| Register | Meaning | Default | Data type |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 0 | [Digital inputs 1–8](D-SE-0070327.html#D-SE-0070327) | – | USINT |
| 1 | [Digital inputs 9–12](D-SE-0070328.html#D-SE-0070328) | – | USINT |
| 18 | [Digital input filter](D-SE-0070329.html#D-SE-0070329) | 10 | USINT |

For further information, see the TM5 digital I/O modules hardware manual.
