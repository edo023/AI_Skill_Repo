# General

## Description

Bus receiver, infeed 24 Vdc, and bus

Receiver module 24 Vdc for data reception on the TM5 data bus and power distribution (TM5 power bus, 24 Vdc I/O power segment)

## Configuration Register

| Register | Meaning | Default | Data type |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 0 | [Module status](D-SE-0070206.html#D-SE-0070206) | – | USINT |
| 2 | [Bus supply current](D-SE-0070207.html#D-SE-0070207) | – | USINT |
| 4 | [Bus supply voltage](D-SE-0070208.html#D-SE-0070208) | – | USINT |

For further information, see the TM5 Receiver and Transmitter Modules Hardware guide.
