# General

## Description

12 DI; 12 DI; 6 DO; 6 DO; 6 DO

Compact I/O module 24 Vdc with 2x12 digital inputs and 3x6 digital outputs

The module consists of the following five submodules:

* Two submodules with 12 digital inputs
* Three submodules with six digital inputs

For further information, see the TM5 compact I/O modules hardware manual.
