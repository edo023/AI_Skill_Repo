# Status of Digital Outputs 9-12

| --- | --- |

| 1 | 0 = Output 10: no detected error  1 = Output 10: short circuit or overload |
| 2 | 0 = Output 11: no detected error  1 = Output 11: short circuit or overload |
| 3 | 0 = Output 12: no detected error  1 = Output 12: short circuit or overload |
