# General

## Description

Safety digital mixed module, 24 Vdc

* Two safety-related inputs
* Two safety-related test outputs
* Two safety-related relay outputs, normally open contacts, 48 Vac / 6 A, 24 Vdc / 6 A
* Configurable input filter can be set by channel
