# TM2DDI8DT

![](images/logo.png)

TM2DDI8DT

Introduction

This expansion module is an 8-point, 24 Vdc input module with a terminal block.

For further hardware information, refer to [TM2DDI8DT](../../../../../../api/crossBook?lang=en-US&virtualBookName=tm2diohw&topicID=D_RU_0004760_1).
