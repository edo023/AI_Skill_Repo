# Connecting the TM2DDI32DK Module

![](images/logo.png)

Connecting the TM2DDI32DK Module

Introduction

TM2DDI32DK is a 32-channel, 24 Vdc input module.

This module is fitted with HE10 connector for the connection of inputs and power supply.

Wiring Rules

See [Wiring Requirements](../Modules_General_Overview/Modules_General_Overview-12.htm#XREF_D_RU_0004606_1).
