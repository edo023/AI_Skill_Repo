# Message MS2000: The drive is not assigned to a power circuit.

## Overview

| Message ID | Message description | Message type |
| --- | --- | --- |
| MS2000 | The drive is not assigned to a power circuit. | Information |

## Cause of the Message

The drive has not yet been assigned to a power circuit.

## Effect

Power calculations for this drive cannot be performed.

## Solution

Assign the drive to a power circuit in the Power Circuit  editor.
