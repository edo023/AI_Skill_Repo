# Message MS1202: The selected hardware cannot be operated under the specified environmental conditions.

## Overview

| Message ID | Message description | Message type |
| --- | --- | --- |
| MS1202 | The selected hardware cannot be operated under the specified environmental conditions. | Advisory |

## Cause of the Message

In your Motion Sizer project, you entered values for temperature and operating altitude of your motor, drive, or gearbox in the Environment  editor. These environmental conditions are not supported by the motor, drive, or gearbox you selected.

## Effect

The selected motor, drive, or gearbox cannot be operated under these environmental conditions.

## Solution

Select another motor, drive, or gearbox from the **Device selection** that supports your environmental conditions. Alternatively, adapt the environmental conditions, for example by using cooling devices in a cabinet.
