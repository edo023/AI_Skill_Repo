# Definition of Terms Concerning Inertia

## Overview

Motion Sizer calculates moments of inertia for different shaft positions in the mechanic.

## Definitions

| Term | Explanation |
| --- | --- |
| Load inertia | The total moment of inertia which is closer to the load than the given point on the shaft (sum of moments of inertia from the mechanics and from device characteristics). |
| Motor inertia | The total moment of inertia which is closer to the motor than the given point on the shaft (sum of moments of inertia from the mechanics and from device characteristics). |
| Inertia ratio | The ratio of load inertia and motor inertia (load inertia divided by motor inertia). |
