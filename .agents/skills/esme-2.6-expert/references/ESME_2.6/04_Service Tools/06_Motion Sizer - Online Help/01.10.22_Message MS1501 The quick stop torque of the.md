# Message MS1501: The quick stop torque of the gearbox is exceeded.

## Overview

| Message ID | Message description | Message type |
| --- | --- | --- |
| MS1501 | The quick stop torque of the gearbox is exceeded. | Advisory |

## Cause of the Message

Motion Sizer calculations have shown that the quick stop torque of the selected gearbox does not meet the requirements of the application.

## Effect

If you design your motion process according to the parameters you entered, the quick stop torque of the gearbox is insufficient.

## Further Information

The message MS1501 is displayed together with the messages MS1503 and MS1505. It indicates that the highest peak level, that is above peak torque and the rated torque, is exceeded.

## Solution

Select a gearbox with a greater Gearbox quick stop torque  value from the Device selection  editor.

Adapt the requirements of the application:

* Reduce the load.
* Reduce the inertia.
* Reduce the acceleration and deceleration requirements.
