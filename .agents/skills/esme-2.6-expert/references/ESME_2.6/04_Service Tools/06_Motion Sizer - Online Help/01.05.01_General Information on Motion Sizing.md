# General Information on Motion Sizing

## Why Is Motion Sizing So Fundamental?

Motion sizing describes the process of selecting drive components with optimum characteristics for your given machine application.

The objective of motion sizing is to identify those drive components (drive, motor, and gearbox) that fit your machine application. This has the following benefits:

* Minimizing energy consumption.
* Increasing reliability of the machine (reducing maintenance and increasing accuracy).
* Reducing the hardware size.
* Reducing the hardware costs.
* Reducing the waste heat.

As a precondition for successful motion sizing, you have to analyze the motion requirements of your machine application.

## How Can the Motion Sizer Software Help in Motion Sizing?

The Motion Sizer software helps you to formalize the requirements of your machine application. You can enter the characteristics of your application, starting at the load side. Motion Sizer then compares the characteristics of the Schneider Electric products with the requirements on the drive side and helps you select components to be used in your application.

NOTE: Motion Sizer can only provide results depending on the parameters you enter. You have to test and verify the results carefully. Perform an in-depth system planning that includes safety-related aspects of your application.
