# Mechanic

## Overview

Motion Sizer allows you to model the mechanic of an axis. It consists of a load case and an additional transformation. The load case defines the part of the mechanic that is directly connected to the load. The additional transformation is an additional transformation between the load and the gearbox or motor. The mechanical information that you enter is used as a basis to calculate the requirements and to identify suitable components.
