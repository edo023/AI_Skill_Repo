# Message MS1508: The maximum speed of the motor is exceeded.

## Overview

| Message ID | Message description | Message type |
| --- | --- | --- |
| MS1508 | The maximum speed of the motor is exceeded. | Advisory |

## Cause of the Message

Motion Sizer calculations have shown that the maximum speed of rotation that the motor can provide does not meet the requirements of the application.

## Effect

If you designed your motion process according to the parameters you entered, the motor would not provide a sufficient maximum speed of rotation.

## Solution

Use a gearbox.

Reduce the acceleration and deceleration requirements.
