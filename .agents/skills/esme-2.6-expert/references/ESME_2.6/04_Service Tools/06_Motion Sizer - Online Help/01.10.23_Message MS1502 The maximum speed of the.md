# Message MS1502: The maximum speed of the gearbox is exceeded.

## Overview

| Message ID | Message description | Message type |
| --- | --- | --- |
| MS1502 | The maximum speed of the gearbox is exceeded. | Advisory |

## Cause of the Message

Motion Sizer calculations have shown that the maximum speed of rotation that the gearbox can take up on the motor side due to its specification does not meet the requirements of the application.

## Effect

If you designed your motion process according to the parameters you entered, the gearbox would not support the maximum speed of rotation on the motor side.

## Solution

Select another gearbox in the Device selection  editor.

Reduce the speed in the motion profile.
