# Message MS2070: The power circuit is not controlled from power supply.

## Overview

| Message ID | Message description | Message type |
| --- | --- | --- |
| MS2070 | The power circuit is not controlled from power supply. | Information |

## Cause of the Message

In the power circuit, a power supply is used that does not control the power circuit.

## Effect

Motion Sizer cannot calculate the maximum speed of the drives because, without a power circuit controlled by the power supply, the voltage in the power circuits drops when one of the connected drives accelerates.

## Solution

If a stable power supply is required in your application, use separate power circuits for the power supplies that do not control the power circuit.
