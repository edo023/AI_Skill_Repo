# General Tab of the Controller Selection Dialog

## Overview

To open the dialog Controller selection, click one of the buttons Read from... or Write to... a controller on the Manage images dialog. The Controller selection dialog appears in one of the variants described in this chapter. By default, the General tab is selected. This tab includes the EcoStruxure Machine Expert/EcoStruxure Automation Expert - Motion Network Device Identification function and allows you to [configure the controller access options](D-SE-0031850.html#D-SE-0031850).

To connect to a PacDrive M controller, click the PacDriveM tab. The specific settings for PacDrive M controllers are described in the chapter [*Description of the Controller Selection Dialog for PacDrive M Controllers*](D-SE-0031849.html#D-SE-0031849).

## Images on CF Card, SD Card, or USB Mass Storage Device

Images are read from or written to a CF card (PacDrive M controllers), an SD card (Modicon M221, M241, M251 Logic Controllers) or to a USB mass storage device (Modicon M258 Logic Controller, Modicon LMC058 Motion Controller). Configure your controller for this operation as described in the documentation of your controller.

In the Drive selection dialog, select the drive of the SD card or USB mass storage device and click the Reading or Writing button.
