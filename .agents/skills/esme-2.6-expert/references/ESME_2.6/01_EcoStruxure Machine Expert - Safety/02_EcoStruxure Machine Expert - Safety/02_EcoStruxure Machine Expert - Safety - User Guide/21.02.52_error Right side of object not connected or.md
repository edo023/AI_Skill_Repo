# Error: Right side of object not connected or illegally connected (e.g. contact on right power rail!)

This error may have one of the following reasons:

* A network is not complete because, for example, a contact is not connected at its right side.
* Two objects are connected that do not match syntactically. This can be, for example, a contact connected to the right power rail (this is only allowed for coils).

Double-click the error in the message window to jump to the suspected worksheet (where the error is marked) and correct.
