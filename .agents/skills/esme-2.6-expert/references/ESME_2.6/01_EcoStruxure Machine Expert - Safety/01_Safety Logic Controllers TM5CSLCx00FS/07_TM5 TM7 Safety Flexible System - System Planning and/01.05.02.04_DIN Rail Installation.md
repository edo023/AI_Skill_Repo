# DIN Rail Installation

## Grounding Function

The DIN rail must be attached to a conductive backplane that itself is connected to a [protective ground (PE)](D-SE-0002601.html#D-SE-0002601__D-SE-0002601.9).

In the mounting channel of each piece of the TM5 equipment is a metal spring contact. When properly mounted on a metal DIN rail, these contacts provide connection to the [functional ground (FE)](D-SE-0002601.html#D-SE-0002601__D-SE-0002601.8) for the entire TM5 System.

## Mounting the DIN Rail

The TM5 System components are designed for mounting on rail conforming to IEC 60715.

To help achieve the stated TM5 System performance characteristics, the mounting hardware must be installed at the end positions and at 100 mm (3.94 in.) maximum increments along the length of the rail.

Failure to follow these instructions can result in death, serious injury, or equipment damage. |
