# Installation Notes

## Installation Notes

Products must be protected against dirt and grime. TM5 Safety I/O modules are protected from dirt and grime up to Pollution Level II in the IEC 60664 standard.

TM5 Safety I/O modules should be installed in IP54 rated cabinets to help protect the modules from hazardous pollution levels. Pollution levels greater than specified by Pollution Level II in the IEC 60664 standard can result in unreliable operation of the equipment.
